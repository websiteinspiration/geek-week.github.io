Furniture gas stops saved the giant of thought, solving at the same time the issue of fixing the monitor in the open position.

At the same time, the point of attachment of the stops is made far back beyond the axis of the loop, which ensures the greatest lifting force of the monitor from the lowest point. I have two stops of 60 Newton each, which in total gives 120 Newton (11.7 kg at the surface of our planet) efforts at the point of attachment of the stops. The mass of the monitor is 3.18 kg. We calculate the shoulders of the lever: from the axis of the loop to the point of attachment of the stops 60 mm, and from the axis of the loop to the point of attachment of the monitor arm 230 mm. We calculate the moment necessary to raise the monitor from the bottom point: (3.18 kg x 230 mm) / 60 mm = 12.19 kg. Despite the fact that, according to calculations, the emphasis is not enough, the monitor rises independently, and even too briskly. Apparently, the manufacturer slightly increased the gas pressure to ensure compliance with the specifications.
