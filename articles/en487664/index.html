Learn foreign words

The topic of language learning, on the one hand, is extremely relevant for almost everyone, but on the other hand, few people put in enough effort to achieve the desired result. In this article I want to share my best practices in the field of language learning. But first, what does it mean to "learn a language"?


The focus of this article is not the study of the language as a whole, but only one aspect - replenishment of the vocabulary. However, first of all, we need to understand what and why we are teaching. What does it mean to learn English, for example? I suppose that for some it is an extremely vague result of certain efforts: you mentally imagine how you are talking to someone in a language you don’t know, and get the approval of the interlocutor.



But, let's figure it out: who are you talking to? What for? What question are you discussing? How do you get approval? Not important? Someone may imagine a more specific result, for example: a tea party in a London mansion with a professor of linguistics from Oxford, and a British accent that delights the interlocutor.
