Future challenges

This article is just thoughts about the future. In my understanding, the future is a new round of evolution, and here whoever has the technology is the main one.

A few years ago, I became interested in the topic of machine learning. In view of my programming skills, I bought a python book and 3 machine learning books. I quickly mastered the recognition of numbers. In the end, I managed to successfully apply AI to trade in the financial markets. Since then, thoughts about the future do not leave.

Having seen videos of Boston Dynamics achievements, in particular a somersault video , it became clear to me - the future has come! Somehow the question arose in itself, but what will happen in general. What we ordinary people will face. What our children will face.

What exactly will be: a smart home, unmanned vehicles. Where it leads? At a minimum, such a profession as a taxi driver and trucker, driver of combines and trucks of all kinds will die out (thanks to Mask). It may be prohibited to drive vehicles on their own. A type of race will appear in the competition, where teams will compete in unmanned race cars. Watching the successes of SpaceX, I think that the profession of a pilot is also in question. What simple steps can be taken, knowing this. At a minimum, get rid of investments in transport companies and transfer money to Tesla and similar startups. Whoever has the technology is the main one.



Each in 30-40 years instead of a car will have its own robot. As once a mobile phone and laptop came into our lives, a personal robot will enter it. Most likely it will be a humanoid robot. I think by that time technology will allow this to be done. Thinking about this, I first think about these questions:

I will try to answer these questions myself and will gladly discuss in the comments.

What will a person need if he has his own high-tech robot?

This is my main idea. In fact, now the whole economy is implicated in basic instincts. Which basically comes down to getting pleasure. Instead, people work or do business. It turns out that you can buy a robot that will grow food, cook it for you, satisfy sexual needs in the evenings, and in which case it can intercede for you on the street. Then what else will you need? Clothing? What if he can sew it himself? A machine? What if the robot collects a large 3D printer for you and prints a cool car on it for you. I sincerely believe that in the future the robot will actually close all your needs and this will be the only thing that people will want. Everything else can be given to them by this robot. What do you think about that?

Will this robot instead of people go to work?

I think that the robot can be leased, but the purpose of this is not clear to me. Most likely in the future there will be a shortage of land for living and in order to assert your right to a piece of space under the sun, you will have to have some money or points in the global system. And otherwise they will not be available. In fact, you work before buying the robot, after which it will work for you. It is difficult for me to guess the answer.

Will it be necessary for the world‚Äôs strengths to hire someone if they simply can prescribe the purchase of robots in their business plan?

I think this will be the biggest problem for that part of the population that will not have access to technology. Think for yourself. Suppose you were able to invent a humanoid robot. Your financial condition allows you to create another 10,000 of the same universal soldiers who need only electricity from your solar power plants. What is the likelihood that you will agree to exchange them for money? Does that make sense. After all, each of them will be in fact your slave. These products can create any product for you yourself, without the need to buy anything. And what do you think?

How will this affect the cost of products, and will there be any sense in trading at all, and in money as such?

I think the world will be divided into two parts: those who have robots and everyone else. If I were in the place of people with robots, my main task would be that those ordinary people would not touch me. Oddly enough, we are already observing this in the world. Division into countries and countries of the 3rd world. The first are fenced off from the second. I think that prices for products will either not change or products will be free if robots make them. Regarding trade, it will remain only among those who do not have a universal robot.

How will the economy and the principles of existence of us as people change?

I think I already answered this question above. The economy will lose its meaning as such, and will be distributed only among the population without technology. The clearest example that confirms my words is our games. Take the game for examplewarcraft



The game is based on workers who saw wood, hammer stones, gold and build. Now imagine that you have a room that gives you 10 slaves every day. These slaves, in turn, are able to do anything, including the construction of another room for the production of slaves. Why do you need money?

Will ordinary people be able to access robots?

I think this is the main question that you and I need to think about. Will we have this technology. Will Musk share, as he promised the OpenAI source code . I think no. Bill Gates joined them, adding his billion. As you know, everything is paid for on Windows. The only good news is that you can download a broken OS.

Will Boston Dynamics give us access to drawings, assembly technology, and source codes for their robots? I think no. There are very big risks that a simple person will be able to teach a robot to keep a robot and make it rob banks. (For those who are not in the know, robots are widely used in modern military conflicts. Unmanned aircraft , unmanned autonomous submarines , unmanned self-propelled installations )

My personal conclusion to all that has been written is that we must now make every effort to develop robots and AI. Otherwise, tomorrow you and our descendants may not come. Whoever has the technology is the main one.

Do not take to heart. This is just my reasoning. Have a nice day, everyone. Let's discuss.
