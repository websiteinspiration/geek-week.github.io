This year, we remained true to the DIY spirit. Moreover, we decided to give the conference participants the opportunity to fully customize their badge. The scale of the upgrade is limited only by imagination and a very specific specification of the Shitty Add-On V1 connector (for more details on the SAO standard, its technical characteristics and other things can be found here ). The standard is simple and straightforward: even participants who are not very sophisticated in soldering and electronics will be able to prepare add-ons based on it in advance.
