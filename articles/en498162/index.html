We invite you to an IT internship at Alfa Bank

Hello! For already 7 years, we have been working on an internship program called I choose Alfa. This year we continue the recruitment of interns, shifting its format to a remote one. As usual, an internship for six months is paid, it can be combined with study. Interns who will show themselves well in the process of work will be able to remain on staff.

Applications are accepted until April 25, you can register here on this page .

An important geographical limitation: the program so far (so far) works only for Moscow, St. Petersburg, Barnaul and Yekaterinburg. Because, although we transferred to the remote rails all the possible stages of testing, the very final interview will still be in-person. Like an internship. Read more about the internship, as well as reviews from a couple of guys - under the cut.





About the program


When we first started in 2013, we had two interns. That's why he is a pilot. Then, every year, we gradually expanded the program and its geography. In 2019, for example, there were already 50 interns, of whom 38 remained in Alpha.

And this year we have 80 interns for 75 different projects. Of the available for registration today - IT and analytics.

The trainee is required to devote work 30 hours a week, the curator helps at all stages, the internship is paid. The total duration of the internship is 6 months. If desired, you can choose two directions at once: one that you definitely want and is interesting, and a spare one, just in case. The internship itself is not much different from regular work, so it will take place in the office. We hope that by July 1, getting around the city will be easier.
