SIT Master's Insights: What does a Master's program in Switzerland give and how much does it cost?

If you ask such a question, then the founders of the Schaffhausen Institute of Technology (SIT) will conduct a webinar specifically for you, where they will talk about the features of the new master's program of the institute. SIT Master's program is a real opportunity for university graduates from various countries (including Russia and the CIS) to receive a world-class education that meets market requirements and the needs of society. Brief information about the webinar, as well as a link to register - under the cut.







It is no secret to anyone that university graduates often lack qualifications, and corporations cannot find worthy candidates for the roles of CTO or CISO. For example, according to APKIT for 2020, the need for highly qualified IT specialists in Russia now amounts to 222 thousand employees, and by 2024 the total annual demand of the country's digital economy for highly qualified personnel will increase by a quarter - almost to 300 thousand people a year. A study by Microsoft and IDC for 2019 showed that 96% of IT professionals in Russia and Central and Eastern Europe are not qualified enough.



It is logical that someone should offer a solution to this problem. This summer, they announced the recruitment of students for the new Schaffhausen Institute of Technology (SIT), which was created to train the IT professionals needed by business. SIT is developing in such strategic areas as quantum technologies, new materials, machine learning and cyber defense - the most popular branches of science and technology today. SIT is located in Switzerland, but is an international project. Talented university graduates from around the world can get a master's degree in it. Any resident of Eastern Europe (including Russia and the CIS) or South Asia can apply for admission to the Master's program in Computer Science and Software Engineering . To submit documents, it is enough to have a bachelor's degree in a specialized field, as well as speak English at the level necessary for training (the ability to understand the person you are talking to and express your thoughts in English). Tuition at SIT is on a par with TOP-10 Russian technical universities, and discounts on tuition and scholarships are provided for the most talented students and winners of competitions.



By the way, right during the training, SIT students can take an internship at large technology companies (including Acronis), take part in real projects with official employment and salaries.
