ClickHouse in Avito: live gatherings with Alexey Milovidov

Hello, Habr! On April 9, from 17:00 to 20:00, our engineers will gather for cozy gatherings with the chief developer of ClickHouse Alexei Milovidy. For obvious reasons, we are not holding events at the office right now, so we invite everyone to watch the live broadcast of the meeting. Format: three micro-reports and a large Q&A session where viewers can ask their questions.
