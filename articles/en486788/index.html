Webinar “Remote monitoring and equipment diagnostics. Winnum CNC Solution Case Studies »



Colleagues, we invite you to participate in the webinar “Remote monitoring and equipment diagnostics. Winnum CNC Solution Case Studies. ” The webinar will be held on February 11 at 11:00 Moscow time.




How to increase the efficiency of equipment operation?
Winnum CNC is a solution for remote monitoring and diagnostics of CNC equipment.
The solution provides the most important tasks aimed at improving the efficiency and operation of equipment. It allows you to increase uptime, prevent equipment failure and minimize downtime.

Who will be useful


The webinar will be useful for:
• IT professionals;
• project managers;
• Engineers;
• Technical and network specialists;
• Heads of enterprises;
• Heads of structural divisions, industries, workshops;
• Specialists in the development of production systems;
• Researchers.

At the webinar we will tell you about the advantages of remote monitoring and digital transformation of production, we will demonstrate the operation of the Winnum CNC solution and answer the questions asked.

Key features of Winnum CNC:



Automated collection of information.
Winnum CNC performs automated data collection from machines connected to the local network of the enterprise, thereby eliminating the “human factor” and guaranteeing the objectivity of the data received.

Analytics and reports.
In addition to the specified time limits, reports and graphs in Winnum CNC are formed according to organizational sections (from one unit of equipment or a production cell, to workshops and several factories).

Diagnostics of equipment.
The Winnum CNC solution includes functionality for creating and running diagnostic programs and algorithms that can be applied to one or more pieces of equipment.

Support for any equipment modifications.
The Winnum CNC solution for each piece of equipment is configured using templates that include information about all the nodes and sensors used on a particular model, a list of possible breakdowns, malfunctions and errors.

Distributed cluster architecture.
The storage architecture in Winnum CNC is based on the use of modern distributed cluster “Cache storages”. This provides work with a large amount of data that comes from the equipment, and also guarantee stable operation and very high performance.

Security and data protection.
For remote monitoring and diagnostics of equipment, the way to organize data exchange is important. Winnum CNC offers a variety of architecture options that meet the most stringent security and data protection requirements.
