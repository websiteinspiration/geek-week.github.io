What is a digital double?


The digital double implements full-fledged feedback from production based on the collection of production data in real time and their presentation on an interactive 3D scene.

A digital double describes the actual state of production, from simple visualization of the state of equipment to visualization of material flows, personnel movements and the status of production tasks.

The technology allows you to simulate almost anything in a virtual environment, from machines and controllers to entire production lines. Continuity of data transfer saves time, prevents downtime and reduces power consumption.

During the webinar, our experts will tell you how the digital double helps customers improve the quality of equipment operation and what advantages Winnum provides for its use. The functionality of the digital double, its applications and implementation efficiency will be shown on real examples.

Particular attention will be paid to the possibilities of modeling objects in a virtual environment, the uniqueness of using WebGL technology and communication resources.

The webinar will consist of the following blocks:

  • The purpose and advantages of using the digital twin platform Winnum;
  • Examples of use by customers;
  • Demonstration of preparation and training of a digital double of the technological process.
