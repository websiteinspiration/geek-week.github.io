Hey, Fermenter, I salute you! I would be grateful to all your feedbacks.







A little background



In 2019, I became interested in iOS development and decided to try to get on the course from Mail.ru in their Technoproject with our university. I finished this course with honors. Many thanks to Dima and Gene for the excellent course. After this course, I began to actively attend meetings of iOS developers. In the summer I didn’t particularly go down for iOS. In the fall, he decided to find a job in this area and develop further. I decided to start with small companies , so I googled the top 100 outsourcing companies for application development. I wrote to all the companies that were in Moscow.



Out of about 70 companies, about 15 responded and about 3-4 were invited to an interview.



The first interview, as expected, failed, because he did not know what would be there and how to prepare. In the second interview, they gave a test task, not difficult, but because I didn’t have much experience, I killed more time trying to figure out the implementation and did not meet the deadline. At the third interview, the guys said that they now have no opportunity, because they urgently need to finish the project and that they are ready to take it in about a month, if I want or if I don’t find another job.



At this time, I found a course on iOS development from Yandex and passed it.

Somewhere closer to the new year I received a letter about the successful completion of this stage. In the 20th of January, she contacted HR and she said that the last interview was left (platform section). Then they appointed a date the day after the last exam. On New Year's holidays and during the session he was preparing for the social security. In a word, it was difficult to prepare for both the exam and the social security.



He came to the social security agency, he was led by Glory from the Browser. We talked a bit about the algorithmic section. Then he asked me to write a schematic code that implements multithreading. Since before this had not worked especially with multithreading, due to unexpectedness I fell into a stupor for 20-30 minutes, I just did not think that I would have to write code. In theory, he answered not bad, but there were problems with implementation. With all this, I really liked the social security. We did not meet the hour and Slava offered to finish it, so we had to do it in the corridor, then we talked a little. It was great, because some guys are oriented strictly on time. Two weeks later, they wrote that he had failed. It was very disappointing because getting ready and really, really wanted to get there.



The huge minus of interviews in Yandex is that they don’t care whether you get to them or not. Because of what, they often ignore you or forget to write a letter with the result of / (on the appointment) of the interview, and you yourself have to constantly write / call to find out something.



Interview in MegaFon



The lightest and slightly unclear interview of all that had to take place in large companies. The social security lasted for about 20-30 minutes. We talked about what I know how, what I am fond of, what grades I get at the university, etc. There was not a single question on the platform and algorithms. Most likely, this social security certificate did not pass either, for it received no information.



Then he talked with many companies, but due to the lack of commercial experience, most companies lost interest and were not invited to interviews. Finally, around the 20th of March, some companies appointed social security, but then, due to the situation with self-isolation, they had to be suspended indefinitely.



Self-isolation says: “No to face-to-face interviews!”







Interview at Sberbank



There were two telephone interviews, where he talked about his interests, about why he decided to try to get to Sberbank. The latter asked a couple of theoretical questions related to iOS and in general about programming. The first and second interviews passed, a date was set for the full-time (last stage) social security interview, which was carried out on Skype due to the self-isolation regime. We had a great talk with Glory, talked completely on the platform part. A couple of days later, a letter arrived saying that the social security passed successfully, but there is no place in this team, so they will offer a candidacy to another team. After 2-3 days, another letter came that the internship application was closed.



Interview at VTB



He walked in parallel with the social security to Sberbank. The first interview was over the phone, then there was a small “test”, where it was necessary to answer a couple of questions related to experience and knowledge in programming, etc. Then there was another telephone interview. Next was a test of 6 blocks per hour.



Passed them successfully, then they held the final stage of the interview with Misha. Because They said that there will be just a platform part and it is unlikely that there will be anything else, it was not particularly prepared. During the interview, they talked with Misha about algorithms, structures, networks, multithreading, etc. The interview took more than an hour, but it was very interesting and cool.



About a week later they called and congratulated on the successful completion of the selection for an internship.

The other day, an agreement was signed, on the same day I received a pass, and then the equipment. The internship was supposed to take place in the office, but at the last moment, nevertheless, to our great regret, they were transferred to a remote location.



Conclusion



At the same time, I took a course on iOS development at CoursEra, but due to the fact that the session and internship started now, I had to suspend activity at CoursEra.

  1. It is necessary to understand the theory well, because learning to code is not difficult.
  2. Respond to middle / senior vacancies, as finding an intern / junior job is almost impossible.
  3. If you can get an interview, then go, because this will help you raise your skills. (Even if you know little)
  4. Examine the company in which you will have an interview and show the interviewer your interest in working with them.
