Intel Xeon Scalable Gen2: Big Sale of Cores



The second generation of Intel Xeon Scalable processor family is replenished: to the 60 models released last year, 19 new ones were added . Most of the new products belong to the "middle class" Gold, there is a bit of Silver and Bronze, but Platinum is not a single one. The point here is, I think, in the trends and nuances of the situation, at least one pattern is clearly visible: the 2020 models have moved up in terms of characteristics at a constant price. Whether this will happen with the top models, as you can see, will be decided separately.

So, when updating the line, the standard status quo was observed for Intel: heirs have the same cost as their predecessors. However, this time it is rather a formality: we see that the characteristics (frequency and number of cores) are significantly improved within one generation. Consider an example.
