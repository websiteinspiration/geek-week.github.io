Risk management functions


Trading software should make it possible to configure risk management parameters that will allow controlling risk, minimizing possible losses during trading, and also more reliably taking profit on transactions.

For example, it is good if the program allows you to configure a ban on placing new applications and opening new positions on a specific account in case of violation of the established conditions.

It is also important that the software has the ability to use orders such as stop loss, take profit and sliding stop orders. Such orders are used to protect open positions - if the price goes down sharply, losses will be limited, and profit will be fixed. The SMARTx terminal has the ability to place bundles of such orders (and there is also a module that prohibits transactions in case of violation of the conditions).



Performance


For the trading terminal, performance is important. The program should work equally well on old or weak computers / tablets, when making tens or hundreds of transactions per day, with large-scale movements in the market.

In the days of the release of important economic news, the amount of market data (market data) sent by the server to the terminal can increase tenfold in a short time. If the program is not optimized enough and is running on a weak device, it can simply freeze at the most crucial moment, which can result in a user losing money.

In addition, there is a whole class of active traders on the market (for example, scalpers, arbitrageurs and high frequency traders) who carry out a large number of orders and transactions even on a regular trading day.
