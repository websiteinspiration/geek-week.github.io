Electronic voting control requirements

Constitution and onwards everywhere



Greetings to all, I recently suggested that you discuss the voting system developed by the Department of Information Technology (DIT) of Moscow.







The discussion turned out to be very productive, and based on its results, some recommendations on the voting procedure can be developed. Everyone who is interested is invited to cat.







Introduction



The system under consideration was first used in municipal elections, and now it is to be tested in the cities of Moscow and Nizhny Novgorod at a vote on the Constitution . You do not need to be seven spans in your forehead to guess: the geography and scope of the system will expand further.







In the municipal elections, there were a lot of comments on the system under consideration. I think there will be comments on the constitutional vote. We all have to develop rules for monitoring and controlling the voting in this system, to exclude its use for falsifying voting results. So far, observing her work is very formal: none of the observers, in fact, understand how it works and where weaknesses may appear. The observer community expects IT experts to provide recommendations and guidelines for observing it.







Over time, one can also predict an increase in the number of offers on the market for the deployment of such systems from independent suppliers. Whichever system is used in the future, the experience of observing this system will be useful for organizing observation of other systems of this kind.







Recommendations made



I took the liberty of collecting and formalizing almost all the comments and recommendations for improving the system itself and its control, expressed in the comments to the previous article, and also added my thoughts to them. I bring them to your attention.
