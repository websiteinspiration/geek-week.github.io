Bookmarks - is there a limit?



Hello everyone!

It's no secret that the speed of the browser is very often a critical parameter when choosing users. At the same time, this speed directly depends on the amount of data processed by the browser. In particular - now we are talking about bookmarks. This is a convenient tool that allows us to keep frequently used resources on hand or to postpone for further reading any new links that interest us during the current work. And it’s no secret that the more bookmarks in our storage, the more likely the browser will start to slow down when working with them.

Therefore, today we want to ask you about how many bookmarks are currently stored in your browser.
