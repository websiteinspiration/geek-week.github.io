How to maintain the quality of work under quarantine

In the quarantine office, employees stayed at home. What does this mean for business, and how to continue the work of the company in such circumstances?



First of all, a trading company needs to arrange receiving calls from customers. The remote sales team must meet two requirements: continue to interact effectively with customers and remain manageable. If we are talking about a real estate agency and companies where employees often attend meetings and work with clients assigned to them, the key is control.

In this article we will look at several cases on how to quickly and painlessly transfer an office to remote work with MegaFon's Virtual PBX .

When a company works on mobile phones


If each employee of the company has a mobile phone to which he receives and from which he makes calls, you need to create employee records in your Virtual PBX account. After that, in the "Numbers" section, select "Add SIM Card" and then add MegaFon mobile numbers to your employees, whose work you want to begin to control.

For employees, when processing calls, nothing will change, but in the "History" section you will see all the information on calls of any of the added employees:

  1. The number of incoming and outgoing calls.
  2. Share missed.
  3. How long the client waited on the line, and at what second the receiver was picked up.



In addition, in the "Statistics" section you can see detailed statistics on the telephony of the company in a graphical representation.



Also available for analysis is the rating of each employee relative to his colleagues both in terms of the number of calls and the duration of incoming and outgoing calls.



Separately, it is worth noting that all calls can be recorded - both incoming and outgoing. You can listen to them in the call history.



An important difference between the MegaFon Virtual PBX and other similar solutions is that the recording of calls is unlimited in volume and the storage period is one year. Not all calls must be heard - it is important that employees know that any conversation can be heard. With remote work, this is a serious discipline.



In the MegaFon Virtual PBX mobile application, you will see statistics and timely monitor employee calls from a smartphone from anywhere. Thanks to this, you can control the communication of employees with customers even more quickly than it could be implemented in the office.

If the company collectively answers calls to a mobile phone


Another popular case: on the table there is one or two mobile phones, and the one who is now in the office answers the calls. In this case, MegaFon‚Äôs SIM card is connected to the Virtual PBX in the same way, but the number is made by the general number of the company. Now the calls to the number will no longer get on the mobile phone - they will be sent to the MegaFon Virtual PBX, where you can configure their distribution.



By calling your company number, the client will hear a greeting, after which it will be sent to a specific employee or group of employees (department). When a call falls on a group of employees, the call will be processed depending on the selected rule: all at once, in turn, in incremental or even fashion.
The advantage of this solution is that it will work not only with MegaFon SIM-cards - employees will be able to receive calls on SIM-cards of other operators as well. As in the previous case, in the call history there will be all information on incoming calls, each can be listened to.

Conclusion


How to make and receive calls to employees working from home?

  1. The easiest way is with MegaFon's corporate SIM cards. Employees do not need to do anything specifically - all settings for remote work are done by the administrator, special knowledge is not required to manage the service.
  2. To SIM cards of other operators. If the company does not yet have MegaFon SIM cards connected, employees will be able to receive calls from the company's general number to those SIM cards that they have now.
  3. Softphone on the computer. Employees can install the MegaFon Communicator proprietary application on their computers and connect a headset. Both incoming and outgoing calls will be recorded and saved. A virtual PBX can also be used through a softphone for a mobile phone. In this case, even a headset may not be needed.
