Reddit PC gamers pool their computing power around the Folding @ Home project to fight COVID-19

The moderators of the PC Master race subreddit urged the owners of powerful gaming video cards to join the scientific cloud computing of the Stanford Folding @ Home project , which is now engaged in the calculation of models in eight directions at once to combat COVID-19. The Reddit community initiative was already supported by Nvidia, which called on Twitter to provide scientists with the computing power of their video cards:


It is important to note that the PC Master race subreddit is not trying to hype on the topic of coronavirus : Folding @ Home support and the first systematic mega-thread dedicated to the project were published 4 months ago, when they did not even hear about coronavirus. In addition, users have mentioned F @ H in subreddit for the past five years.

How to take part in collective calculations, even if you do not care about COVID-19 and you want to quickly find a cure for cancer - under the cat.

In order to participate in the calculations and support scientists with their own capacities, you have three options.

First: join the computer reddit group of the subreddit PC Master race.

Second: enter the specific code of the study of interest and count it yourself, in the chosen direction.

An option for those who do not care about COVID-19: choose from the four proposed areas (cancer, alzheimer, etc.) and count there.

In order to become part of the computing group, you will need to install special F @ H software on your PC. A complete list of installers can be found here . Now out of the box, Windows 10, MacOS, Ubuntu, CentOS, Fedora, RedHat, Debian, and Mint are supported. Also worth reading this article.and the previously mentioned thread on Reddit itself . For those interested, a repository on GitHub .

So that you do not waste time: in order to connect to the calculations, you must specify the code of the project (settlement group) in the software settings F @ H. The developers suggest using a web interface, but it can be called stable with great difficulty.
