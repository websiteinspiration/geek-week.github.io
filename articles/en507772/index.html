Most Important From Apple's WWDC'20 Conference

Tonight was the 31st Apple Developer Conference - for the first time completely online. In this report, we listed the most significant innovations that were presented by the company employees for their operating systems and devices.







iOS 14



The new ‚ÄúApplication Library‚Äù automatically organizes applications into categories - like folders, only better. It will also be possible to hide entire screens with applications on the main page.



Widgets have become more interactive and informative. You can rearrange them and resize them, transfer them to the Home screen, as well as create smart collections that will change according to your schedule. In addition, the new Smart Stack widget allows you to swipe the most relevant widgets depending on the time of day.







The Picture in Picture function has appeared: now the video can be fixed on the screen and viewed in the background while you are doing your business.



Siri got a redesign and got smarter. Apple Translate has more pairs of languages ‚Äã‚Äãfor translation and the possibility of simultaneous translation, using speech recognition through machine learning. The application can work completely offline.



In the "Messages" there was an opportunity to consolidate the most important dialogs. Group chats have been redesigned: now you can reply to a specific message, mention individual participants and view threads. Three new memoji appeared, a lot of settings for their appearance, including a change in age, and you can add a medical mask.







Maps have become more detailed. New feature: Guides with recommendations for visiting nearby places, including attractions and eating places. Now the maps show how to ride a bicycle and how busy and easy to pass while waiting for the road. For electric car drivers, the app indicates where they recharge on the way.



Now iPhone owners have access to a digital version of car keys. To use it, you just need to use an iPhone with an NFC chip to unlock the door. You can disable these keys through iCloud or set up individual access to the car - for example, by age.







The App Store has an innovation called App Clip. With it, you can launch a small part of the application without installing it on a smartphone and use it only when you need it - for example, to order coffee or rent a scooter. App Clips are opened using NFC tags, QR codes or via links, and they can also be tied to locations on maps.





iPadOS 14



The system also received a massive redesign, in particular a widget redesign similar to iOS.



The photo gallery has received a new sidebar for faster navigation through folders, access to photos, organize and view them. Photos can now be dragged between folders and sections. In other applications, such as Notes and Files, similar sidebars will appear.







Siri has also been improved, and its popup now appears in the lower right corner.



Calls and FaceTime no longer occupy the entire screen, but appear with widgets at its top. The same change applies to iOS.



The search also underwent a redesign and became universal: it is now performed in messages, contacts, files and on the Internet, while redirecting it directly to a page in Safari.



Big changes await Apple Pencil with the new Scribble feature. Handwritten text is now automatically converted to typewritten, and the drawn figures are approximated to the perfect shape. This handwritten text can be selected by double-clicking, and then pasted, for example, in Pages, and it is immediately reformatted to machine. It will also be possible to write by hand directly in the search bar or in two languages ‚Äã‚Äãin one sentence at the same time.









Airpods



Automatic switching between devices will appear. So, if you listened to music on the iPad, and at that time you received a call on the iPhone, the headphones will automatically switch to the desired device.



AirPods Pro now uses a built-in accelerometer and gyroscope, thus tracking your movements to synchronize the spatial sound in the headphones with the image on the screen of the device on which you are watching a video or playing a game.









watchOS 7



Using the Face Sharing function, watchOS 7 allows you to share your watch faces with your friends and family.



Bicycle routes have been added to the Maps application: their approximate duration, the number of steps to be overcome, height difference, etc.



Dances of various styles (Indian, hip-hop and even Latin) have been added to the training modes, as well as a redesign of the conservation training progress.



Tracking your sleep, you can now set not only an alarm clock, but also the time for going to bed. In this case, it will be possible, for example, to turn on relaxing music, otherwise the devices will switch to the Do Not Disturb mode.



An automatic hand washing detector has been introduced, which detects the sound of flowing water, after which the required 20 seconds are recommended for the procedure.





Safety



Signing in with Apple can now be converted to existing accounts.



From now on, you can share with the application not your exact location, but only approximate. Applications will also ask permission to enable tracking of your movement and to collect data for advertising.



Now in the App Store on the application page there will be an opportunity to see what data the application requests from you.









Homekit



Apple teamed up with Google, Amazon, IKEA and other companies to create a universal ‚Äúsmart home‚Äù standard.



On the Home screen of your device, you can set up automatic notifications about whether you turned off the light or closed the door.



The adaptive lighting setting has been introduced, in which you can change the color of the incident light, turn it on and off.



On surveillance cameras, you can mark the most important and active zones and face recognition, as well as broadcast the image on the screen of your TV.









tvOS 14



Introduced support for multiple users, as well as synchronization with the Xbox Elite 2 and Xbox controllers.



Apple TV + can now be watched in the Apple TV App.



The conference announced the new Apple TV + series by Isaac Asimov, The Foundation, which will be released in 2021.







macOS



The new macOS Big Sur operating system has undergone the largest redesign since macOS X. The system has become simpler and more minimalistic and in general is much more similar to iOS and iPadOS, and at the same time adopted many of their innovations.



A ‚ÄúControl Center‚Äù has appeared, largely copying that of iOS - with the same brightness adjustment, access to settings and switching between light and dark themes. The Safari browser has become much faster and can hold more tabs, and you can now view the contents of a specific tab when you hover over it. And most importantly: MacBook computers will now be available on their own chipsets. Apple Silicon SoC will have higher performance and "perfect for gaming." Universal 2, in turn, will allow you to perform the reverse transition and run applications for Apple processors on Intel. To run Linux on the new chips will remain a virtual machine.







To adapt programs for new chips, developers will need to use the new version of Xcode. The presentation showed that Microsoft Office 365 and Adobe Creative Cloud will support the new chips already at the start.



For developers, Apple will release Mac mini with A12Z Bionic, 16 GB of RAM and 512 GB of storage to create and adapt applications on Mac with new chips. These girls will be available in June.
