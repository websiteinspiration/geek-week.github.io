Creation of Enterprise-architecture in NSPK


Hello, Habr! My name is Igor Tulinov. I am the head of the IT architecture center in the National Payment Card System (NSPK JSC) and I want to talk about how our company came to the decision to implement Enterprise architecture management processes, allocate staff of enterprise architects and implement architectural control at key stages of creating IT value.


In the IT industry, the term Enterprise Architecture appeared more than thirty years ago. Over time, a wide range of definitions of this concept, models, frameworks, and standards for its description arose. Many IT and fintech companies in one form or another have implemented corporate architecture management processes.


But at what exact moment does Enterprise Architecture appear in the company? When and on the basis of what does the organization come to the understanding that it is time to implement corporate architecture management processes?
