To whom it is bad


Dismissals are taking place in companies right now - including in large ones and even in digital. One of the leading Ukrainian agencies laid off 40% of its full-time employees, and reduced the remaining salaries by a third or 50%. I hear the same thing about other companies. The other day, a marketing manager of a large restaurant chain came to me for an interview: half of their staff were fired, and the rest were cut so much that many left.

Ukraine and Russia suffered much more than the West. Firstly, because we have a lot more business tied to the service sector than to the production of specific online products. Secondly, because in the West, business has a much larger financial pillow and there is both free aid from the state and interest-free (or low interest) loans for paying salaries.
