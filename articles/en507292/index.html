Admission to the Moscow Institute of Physics and Technology continues

MIPT Olympiad schools continue recruiting for the second and third shifts for students in grades 7-11. Hurry up to write testing in the chosen direction by June 23!

MIPT Olympiad Schools is a university camp for schoolchildren, where schoolchildren visit couples every day, like real students, listen to popular science lectures from existing scientists, developers, employees of large Russian IT companies, and also participate in intellectual games, creative contests, film screenings, sports tournaments and more.



Classes are conducted by experienced teachers, jury members of the All-Russian Olympiad for schoolchildren, teachers and graduates of leading Russian and foreign universities: MIPT, Moscow State University Lomonosov University, Higher School of Economics, St. Petersburg State University, KFU, Ecole Polytechnique and Vedecom Institute (France), Northeastern University of Boston (USA), Bar-Ilan University (Israel).

Directions of the second online shift (July 12-23): Mathematics , Physics , Chemistry , Biomedicine , Computer Science , Computer Vision , Information Security , Machine Learning and Data Analysis .

Directions of the third, pre-full-time, shift (July 26 - August 7): Mathematics, Physics, Computer Science.

If you want to participate:

  1. Register on the site and fill out the form
  2. Take the test on the selected subject until June 23
  3. Expect a confirmation email from us.

Details on our website and in the VKontakte group .
