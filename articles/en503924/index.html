About Medit



The founder of Medit, Minho Chang, was professionally involved in digital data processing technologies before starting his own business. He understood that dentistry was a promising niche for the development of 3D technologies. At that time, dental scanners were bulky and expensive.

In 2000, Medit was created. Its first products were large appliances familiar to the market. But by 2008, the company developed and released its first compact scanner, which could be placed on the desktop. It was an innovative product for its time. In the future, the main activity of Medit was the creation and production of portable scanners for the dental industry.

Features Medit i500

Description



The Dental Intraoral Scanner Medit i500 was created for 3D-digitization of the oral cavity in order to create crowns, abutments, dental bridges, veneers and other orthodontic procedures.

The device scans soft tissues and teeth in color with an accuracy of 0.0032 mm.

Two working chambers are installed in a compact housing. A tip is inserted into the oral cavity of the patient, which is made removable for ease of use: with a large load, the doctor does not waste time disinfecting the equipment.


Source: youtube.com

The start and stop button of the device is located on the body. The dentist does not need to adjust the workflow, since the scanner automatically synchronizes with the iScan program, which saves working time.

The developers took into account many specific nuances of the doctor‚Äôs work to make using the device as comfortable and effective as possible. For example, a removable tip is equipped with a mirror, the image from which falls on the camera. To protect against fogging, a continuous air stream is directed at the mirror.

Intraoral Medit i500 weighs only 0.28 kg, easily takes pictures even of distant teeth. It is very easy to use - at exhibitions, employees of the Korean company offer visitors to the stand to independently make scans of their oral cavity.
