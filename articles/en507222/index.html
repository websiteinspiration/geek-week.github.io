Why everyone should wear masks

People get sick and die from the virus!

Among such measures, one can distinguish wearing masks, smoking or non-smoking, alcohol, destroying cell towers, magic diets, self-medication, prayers, home-made devices for disinfecting air and antiseptics, and many others. The measures taken should be rational and have proven effectiveness outweighing the side effects.
