In conducting classes, too, had its own trick. At the beginning of the lesson, the teacher announces a list of topics. Before starting the theory, he shows the text of the homework and conducts a survey of how many students understood him. Usually 1-5 people are ready to go right away. After the lesson, the teacher repeats the survey and by its results you can understand how well the lesson went. The approach significantly improves concentration: when students hear key words or definitions from homework in a lecture, they understand that they need to focus on this moment.
