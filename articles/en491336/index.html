Play like a game designer

‚ÄúPlay as a game designer‚Äù is one of the most popular recommendations for those who take their first steps in the profession. What is really hidden behind these words? The authors of books and articles recommend playing as much as possible, playing games of different genres, playing not only good games, but also bad ones, in every way increasing their pretense.


There is nothing wrong with increasing simplicity, but for some reason it is not obvious to everyone that simplicity alone is not enough. It would be nice not only to consume games, but also to treat them critically.


The result of a critical analysis may be an article / video about how in one game or another the developers' tasks were solved.


Who needs it and why? First of all, such work will be useful to you, as a developer, as well as to your colleagues. Unlike game journalism, intended for players, game criticism is useful to specialists. The benefit may lie in describing strong solutions, interesting findings, or in pointing out errors.


I suggest you familiarize yourself with the option of conducting such a critical analysis.
