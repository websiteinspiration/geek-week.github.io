Best Small Business Servers in 2020



Despite the widespread adoption of cloud servers, many opt for on- premises servers that provide more control. The TechRadar portal has compiled a list of the top 10 servers, from those designed for small businesses to those that large corporations might need.

1. Dell PowerEdge T30



A Dream on Xeon with Many Connectivity

Processor: Intel Xeon E3-1225 v5 | Graphics: Intel HD Graphics P530 | RAM: 64GB | Storage: Up to six SATA HDD | Connection Interfaces: 10 x USB Ports, HDMI, DisplayPort, VGA, Serial Port, PS2, Gigabit Ethernet | Dimensions (width x length x height): 17.5cm x 43.5cm x 36cm

+ Fast quad-core Xeon processor
+ Holds six hard drives

- One gigabit network port
- No hot-swap drives

There are so many different servers around that sometimes you need to study entire price lists in order to find the best small server that suits you. Take a look at the Dell PowerEdge T30 - you can buy it in a version for self-assembly and configuration, or in a completely finished form. Targeting the entry-level and small office market, the T30 has great potential for expansion into a small tower-shaped building, and also comes with a lot of default server functionality, making it an ideal alternative to an office workstation.

3. Lenovo ThinkServer TS150



High Performance Tower Server

Processor: Intel Xeon E3-1200 v6 | RAM: up to 64GB | Storage: HDD up to 40TB | Connection Interfaces: 8 x USB 3.0, Serial, Video, 2 x DisplayPort, Audio, Gigabit Ethernet | Dimensions (width x length x height): 17.5 cm x 37.5 cm x 43 cm

+ Absolutely quiet

Lenovo seized the x86 server market from IBM in 2014 and created one of the best lineups, the ThinkServer. The TS150 is one of the most affordable models in the range, which is an enterprise-class 4U server comparable to the Dell T20. It has RAID 0,1,10 and 5 support (through the controller on the motherboard). Like competitors, this server can accommodate up to four 3.5-inch hard drives, which means it can have storage up to 40 TB when installing the corresponding hard drives. The relatively fresh Intel Xeon processor E3-1200 v6 should be sufficiently productive for small and medium enterprises.

Lenovo also claims that the TS150's work sound is quieter than the noise level in the library, at about 26 dB. As with other servers, you get an impressive array of ports and connection interfaces: 8 USB ports, 4 PCI / PCI-E slots, three video outputs (including two DisplayPort connectors), a serial port, gigabit Ethernet, and 3 connectors for audio.

If you need a reliable and versatile server, then HPE ProLiant ML350 Gen 10 is a great choice. This model provides high performance due to installed Intel Xeon Scalable processors. You yourself need to install your own storage, but this server supports a wide range of fast operations, including working with graphics and computing tasks. Although the HPE ProLiant ML350 Gen 10 comes as a tower server, with the expansion of your business, it can be converted to a rack server, which allows you to expand your business without the extra cost and the need to buy a completely new server.

5. Fujitsu Primergy TX1310 M1



Fujitsu Reliability Guaranteed

Unmatched Processor: Intel Xeon E3-1226 v3 | Graphics: Intel HD Graphics P4600 | RAM: 16GB | Storage: 2 x 1TB HDD | Connection Interfaces: 5 x USB 3.0, 2 x Gigabit Ethernet Ports | Dimensions (width x length x height): 17.5 cm x 42 cm x 39.5 cm

+ Incredible warranty

- No RAID 5
- Fujitsu - not the most obvious brand

Fujitsu is not the first brand that comes to mind when it comes to servers. However, Fujitsu is one of the few manufacturers to deal with everything from business servers to supercomputers. The TX1310 is an entry-level business-oriented server that has proven itself in operation and has the best reliability guarantee on the market. If the server fails within a year after the purchase, Fujitsu will not only repair or replace it, but will also refund you the amount you paid for this server.

Like most models in this market segment, the server is designed to work 24/7 and supports RAID 0/1/10 (no RAID 5 support). Intel Xeon E3-1216 of the third version is installed in it, two hard drives with 1 TB and 16 GB of RAM. Also a plus is the fact of having an optical drive and two gigabit Ethernet ports for reserve. With four DIMM slots and four storage bays, this server can store up to 32 TB of data and supports up to 32 GB of RAM.

Compact but slightly noisy server

+ Professional build quality and ease of access
+ Compactness

In one of the fastest growing market segments, only one company dominates. The HP Proliant Microserver Gen8 succeeded in competing with an attractive combination of features and many discounts - and ultimately took over the market. These small servers have also gained demand outside their niche due to the popularity among professional consumers and advertising of advantages over NAS NAS.

Despite being compact (less than 0.013 m3 in volume) and lightweight (less than 7 kg), this machine has impressive capabilities. We are talking about support for the Intel Xeon E3 processor family, the ability to install up to 16 GB of RAM, system management mode, two gigabit Ethernet ports, one PCIe slot, support for RAID 0/1/10, a DVD writer, up to four hard drives , an internal microSD card slot, an integrated Matrox G200 graphics chip, and seven USB ports. On the other hand, this server has only one VGA-connector and only two connectors for RAM.

A powerful server does not have to be expensive - this is what Scan wants to convey to its potential customers. Mainstream tower server with a margin of flexibility

Processor: Intel Xeon E5-2600 v3 | Graphics: unknown | RAM: depends on the order | Storage: drives not included | Connection Interfaces: 8 x USB ports, VGA, PS2, 3 x Gigabit Ethernet | Dimensions (width x length x height): 22 cm x 54.5 cm x 45.5 cm

Like Supermicro, Asus is not very well known for its servers. The Taiwanese company, which is one of the largest suppliers of computer components, is better known for its wide range of consumer products, including motherboards. The TS500-E8-PS4 is a 5U mainstream tower server ideal for use as either a server or a workstation.

It is equipped with the latest Intel Xeon E5-2600 processor of the third version, eight DDR4 DIMM RAM slots (supports up to half TB of RAM), six expansion slots, three 5.25-inch storage bays and a 500-watt power supply with 80 Plus Bronze certificate .
