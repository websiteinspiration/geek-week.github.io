How to monetize your knowledge and experience


Teaching! If you feel that you have something to share, you can record your online course.

Algorithm for creating an online course:

  • The choice of topics. It must meet two criteria: 1) you are an expert in this, 2) it is interesting to your audience.
  • Course program development. All training is divided into modules, each of which addresses one specific issue. A popular learning format is educational video + homework.
  • Video recording, presentation design. In addition, you can prepare bonus materials for students - a list of useful sources of information, check lists, reference books, etc.
  • The solution of technical and organizational issues. Decide where the course will be placed and how the search for customers will take place.

In addition to theoretical and practical knowledge from the IT sphere, include one block about how to search for orders and communicate with customers, because these are no less important skills for developers.
