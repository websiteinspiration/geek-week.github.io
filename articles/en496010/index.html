Vedomosti :
“So far, the terrorist attack in the United States on September 11, 2001, was the biggest blow to the airline industry. According to IATA, the companies lost in 2001-2002. amounted to $ 25 billion. The last series of what happened with the transport.

All tours were removed, including directions within Russia until mid-April for all. Further, the dates vary depending on the tour operator and direction. All tour operators, except the Biblioglobus, do not return money to tourists for them, but offer to deposit (they can choose another tour for this money until the end of 2021). Biblioglobus gives money. We were not ready to take into account such deposits technically, and there are lots of issues legislatively. And the syura adds that claims can only be accepted in the original, which does not fit with quarantine and self-isolation. Who accepts electronically - we send inquiries and we accompany, helping tourists.

Air: Last week there were single return flights from China. Suddenly sales started in Italy in November: it seems that our citizens are not optimistic. Flights across the Russian Federation are gradually being canceled: here both the fact that certain regions close, and the fact that airlines are taking off flights due to falling load.

Trains : Russian Railways has reduced the depth of sales to 45 days in all directions, so they are not buying summer yet. Most of the purchases go in the coming week, the most common case is a purchase for tomorrow. Trains are underloaded, and their number is declining. Passengers are either returned the full amount or transferred to another analog train, because cancellations are mainly for duplicate trains. Cancellations can be viewed here .
