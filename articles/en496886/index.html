Elon Musk revealed the purpose of the camera over the rearview mirror in Tesla Model 3



The purpose of most cameras, sensors and sensors of the Tesla Model 3 electric vehicle is known. But there is one camera that was installed, it is not known why. This is a camera located above the rearview mirror in the Tesla Model 3.

Since the release of the electric car, its purpose has remained unknown. There were rumors that the camera was needed so that the company could track the actions of the driver during Autopilot operation. After the next update of the electric car software, the camera was tied specifically to the autopilot and Tesla Network, so everything seemed quite logical.

And this assumption turned out to be almost true . Recently, Elon Musk revealed the purpose of the device. According to him, the camera is needed so that the Tesla car can be used as an autonomous taxi. And also so that electric cars can be rented to third parties. The rental function is already provided in the software, but remained inactive for a couple of years.

Musk said that the camera will monitor the actions of drivers of rented robomobiles, including when autopilot is active. If one of the drivers decides to ruin the interior or break the rules, the camera will see it.

The two owners of the Tesla Model 3 managed to use the camera and show everyone what is in focus.



No wonder, the usual photo of the cabin. You can see that all seats fall into focus, so that the camera will be able to evaluate both the actions of the driver and the actions of passengers. After all, they too can begin to spoil the salon, as is often the case in car-sharing cars.

In addition, Elon Musk previously mentioned another possibility of using the camera. She will be able to record the actions of drivers and passengers during the activation of the Caraoke function, which is available from the moment of updating the software to V10.
