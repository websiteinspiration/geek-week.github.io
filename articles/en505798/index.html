PHP 25 years: a brief history of the language and a 50% discount on PhpStorm

Hello, Habr! Today is a special day - exactly 25 years ago, Rasmus Lerdorf introduced the world of PHP. On the birthday of the language, we want to thank you for the years of its support.

We are all waiting for the release of PHP 8, which is scheduled for this December. In the meantime, we suggest recalling the achievements of the PHP community over the past 25 years: we have collected in a large timeline all the most important events from the history of the language.


Over the years, PHP users from around the world have formed a friendly community and a rich ecosystem of tools, resources and events that continues to evolve, adopting best practices and adapting to new conditions.

Timeline data is freely available on GitHub - you can use it however you want. If you notice an error, create a pull-request with a fix. For now, we accept only typos or date corrections. We are very grateful to everyone who helped us in compiling the timeline.
