Survey of IT executives about changing needs for IT services

Consist Business Group, with the support of the LANIT group of companies and the domestic vendor of TURBO business systems, is conducting a study of the changing needs for IT services in Russian companies.


External economic factors, doing business in a pandemic, are changing priorities in the activities of companies, including the digital transformation of processes. A survey of IT executives will demonstrate the dynamics of ongoing IT projects:

  • the impact of the coronavirus situation and the transition to remote work on the company's IT strategy, 
  • new business opportunities in terms of IT,
  • which solutions will be headlined in the changing IT landscape.

We invite you to take part in a survey of the heads of IT departments of companies, specialists responsible for the digital transformation of enterprises, IT experts, business analysts, and development managers for various business sectors.

The results of the study will be presented at an online event with the participation of invited IT experts. Each survey participant will receive access to the webinar and follow-up materials by e-mail.

You can take part in the survey here . Respondents' answers are accepted until June 11th.
