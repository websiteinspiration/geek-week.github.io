Industrial touch monitor FPM-215W



The FPM-215W monitor is intended for installation in embedded systems, human-machine interface terminals, for process control. Equipped with a built-in capacitive touch panel with multi-touch support.

The one-piece faceplate protects against moisture and dirt entering the enclosure. The monitor can be used as a user terminal in information kiosks with a high load. The touch panel is connected via a standard USB interface, is defined as a HID device and works without drivers on most operating systems.

In the article we will test the monitor, consider how the touch panel and monitor mounting options are connected.

Sealed faceplate




FPM-215W has a solid front surface without holes and protrusions, which is why it is protected from liquid and dirt from entering through the screen surface. This design allows you to easily remove dirt from the screen and prevents the accumulation of dirt in the corners, as is the case with conventional household monitors.

Specifications



  • Touch panel: capacitive (multitouch up to 10 simultaneous points).
    USB connection
  • Resolution: 1366 x 768
  • Aspect Ratio: 16: 9
  • Video Interface: HDMI
  • Power Supply: 12V


The monitor comes with all the necessary components for work. The package includes: the monitor itself, USB-B to USB-A cable, full-size HDMI cable, power supply, as well as a set of mounts for installing the monitor as a mortise panel.
The touchpad is connected via a USB port. For secure mounting, the USB-B connector is equipped with screw mounts.



Touchpad



The touchpad connects to the computer as a separate device via USB and works independently of the monitor. For convenience, it is defined as a HID device and works on most operating systems immediately without installing drivers. Supported operating systems are Windows, macOS, Linux, Android.

Mounts


The monitor can be installed as a mortise panel. For this, special mounting brackets are used, which are installed in the holes around the perimeter of the monitor. The rubber seal around the perimeter prevents the penetration of contaminants beyond the working surface.



Also on the back cover there are holes for standard mounting VESA 100x100 mm screw M4.



Conclusion


The FPM-215W monitor can easily be integrated into any industrial system. No complicated setup required thanks to the Plug-And-play touchpad. Various mounting options allow you to install the monitor in a variety of configurations, from mortise panels, to installation on the table.
