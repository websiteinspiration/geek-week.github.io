Why the Xbox Series X will be the main purchase of 2020

In the battle of game consoles, the PlayStation has always been ahead of the Xbox. In March, companies announced the technical specifications of the platforms. This time, the Xbox beat the competitor in almost all respects, which caused a lot of discussion among gamers.



Xbox surprised by the quality of iron


Generational consoles are a rare and high-profile phenomenon in the industry. As a rule, this happens every 7 years. Xbox and PlayStation fans argue over which console is better. Companies are trying to lure gamers to their platforms through technical updates, game development, and marketing campaigns. All this translates into a tense confrontation. How it will end this time remains to be seen.

In terms of technical parameters and the number of users, Sony has always been ahead. According to the British business intelligence and research portal VGChartz, PS4 sold 154 thousand more consoles in the United States in November 2019, and 701 thousand more per year.
