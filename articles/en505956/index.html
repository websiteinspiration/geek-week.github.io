20 platforms for making money on testing

To test websites from home, you must be familiar with the Internet and speak good English. You will also need basic equipment such as a computer with a microphone and camera and a stable Internet connection. Most tests take 5 to 25 minutes, and on average, companies pay $ 10 per test.



UserTesting


Earn $ 10 for every 20 minutes of work (up to $ 60 per test). To become a website tester, send your email address and fill out the application online. Potential testers must pass the test to get any suggestions.

Respondent


Respondent is a research platform for conducting surveys, target groups and website testing. To get started, create an account using your email address, Facebook account, or LinkedIn. Then fill in your contact information, demographics and employment status. Once everything is ready, you can start viewing projects and apply for those that you like. At the time of writing this post, there was one proposal for testing a website where they paid $ 40 for 20 minutes, and in another proposal - $ 100 for 60 minutes. On the Respondent platform, you will need to log in and check the status of pending projects. Click here for a full overview of Respondent.

Userbrain


Earn money by expressing your opinion, completing tasks in Userbrain. Each project takes about 5-15 minutes, and you will be paid $ 3 for the test in your PayPal account. Payments are made weekly.

Testingtime


Earn up to 50 euros per review. Each review is conducted on Skype and usually takes 30-90 minutes. After completing the review, you will receive payment within 5-10 days to your PayPal account. TestingTime is available to residents all over the world who have an Internet connection and Skype installed on their computer.

TryMyUI


Earn $ 10 in 15-20 minutes of your time. Register first to become part of the TryMyUI team. Next you need to pass and pass a qualification test. A qualification test shows that you understand the process and requirements. Payments are made once every two weeks to your PayPal account.

Userfeel


Get $ 10 for expressing your opinion on different sites. To become a website tester, register and take the test task. Once your review has been approved, you will begin to receive orders by email. Payment is made through PayPal at the end of each week, and each test takes approximately 10-20 minutes.

Userlytics


Make money with reviews of websites, apps, models, concepts, and more. To apply, register an account, and then wait for offers to complete the task. After completing the work, you will receive payment through PayPal in the range from 5 to 90 US dollars, depending on the scale of the project on which you worked.

IntelliZoom


IntelliZoom is a company that conducts UX tests on computers and mobile devices. For tests in the form of simple surveys, they pay $ 2 and $ 10 for video reviews. Most tests take from 10 to 20 minutes, and payment is made through PayPal 21 business days after the completion of the review.

Validately


Validately hires testers to review mobile and full company websites. The cost of work varies depending on the scope of the review, but according to the description of this service, some companies pay up to $ 100 per test. Payments are made through PayPal within seven business days from the date of testing.

uTest


uTest employs independent contractors to test the quality of various software and hardware. According to their description of work on the Dice website, their customers are brands such as Google, Amazon, Netflix and others. To get started, fill out the online application (it will take about 10 minutes), go through the test task to demonstrate your technical skills, and then wait until the applications are sent to you by e-mail. I could not find out how much testers are paid, but the list of vacancies on Dice.com read: "In 2016 alone, we paid more than $ 20 million to testers around the world."

Loop11


Loop11 is an Australian company that sometimes accepts applications for paid website testing. You must pass a 5-minute qualification test to prove your suitability. There is no information on the size of payments on their website, but it is indicated there that they pay above average rates and bonuses. They also claim to work with companies such as IBM, JPMorgan Chase, and GoDaddy. According to the profile of the founder of LinkedIn, Loop11 has been operating since 2009.

Enroll


Earn money by testing sites. To register in the program, enter your email address, password and select your preferred test method (desktop computer, tablet or smartphone). Once you are fully registered, you will receive emails with offers. Tasks differ in nature, as does payment. I did one job, which took less than a minute, and they paid me 10 cents. Enroll makes payments through PayPal.

test IO


With the IO test service, you get paid for testing websites, applications and games, and you will earn $ 50 if you find a failure or error in the platform, if this is a critical problem, you will earn even more. If you did not find errors, you can also get paid for evaluating applications. The IO service pays monthly work payments using PayPal, Payoneer , Skrill or via bank transfer. The IO Test Platform was founded in 2011 and has offices in San Francisco and Berlin.

Intuit User Research


Test web products like TurboTax, QuickBooks, Mint, and their ProConnect products like ProSeries and Lacerte in Intuit. Tell customers about products, what you like and what not. The test lasts from 30 minutes to two hours, and for every 30-minute interval you get $ 50 on a Visa card. Most tests are done remotely using the BlueJeans web conferencing tool, although sometimes in-person tests are also conducted.

Checkealos


Get paid for testing sites on Cheakealos. Review sessions last 15 minutes and you will receive 8 euros for each session. This opportunity is open all over the world; you just need a PayPal account, computer, smartphone or tablet, a good internet connection and the ability to speak English or Spanish.

Pingpong


Get paid for testing websites and apps through PingPong. According to the description of their service on the website, you can earn 40-200 euros for 30-60 minute tests. Payment is made seven days after the test, and payments are distributed through Transerwise or PayPal. You must have a working computer with a webcam, but otherwise this opportunity is open to residents around the world.

Ferpection


With Ferpection, you can earn money by completing tasks using your mobile phone, tablet or desktop computer. I recently ran a test through their system, which took 45 minutes and they paid $ 25. What I like about Ferpection is that you don‚Äôt have to do the test with audio or video. You just take screenshots and answer text questions. Payments are made through PayPal on the last Friday of each month.

UserCrowd


At UserCrowd, you conduct quick design reviews to help improve products and websites. For most tasks, they pay 1-2 credits per test, and each loan costs $ 0.10. Once you collect 100 credits ($ 10.00), you can request payment via PayPal. I have not tried it yet (yet), but one user said that, in his opinion, you could earn $ 100 a year using this platform.

UXCrowd


The platform brings together users who are ready to test, and those who need testing. Clients get results much faster than at the agency, pay significantly less, and the choice of users is much wider: men and women, adults and young people, with different income levels, living in different cities and countries - all this will allow you to test the site on your own target audience.

Taskpay


Here we can earn thousands of rubles a month by performing very simple tasks, including testing.

Register on as many platforms as possible that test websites.

Make sure that you can use your advantages in practice and testing opportunities, as many companies will allow you to pass the test task only a couple of times.
Always check your work in the most thorough way, as many companies evaluate their testers, and those with higher scores tend to receive more and better-paid offers.

While you are waiting for these companies to send you an email with information about testing options, be sure to add short tasks, focus groups and tasks for digital earnings to your routine - this will ensure a constant flow of work and income.
