Maintain your own budget in Excel: the path of (non) analytics

Money loves an account!
Hello, the inhabitants of the planet! My name is Anton and I have been conducting personal accounting for 3 years. There are not very many conclusions yet, but they are also quite interesting. The first 2 years I used a special application, but in 2020 I switched to bare Excel and formulas - no programming and scripts. How it was and what came of it - under the cut with pictures.



First, a little introductory



At the end of May 2017, I thought it would be nice to start tracking what and how exactly I spend my money. I had small incomes then, but enough for a living. It was decided from June 01, 2017 to write down your income and expenses in a special application and look at what the money goes. After several test launches, the choice fell on one Polish developer with free features. So, on 01.06.2017, my starting balance was 4,031.49 rubles.



Until the end of 2017, entries were made from time to time. The application gave out not informative statistics, it didn‚Äôt suit me very much. Therefore, from 01/01/2018, bookkeeping is carried out strictly and carefully - up to the fact that every Sunday every Internet bank is opened and current balances are checked. This gave good results after a couple of months. The habit of making everything fastened fast enough, and things went more productive.



The application I used allowed me to keep track of 2 accounts for free. But soon I got too many of them and a couple of times I had to pay for the application. As a result, at the end of January 2020, I felt sorry for the money for a new extension, I downloaded all the data and started keeping records in bare Excel. Six months later, I can say that it is much more interesting and informative than in the application. I immersed myself in Excel itself, in statistics, and am ready to show my first results to the community.



Start. Reduce the balance from the application and in Excel



The application, although good, was given only records for export: 5600 lines of completely unstructured information. Okay, we took the file, recognized and ennobled all the necessary columns, cut all the unnecessary and gave it a beautiful view of the table.



At this moment, I begin to understand that the balances do not converge - I only have records, without starting amounts. A new table is created, all accounts and their balances are entered at the beginning of accounting - 06/01/2017. In order not to suffer with the categories of expenses, it was decided to completely copy the structure from the same application. A pinch of magic - and I got a very small and simplest file. For good, he showed me only my current balances, the level of income and expenses by years, and all records were kept in it.







First attempts to make friends with Excel



Losing all the existing analytics from the application was sad, so I started to slowly restore it on my own in Excel. It was immediately decided to abandon VBA since I am not a programmer from the word at all. I‚Äôm trying something, but it‚Äôs more like pampering. But back to accounting.

First, with the help of filters, obvious errors were cleared when filling out the entries themselves: typos, duplication of names, declensions and other features of the Great and the Mighty.



The second stage began experiments with analytics - movement charts, disaggregated by currency, by date, by account, and much more. At the same time, everything that was possible was actively googled and it looked like others keep records (in any way. It is too difficult to make and too simple an analysis).



I wanted to do everything as automatically as possible. Create a default configuration at the start, register all formulas and make only entries in the income-expense table. Excel should consider the rest itself. Too perfect, no?



2 months after the start of using Excel, I learned to handle tables, learned to automatically calculate the balances on all accounts and in total, figured out the top ten most used formulas, and began experiments on pivot tables and conditional formatting. Interestingly, and 2 months before pivot tables is this a lot or a little for a beginner?







PQ and PP



After about 2 weeks, I met with PowerQuery and PowerPivot. And if the first one didn‚Äôt help me much (since everything was done in one file), then the second one solved many problems. Pivot tables have become a little easier to create, and a few columns have been eliminated from the record table - they can be calculated in PowerPivot.



In general, PowerPivot is a rather complicated thing, and extremely sensitive to any actions. At the same time, any materials on it are few and many have to understand yourself. But the result can directly please, it is noticeable.



For example, through PowerPivot and table relationships, it was finally possible to build a summary with costs broken down by category (150+ pieces!). We had to build such a ‚Äúforehead‚Äù through a summary of the records, manually group the categories to the desired level and visually compare the values. This is very inconvenient, if only because any new category in the record table broke the entire structure of the summary. And it took a lot of time to recover. With PowerPivot, it took 3 clicks of the mouse.







The revolution



Somewhere around the same time, I get crowded in my file and another one appears for testing. In it, I can do whatever I want with the data, without fear of spoiling the results basically. There is only one problem - so that the formulas (and the results) are the same, you have to keep a table of records in both files at the same time. Simply copying new lines breaks the formulas and you have to interrupt everything with your hands. And after new hands-on line-by-line testing, transferring new features to the main file is also such a pleasure.



Therefore, in early June, it was decided to put the table of records in a separate file, and read formulas and graphs separately. Together with the table of records, the table of accounts and some noncritical little things moved. In the DATA file itself, the minimum calculation is only a couple of named ranges and a formula for calculating current balances. Just because downloading the file with the calculations to see the leftovers turned out to be a stupid undertaking.



The purpose of this file is to add rows to the records table, control account balances and add accounts, if necessary. For everything else - a file with calculations.



And here in it all the magic and power of Excel opened to the full. The file with calculations receives data through PowerQuery, works on them and gives them to formulas and pivot tables. Next to it, PowerPivot gives its data and links the existing tables into a single structure. From all this, a very good analytics is obtained. The number of graphs and calculations is growing every day, the volume of the file with calculations is constantly increasing, something is changing on the pages. And all this is 90% automated!







Future



Now I keep records in three files - this is DATA + file with simple calculations (BASIC) + file with advanced calculations (TEST). There are already 6800 rows in the records table, the total account balances have increased several times. I began to spend significantly less on impulsive purchases - they are just too lazy to make them, and if I bought them, then I am ashamed when you make them. In the summary table with the spending by categories it is very clearly visible how the costs of self-isolation changed - public transport and trips to cafes / restaurants + lunch at work dipped to zero. In June there is a good chance to finish the month in positive territory - the third time in 3 years, and even the third in a row. And it is very clearly visible at what point my wife stopped transferring money to me to pay bills and I began to transfer money to her to pay for products. But this is our inner kitchen.



I work on files every day in my free time. Today, for example, I interrupted all formulas for dividing into formulas IF ERROR - so less spam pop-up. In general, in the notes I have more than 20 ideas that can be worked on. Something is done easily, for some reason it is necessary to change the structure of all three files (I don‚Äôt want to), but something I just don‚Äôt know and need to google and try. For example, I can‚Äôt figure out how to build a budget for a month and control its implementation without breaking the table structure.



In general, I know what I want, I know how it should look. But more and more often, I come across the limitations of Excel itself and its capabilities. It‚Äôs interesting to try their own add-in for personal records, but what I saw I don‚Äôt like anymore. I think that I have more, more detailed and more accurate. Well, and much more in the process, I just realized all the analytics that was in the application. In any case, to be continued ...
