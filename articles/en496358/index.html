Getting ready to celebrate Cosmonautics Day online (the list is being updated)

For the first time, the planet is preparing to celebrate Cosmonautics Day with so many people under quarantine of varying degrees of intensity. Alas, organizing an offline event in order to celebrate a holiday in Europe, the USA, Russia and a large number of other countries will be at least irresponsible action. He came to the rescue online, where the celebrations moved. Under the cut is an updated list of links to events.



Global event "Yurina Night" : Due to the pandemic, large events in the Los Angeles Space Center named after Kennedy, London and other places. But the organizers of Yurina Night announced a global livestream featuring astronauts, popularizers, actors from the Star Trek: Voyager and Space TV series and other interesting people. The stream starts at 2 a.m. Moscow time on Sunday, April 12, in our hemisphere this is somewhat inconvenient, but the recording should remain.

The Cosmonautics Museum in Moscow conducts educational online for the second week. They promise events for the holiday, but have not yet announced which. Follow the page of online activities and the website of the museum.

North-Western Organization of the Russian Federation of Cosmonautics announcedhours-long festive stream on the Raphael and Co. canal. Cosmonautics historian Pavel Shubin will tell about his books about moon exploration, Alexander Khokhlov will outline the rules for selecting cosmonauts for the Roskosmos squad this year, member of the Cosmonautics Federation Dmitry Oliferovich will share the latest news from the American Artemis program to bring people back to the moon, and honored space technician Andrei Emelyanov Polina Kuznetsova, a researcher at the SSC of the Russian Federation - IMBP RAS, will tell about the cosmodromes of the Earth, will reveal to us the features of isolation, based on her work experience in space psychology and in SIRIUS isolation experiments, the artist Anastasia Prosochkina will give us her own view of space painting, and the St. Petersburg thereminist, multi-instrumentalist Alexander Romanova will play a dedication to the moon and inner space. Keep track ofFederation page.

Betelgeuse Radio will conduct a festive broadcast with the participation of astronomer Vladimir Surdin, writer Pavel Shubin and popularizer Alexander Khokhlov. There is no announcement yet, follow the official website and the FB page.

The ARCH Center will hold the Astrophysics News online on the evening of April 11 with Sergey Popov (watch the channel ) and in the evening of April 12, the director of the Skoltech Space Center Anton Ivanov will tell about the secrets of the fourth planet .

“Yurina night” in Perm - on April 12 they will spend online from homeevent organizers: Cyril Tsiberkin prepared a news digest about space 2019-2020 - about Nobel laureates, robots on the ISS, fading stars and exoplanets. Sergei Polishchuk will tell you how to maintain a “connection with space” even in quarantine, watching the stars from the balcony or window sill.

And finally, your humble servant plans two streams: April 11, 20 MSC lecture “Cosmic Tales”, April 12 at 20 MSC a stream with Kerbal Space Program, which I, it turns out, did not play for two years. I hope it will be fun to remember how there and what. Follow my channel .
