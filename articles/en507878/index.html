How to make Google fall in love with your author?

In September 2019, a new algorithm from Google EAT was released to combat low-quality and non-expert content. Most affected YMYL sites - Your Money or Your Life (wallet or life).







This hit hard on small and medium sites, large sites were only in the black. The reputation of the site and the author is evaluated separately, in order to get the maximum score in the assessment, it is enough to have one high reputation with the author or the site.



The author’s expertise is one of the key factors in ranking the Google EAT algorithm. The creator of the content can be a company or a person, Google speaks about this in the “Guide for assessors”, but it’s easier to create and monitor the reputation of an individual than an entire company. People trust other people more than organizations.



How to create an author’s reputation?



  1. Create an author page on your site. In the title, write "Name of the author" + "specialization";
  2. Put 3-5 links with the anchor "Name of the author" + "specialization" to this page from good sites;
  3. On social networks (preferably Twitter and Facebook), subscribe to other experts and join groups on your topic;
  4. Record some videos on YouTube, it is not necessary to show the face. Name the channel "Author Name" + "specialization."


Making articles and pages about the author



The article page should say who the creator of this content is, in which company and in what position he works. This is especially important for small or medium sized sites. Also a link to a page with a biography of the author.



On the author’s page should be:



  • Photo of the author;
  • Education;
  • Place and position of work;
  • Link to social networks;

Conclusion



Enter in the Google search bar “Author name” + “specialization”, if you are there on several pages, everything worked out for you. You can also add certificates, diplomas and awards on the author’s page that confirm your expertise.
