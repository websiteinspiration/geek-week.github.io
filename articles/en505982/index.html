Webinar "Winnum Machine Tools: Monitoring and Diagnostics of Production Equipment in Real Time"



We invite you to participate in the webinar "Winnum Machine Tools: real-time monitoring and diagnostics of production equipment." As part of the webinar, we will tell you about the Winnum solution for remote monitoring of equipment, consider all the advantages of this solution and conduct a live demonstration. The webinar will be held on June 16 at 13:00 Moscow time.


Winnum - a leading leader in advanced solutions for the industrial Internet of things, invites you to participate in a webinar.

What we will tell



How to increase the payback of equipment


Many industrial companies invest heavily in the re-equipment of production, but do not receive the required payback due to the fact that the production does not reach the planned capacity. The Winnum Machine Tools application makes the production process transparent, providing comprehensive and objective information about the operation of equipment for owners and top management.

How to increase the payload of equipment


Long change-outs, regular after-hours work, loss of time in inter-operational logistics - this is what any production is faced with, without objective control tools. The Winnum Machine Tools solution offers unique tools for loading control - from all kinds of detailed reports to an interactive digital double and provides production services with all the necessary information.

How to optimize manufacturing processes


Technology and management programs are one of the most significant reserves in optimizing production, which allows freeing up time for additional loading of equipment with the new range and updating the production plan. Constant monitoring of work under load in accordance with the specified criteria and monitoring of the cutting tool provides initial data for the optimization of technological processes, including the unification of the cutting tool, the adjustment of modes and the updating of standards.

Who will be interested



The webinar will be useful to IT specialists, production managers, network and technical specialists, project managers, engineers and researchers.

Participation in the webinar is free. To get a link to connect, fill out the registration form.
