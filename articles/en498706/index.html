Series of product webinars from Lenovo Data Center Group experts

We write a lot about unique infrastructure solutions that help various companies to reach a new level: reduce costs, ensure data security and solve other problems.

The current situation in the world has shown how important it is to be flexible and be able to adapt your business to the new realities as soon as possible. Nevertheless, many were not ready for this: in addition to understanding and awareness, knowledge and resources are also needed to choose the right solution, integrate it and work with it.

What do we offer?




We invite you to join online meetings with Lenovo experts from April 28 to July 2. Our experts will talk about Lenovo technologies and solutions for data centers of any scale in a simple and accessible language. Over the next two months, we will introduce you to the full range of our offers, including servers, storage systems, network equipment, software-defined systems, software. We will pay special attention to the issues of administration and maintenance of our solutions - at meetings you will gain theoretical knowledge, master the practical skills of working with Lenovo equipment, and experts from the product team and professional service will answer all questions.

For whom? 


Webinars are designed for a wide audience of IT professionals, especially those who are not yet familiar with Lenovo's data center offerings. However, our existing customers and partners will definitely learn something new.

When and how will all this take place?
