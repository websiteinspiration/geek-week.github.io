DGTL Design Community Meetup at Raiffeisenbank: Broadcast

We invite you to the DGTL Design Community meeting on February 20 : we will learn about the intricacies of working with interfaces for employees and how to conduct cool research.

Join us at 19:00 !



What we will talk about


How to prevent a product from dying quietly

Maxim Derevyankin, Raiffeisenbank

About powerful rangers, identifying problems in a product, checking ideas, rethinking the concept and finding the North Star. About why constantly asking users and what benefits it can bring.

Features of the development of the internal product and self-realization in the subjective world of uncertainty

Tatyana Pinchuk, Sberbank.

In the first part, about the features of product development for employees, about how the work is arranged, and that, as a designer, this product specificity gives me. The second is about the personal path, and about the difficulties that any designer may encounter who will one day begin to be responsible not only for his design, but also for the design of other guys.

Design at the turn of the century, or the role of a designer in 21st century business

Gigi Dzhanelidze, Design MTS Laboratory

When did the first product / industrial designers appear? Product designer, what role does he play in the product team? Self-development and motivation of designers. UX researcher and UX designer. Where does the border go, and is it there? Gigi will reveal all these topics in his report.
