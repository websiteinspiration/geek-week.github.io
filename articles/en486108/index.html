The next new product, BarraCuda Fast SSD, is a more portable solution that can easily fit in your pocket:





The drive is equipped with a USB 3.1 Gen2 Type-C connector and supports read / write speeds of up to 540 MB / s. Using the exFAT file system makes it possible to use the drive with both Windows and Mac computers (immediately after unpacking). LED backlight makes the drive visually spectacular.



This SSD is created not so much for gamers as for active users who need to always have the necessary files at hand - for example, for designers, game devs, photographers, editors, etc. It is not in vain that when buying, they give as a gift a subscription to Adobe Creative Cloud (a plan for photographers). Backups were also taken care of - the backup is carried out using the Seagate Toolkit utility .

BarraCuda Fast SSD has a capacity of 500 GB, 1 or 2 TB, prices - $ 95, $ 170 and $ 300, respectively.

The next step in data evolution


The topic of future data storage was also touched upon. Our company has proposed a transition from isolated disk drives to a digital world - when storages, software and systems not only interact with each other, but also work as a single harmonious organism. 



One of the trends now - autonomous vehicles - few people know, but here our company took part: together with Renovo partner we are working on self-driving cars. At CES 2020, a comprehensive solution of data management tools, software and car safety systems was demonstrated, which allows you to create entire car parks from unmanned vehicles.



Working with video is also not possible for a long time without high-quality and capacious drives. At the exhibition, we deployed a model of the film set to clearly demonstrate the improvement in the speed of post-processing of films through the use of modern solutions for data management.

The Monterey Bay Research Institute (MBARI) is engaged in deep-sea research and accordingly accumulates a huge amount of data that must be safely stored and processed. The latest Seagate solutions are designed to solve the problem and provide research teams with fast and efficient data collection, as well as their transfer to computer centers.

Also, Seagate solutions will not do without the production of a new level - plants, on which most processes are tied to the Internet of things, so the data stream from the sensors is enormous. This is nothing but the fourth wave of the industrial revolution in IT, where everything will be connected: houses, cities, manufacturing enterprises, vehicles, etc. And all this data volume (up to 175 zettabytes by 2025!) Will also need to be organized and stored. We are ready for these challenges!

Well, where now without 5G - not only manufacturers of smartphones and accessories for them work in this direction. At CES 2020, our company introduced the Vapor IO micromodular peripheral data center - it can be used to place data closer to endpoints, which increases the efficiency of information processing.
