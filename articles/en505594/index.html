How to turn a site visitor into a client: a guide on creating lead forms

I am the head of the i-complex Internet marketing agency. In our work, we very often encounter the problem of inefficient sites. But often even a bad site can be improved by working through the capture points of the visitor‚Äôs contacts.

Even the most modern site, created taking into account all the trends in the field of site building, will not lead you to a crowd of new customers if you do not properly work out all the points of contact with them.

It's early to rejoice when a user lands on your site. To make him want to sign up for you, it‚Äôs not enough to talk about how cool you are, how to effectively treat your teeth for 40 years and use only modern technologies and fashionable materials. You need to correctly ask him about it and directly indicate what needs to be done. Filing an application should be as simple and convenient as possible, and those who have not yet decided whether he wants to see you can get it with the right call to action or an appropriate form of capture.

General rules for all lead forms on the site


Consider the main rules for capture forms, violation of which kills the conversion of any site.

  • A call to action is mandatory (more about them below).
  • The field for entering the phone should be with a mask for entering in the format +7 (...) ... without the ability to specify characters other than numbers. From smartphones, when you click on the ‚ÄúPhone‚Äù field, a virtual keyboard with numbers should open.
  • Mandatory fields must be marked so that the user understands why the application is not sent. If the field is empty, it should be highlighted in red.
  • Indicate the call waiting time - it stimulates to action. If you do not work around the clock, then write that you will call back after so many minutes during working hours.
  • In any form where the user leaves his phone, it is necessary to comply with the requirements of 152- ‚ÄúOn personal data‚Äù and place a notice of consent to the processing of personal data with a link to the corresponding page.
  • No open captchas that ask you to enter characters from the picture to weed out robots - this is the last century!
  • On the smartphone, an open window with a form should fit on the screen, the cross for closing should be noticeable and large enough.

You can print it, hang it on the wall, give it to your developer, memorize :) Even one broken item can reduce the conversion of your site, so you should take it seriously.

What are the forms of visitor capture on the site


There are many different methods for converting a site visitor to a client. Consider only those that are guaranteed to work on service sites.

Online recording


If you use any kind of CRM, then it can be integrated with the site. By the way, most CRM have this opportunity, but not all. This gives the user the opportunity to familiarize themselves with the current schedule and choose a convenient time for recording, and you can even sign up without talking to the administrator. At the moment, this is the most convenient recording format that will only develop.





Express appointment booking


If you do not have CRM or you have not yet established integration, then the site should have a record form of at least two fields - name and phone. It is also appropriate to add a calendar with the ability to select the desired date and time, as well as a text field with a proposal to briefly describe what interests the client. This, in fact, gives nothing to the user, but it will be easier for the administrator to navigate the schedule and offer the client a suitable option for recording, which can reduce the total call time.



Call order


In addition to the recording forms, I recommend always adding the ‚ÄúRequest a Call‚Äù form to the site. Its advantage is that it does not visually oblige you to anything, and it can be used by people who do not want to call themselves and do not want to fill out the ‚ÄúAppointment appointment‚Äù form. Even if there is a widget with the current schedule, the site must have a call order - for those who are not ready to sign up, they want to get advice and talk with a specialist.

In the call order form, one phone is enough, the simpler the form, the better.
Suitable calls to action - ‚ÄúDo you want, we will call you back right now?‚Äù, ‚ÄúLeave your phone number and we will advise you on any question‚Äù, ‚ÄúWe will call you back within 1 minute and answer all questions‚Äù. The text on the send button is ‚ÄúOrder a call‚Äù, ‚ÄúWaiting for a call‚Äù, ‚ÄúCall me back‚Äù. And do not forget about the call waiting time. It is obligatory to place a call order in the header of the site and in the Contacts section.



Pop up


Can be displayed on top of the screen in the center or on top of the screen on the side. Basically, they place offers to call back right now and answer questions, but you can inform the client about the current promotion or the opportunity to get a discount on the service by calling right now.

The main ‚Äúdecency rules" when using pop-ups:

  • do not cover the entire screen
  • the cross for closing should be noticeable and large enough
  • don't pop up before 15 seconds

When analyzing the site of dentistry, we noticed that visitors were late on the ‚ÄúPrices‚Äù block, but after that they left the site without completing the target action. The problem was solved precisely with the help of pop-up: after 15 seconds the user is in this block (namely in the block, and not on the page!), We show him a pop-up with a proposal to clarify the cost of treatment.

The positive effect was not long in coming, which confirms that the pop-ups are still working, if used wisely.

Quiz form


Quiz is a trending Internet marketing tool that builds user interaction in a playful way. The bottom line is that we do not offer the visitor to immediately leave their phone, but ask him a series of questions to find out what interests him. This is a great way to start a dialogue with a ‚Äúcold‚Äù visitor. Many people visiting the site are still only looking for solutions to the existing problem, and it is important for such clients to offer help. Quiz on the site will help the user navigate the services and prices and increase the visitor‚Äôs conversion to appointment bookings.



Calculator


For all areas where the total cost of a service or product is made up of many variable components, interactive calculators are relevant. A calculator can be for one key service, such as implantation, or for a number of services. You can connect the calculator with the quiz - first find out about the user's problem, then offer him solution options with a cost calculation. In the lead form, in this case only the name and phone are needed.



Instant messengers


Messengers are now one of the most popular ways to communicate with a potential client. This is convenient for both the user and administrators. Many people do not like to communicate in voice or are looking for a doctor while sitting at work or with a small child. It will be more convenient for the administrator to deploy to answer customer questions and orient him according to the price list. If you use instant messengers - be sure to post information about them on the site, if not - it's time to start using them to increase sales. Telegram, WhatsApp, Viber icons with a call to ask you a question can be placed in the Contacts section, in the footer, header, in the price list, on the pages of services, promotions and in any other place where the user may have questions or desire to communicate with you.

Online consultant


Online consultants are still an actual way of communicating with a potential client in any field, as they allow you to instantly start a chat and do not require the user to enter contact information. The settings of the consultants are very flexible, you can change the appearance time, greetings for calling the visitor to a dialogue. Many services show the user‚Äôs path on the site, from which page he writes that he looked before. Be sure to put a photo of the operator with a real name in the chat - so you are more likely to attract the user to the dialogue and turn him into your client.



Stock Subscription


In addition to the offer to use your promotions, you can also receive an email from the client and notify him of new promotions if you are engaged in email newsletters.



Do not forget about calls to action


It is not enough to convince the user - he needs to say directly what he has to do. In marketing, this is called a call to action or call to action (CTA).

5 main rules for using CTA:

  • The call should be short and clear.
  • The call should concern one specific action - recording, subscribing, receiving a discount
  • The call should stand out from the rest of the page content
  • The call must be appropriate and appropriate to the content of the page / block
  • The call should be in every place where the user makes a decision or performs an action.

Try not to use dry and vague wording like ‚ÄúLeave a request‚Äù, ‚ÄúMake out‚Äù, ‚ÄúSend‚Äù. If possible, use an imperative mood - Fill in, Sign up, Call, Receive - but only where appropriate. Do not repeat one call and one capture form more than two times per page. Ideally, on one page it is better to use several calls and application forms: Make an appointment, Get a consultation, Clarify the cost of treatment, Request a call, Ask a question.

And most importantly, do not forget that you must keep your promises. If you promised to call in 10 minutes, you must do it, otherwise your client will go to competitors. We wish you productive communication with customers and good sales!
