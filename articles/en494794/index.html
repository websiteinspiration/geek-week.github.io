Zoom



One of the main satellite services of the current COVID-19 pandemic is Zoom. As the coronavirus spreads around the planet, forcing cities to quarantine and schools shut down, Zoom has become one of the leading tools with which business continues to work and schoolchildren learn.

ZOOM.US is a cloud platform for conducting online video conferencing and video webinars in high definition.

To start using the ZOOM platform, just go through the simple registration process and install the ZOOM client (client ZOOM) on a computer or mobile device for free. ZOOM.US has a full-fledged client for Windows, MAC, Linux, iPhone, Android +, there is an SDK (to write your own client) and a REST API (to integrate zoom.us into corporate business processes).

Pros:

The largest number of possible participants. Up to 100 people in one conference.
Ease of use. The interface is minimalistic, it allows you to launch, join and participate in conferences from any device.

Cons:

Limitations in the free version. Without a subscription, the conference and recording will last no more than 40 minutes. In addition, in the free version, the administrator of the video conference you created can only be you - with a subscription, you can appoint anyone.

Where to download : website , desktop , iOS , Android .
