Rushing into the abyss of the unknown


Even more fun when you need to deal with incomplete information. If you are not sure that your data covers the question you are interested in, take special care when drawing conclusions. That is why good analysts do not draw conclusions at all.

If analysts understand that they do not have all the facts and look in the dark, they try to be models of impartiality. Your consciousness must be open. Many people think that the goal of search analytics is to answer questions. This is a common mistake. In fact, they need to be set. Data mining by analysts allows you to ask the right questions.
