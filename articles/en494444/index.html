GS1350 - a special series of managed PoE switches for video surveillance systems


The maximum distance to the camera is 250 meters.

Standard PoE switches allow you to transmit a signal at a distance of not more than 100 meters. The GS1350 series of Zyxel managed switches designed for video surveillance systems completely solve this problem by increasing the maximum distance to powered devices up to 250 meters, which greatly facilitates the deployment of video surveillance systems.

Automatic camera recovery

Immediately after a malfunction of the IP camera is detected, the switch automatically restarts it and tries to restore its functionality.

The status of IP cameras is determined through the LLDP protocol , as well as through the
sending of ICMP packets.

Continuous PoE Power

The switch provides uninterrupted power to the IP cameras even when updating the firmware or configuration, which significantly improves the continuity of the video surveillance system. This feature ensures continuous power to cameras and other sensors during maintenance on the switch.

  • upload a new configuration file, or, conversely, return the current
  • settings to previous from backup
  • reset to factory settings
