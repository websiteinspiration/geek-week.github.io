Eco wood art


Part of the Eco Wood Art line is reminiscent of Ugears: also models made of thin birch plywood, also assemble without glue and extra tools. Recently, the brand launched a new line of interior designers: these are openwork designs with a "polygonal" design, which are mounted on the wall.



The details of the interior models of Eco Wood Art are painted on the front side in black, and on the inside they have grooves for fasteners. Squeeze the parts from the tablets, fold them together according to the scheme and fasten, and then hang on the wall. The coolest models: World Map , Eiffel Tower and Unicorn .

Decorate all walls by the end of quarantine: sounds like a plan.
