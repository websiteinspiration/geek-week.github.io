Deploy ASA VPN Load-Balancing Cluster

In this article, I would like to provide step-by-step instructions on how to quickly deploy the most scalable Remote-Access VPN access scheme based on AnyConnect and Cisco ASA - VPN Load Balancing Cluster .


Introduction: Many companies around the world, in view of the current environment with COVID-19, are making efforts to transfer their employees to remote mode. Due to the massive transition to remote work, the load on existing VPN gateways of companies increases critically and a very fast ability to scale them is required. On the other hand, many companies are forced to rush to learn from scratch such a thing as remote work.


In order to help businesses implement convenient, secure, and scalable VPN access for employees as quickly as possible, Cisco provides licenses for the AnyConnect multi-function SSL-VPN client for up to 13 weeks. You can also take ASAv for a test (Virtual ASA for VMWare / Hyper-V / KVM hypervisors and AWS / Azure cloud platforms) from authorized partners or by contacting your Cisco representative .


The AnyConnect COVID-19 license issuing procedure is described here .
