How to reduce the registration of damage under compulsory motor liability insurance from a few days to 60 minutes



To do this, we integrated the explosion schemes into the Concordia app for insurance experts and made the inspection report fully electronic; it can be signed right on the spot.

One of the types of insurance that the ‚ÄúConsent‚Äù provides is CTP. According to the Avito.Auto survey , 69% of drivers had an accident at least once, and 49% of them did it once or twice. Every year, thousands of people are faced with making payments on such a policy.

In order to receive payment under OSAGO, you need to call an expert from insurance. He will take a picture of the car and note damage in the inspection report. Then he will take the documents to the office, where specialists will calculate the amount that the company will pay to the victim.

Why not leave everything as it is


Because it is long and not as accurate as customers and insurance would like. The expert needs to get to the office, where specialists manually transfer the data and calculate the amount of damage. And the act itself can stand in line for consideration for a while.

As for accuracy: the standard act does not have an illustration in which it could be noted that it requires repair or replacement. Damage can only be recorded on such a document. And their number depends on the experience and attentiveness of the insurance expert.

Concord insurance experts have already worked with our application. Through it, it was possible to photograph damage, an inspection report, client documents and send everything to the back office. For this application, we needed to collect the update. In the new release, the task was to reduce the time for preparing inspection certificates for cars and increase the accuracy of calculating compulsory motor third-party liability insurance.


- It was necessary to come up with such a process so that the experts during the inspection could indicate damage using their working application on the smartphone, and the data was automatically transferred to the calculation program. Specialists in the office would only have to check the correctness of the data entered.

Sergey Dryga, project manager

This would help to get away from the paper act and would allow the formation of its electronic version. Such automation would save a significant layer of time required for reconciling photos from the inspection and entering data into the program. The idea was, but there was no evidence that this
could be done.

- We studied the market: they were looking for whether someone could realize a similar task. It turned out that there was nothing special to investigate. Other companies made attempts to transfer the process of indicating damage to the mobile application, but we were not able to find any successful implementations. Neither in the world nor in the Russian market have we been able to find suitable solutions that suit us in quality.

Dina Abyzova, Senior Business Analyst

To implement the idea of ‚Äã‚Äãtransferring damage information directly to the calculation program, we began to look for services that offer desktop programs for calculating damage after an accident.

How was it


There are several such companies on the market, we have chosen the German SilverDAT software product . The company provides industrial data on cars with which you can accurately calculate the cost of repairs in an accident.

It works like this: in the calculation program from SilverDAT, data about the machine is entered and on the explosion diagram damaged parts are noted, and also the repair work that needs to be carried out is indicated. After that, the amount of damage is calculated.


Explosion diagram - a schematic view of a structure with spaced components

Transferring a business process from desktop to application


Finding a service for calculating damage is important, but only part of the task. Next, it was necessary to shift the most necessary of the desktop versions of SilverDAT and the personal account of the insurance expert ‚ÄúConsent‚Äù.

Following several close meetings, the application transferred the choice of a car (type, make, model, modification), the choice of engine and gearbox, the indication of parts and their damage, the choice of repair actions, data synchronization and display of the calculation amount.


Stages that remained in the mobile application + EDS stages

The calculation of damage itself remained on the desktop, this is a complex business logic that is convenient to work with in this way. And before sending information about the amount of damage, the data is checked by specialists in the office. There is no way without it.

We integrate explosion schemes


SilverDAT provided us with svg schema files, and these are just rules for marking up vector files.
We needed to write a parser that would parse the file and pull out the graphics and metadata of the auto parts from it. For a month we collected it and tested it on different mobile devices.

Then it was time to develop a convenient interface for working with explosion schemes on phones. For example, the selected part should be correctly ‚Äúhighlighted‚Äù, without touching the elements nearby, scaled, etc. There were also no ready-made solutions, they did everything from scratch.

Digitally signed electronic inspection report


Ultimately, integration with SilverDAT and the introduction of explosion schemes were needed to automatically generate an electronic act that can be signed right at the site of the inspection.

Due to the fact that now the expert can choose a specific vehicle model in the application, note all the damage and send it to the office for calculation, the inspection and calculation process has collapsed to about 30-60 minutes. All of it lies in the mobile application, and you do not need to go to the office.

After inspection on site, you can immediately form an electronic act. The client signs the document with a digital signature via SMS, and the expert strengthens the digital signature through a special service. After that, the Consent client can go home and expect a payment from the insurance company.


Part of the electronic signature process

What now


Arriving at the place, the expert, using a mobile application, inspects the car and draws up an application for damages: photographs the car, selects the make and model you need, indicates damaged parts in the explosion diagram.

For each part, the application offers different types of repair work: diagnostics, painting, repair or replacement. The application is checked in the back office, then the data is directly loaded into the program to calculate the damage.


Explosion schemes of 60 manufacturers of cars, motorbikes, minibuses and a mobile home are available in the application: 2500 models and 20-30 structural groups in each model. As new models become available, we will update the data.
