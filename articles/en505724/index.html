Russian fair use: where is the copyright border?

The discussion of copyright flares again . People blame copyright for their totality and deafness. Let's see what can still be done in Russia. What are you, Russian fair use?

Yes, you can put a portrait on the cover!

There are many bans in the law, but a number of benefits have been made. I offer a guide to these benefits.

You can use the work if the copyright holder has not objected to you within three years after use. This is called ‚Äústatute of limitations.‚Äù In this way, the author can allow you to use it once, without wasting time on a legal contract, but simply inactive for three years. (Article 196.)

You can obtain permission orally, without a written contract, if you are a newspaper or magazine. (Article 1286.)

You can show a work at an exhibition, and can be reproduced in the exhibition catalog, and can be reproduced in publications about the collection if you are the owner of the work. (Article 1291.)

If you are depicted in a picture or photograph, you can reproduce and distribute them (Article 1291) -

  • If you added this work to your article or book,
  • if you added this work to books and articles about you,
  • if you distribute copies without profit, that is, you can simply show yourself to all people.

You can do anything for personal purposes, only for yourself, if the work has been published. This benefit does not apply to copying buildings and copying books. (Article 1273.)

It is possible for science and study (Articles 1274 and 1275) -

  • to quote published works,
  • use published works in study books and programs,
  • record and transmit electronic abstracts of dissertations,
  • copy published articles and short excerpts of published works for study and exams.

You can perform and perform published works without profit in educational, medical and social institutions. (Article 1274.)

Can be distributed in the media (Article 1274) -

  • articles published in the media on current issues, unless the author explicitly forbids this; what are current issues, God knows;
  • publicly delivered speeches;
  • works that sound and are visible on events, in reviews of such events (this is close to citing).

You can publish books in dotted font for the blind without profit. You can add sign language. (Article 1274.)

You can make skits. (Article 1274.)

It is possible for libraries and archives without making a profit (Article 1275) -

  • copy works and distribute copies inside their premises,
  • copy works for safety,
  • copy published articles and short excerpts of published works for visitors,
  • copy and store sites if these copies are not accessible to anyone.

You can create and distribute photos of buildings and landscape art that are visible to people. This is called freedom of panorama. (Article 1276.)

It is possible to perform a work at official ceremonies and at a funeral. (Article 1277.)

It is possible to perform a work for the court. (Article 1278.)

Anything can be done (Article 1259) -

  • with official documents of the authorities, including laws and judicial acts,
  • with official symbols and signs, including emblems, orders, money and stamps,
  • with folklore that has no author
  • with protocol messages about events and facts where there is no creativity (for example, a bus schedule or a program guide),
