Services based on neural networks for designers

Designer Fyodor Volkov made a selection of services in which neural networks help designers save work time and get rid of routine tasks.


uKit


Service from Russian developers of instant redesign and site page code update. Automatically generates a new version of the landing page or company page, taking into account modern requirements. uKit combines the algorithms of generative design and neural networks. The user configures the service using a convenient and intuitive constructor.



How does it work?


At the first stage, uKit analyzes the structure of the site and recognizes its individual elements. Next, the system separates common design elements from unique content (text, photos, contacts, etc.) and builds materials in the content tree, which repeats the layout of information on the old site. This step is necessary in order to maintain the site structure and simplify the perception of information. Then uKit rebuilds the content tree and automatically generates a new version of the landing page or company page that meets modern and design standards. And after a few minutes, a new version of the site becomes available in the Ukit designer.


In order for uKit to analyze and compose your site, you need to leave a link to your mail and site domain.
