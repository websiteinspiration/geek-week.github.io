Subscribe to JetBrains Academy: getting ready to launch and answering questions

Hello, Habr!







We recently announced that early access to JetBrains Academy is coming to an end and the platform is transitioning to a subscription model. This is an important step for the product. Therefore, we tried to get as much feedback as possible and collect as many questions as possible. Here we will try to answer the most popular of them.







Why does the platform get paid?



We want the JetBrains Academy platform to continue to evolve. Paid product itself ensures its sustainable development. Free products also have a price, they just pay differently. We do not hide our intentions, and our pricing policy is as transparent as possible. Creating a paid product, we, as developers, can afford to establish higher requirements for the quality of educational content and product functionality, as well as receive more direct and relevant feedback.







In addition, when students not only devote time to study, but also invest money in it, they receive additional motivation and are more responsible for learning.
