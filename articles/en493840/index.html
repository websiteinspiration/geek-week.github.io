Get best of the week

Reviews: Asana, Jira, Bitrix-24, Trello, YouGile. What do you like and what infuriates?

I sat down here to choose a management system for our department. We are the second line of technical support, we close jambs that our developers did not have time to fix. We help clients configure software for secure workflow.

We have 17 people in the department. We are doing business in 1C, but the management system for communicating on tasks is clearly not enough. Tired of all the working communications being conducted in Telegram. Where important information and memasics fall. Often - at the same time.

I thought: how to choose a normal system? No specific requirements yet. I looked at the reviews - one pathos: “Helping the business to work”, “Increasing efficiency”, “The best tool for agile teams”.

I decided to choose the reviews.Already another picture, less rosy: “The worst technical support in the world”, “Wild brakes”, etc. In general, I read what people say, cataloged a little.


Collected and summarized opinions on 8 control systems. Started with the least known, finished with the most popular. Divided into two blocks - what “comes in” and what infuriates. At the same time, I made a price table at the end. I’m sharing, it’s useful to someone. Well, we will test and choose in practice the whole team.
