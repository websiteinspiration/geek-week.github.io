The future of voice assistants



Vadim Pushtaev, the head of infrastructure development for the voice assistant Maroussia, will talk about the present and future of voice assistants. Will the time come - and how soon - when it will be possible to communicate with the voice assistant, as with a full-fledged interlocutor? In addition, Vadim will share the experience of the developer and teacher.





Egor Tolstoy - man and ... Submarine
