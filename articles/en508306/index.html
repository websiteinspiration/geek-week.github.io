Is there always one truth?

There are almost 62 thousand people on the Internet who know how I feel about digital “improvements”, “restorations” and “restorations”. It is possible that there are even more of them, if we consider the possible reposts of my habr-text about the neural network . However, few people are aware that for me this topic is everyday and routine, because I read quite often vague inscriptions.



It all started quite invisibly - I decided to write a light text about how reliable the information that the foreman from the movie "Operation Y" reports to Fedya sent to him.







The lyrical part of this story can be found on Picabu ( https://pikabu.ru/@motopila - the text will be laid out in the afternoon), but here I want to talk about a very expected technical problem that I had to face.



The thing is that I came across an inscription in a newspaper being folded by Shurik and I decided to find this newspaper. As a search on the Internet showed, I was not the first to pay attention to the heading “Hatred of Fascism”, but I did not meet any attempts to determine what kind of newspaper it was. I had to take up the mouse, search for it myself and state what was found in the “lyric part”. However, in the process of research it turned out that there are more than one newspaper in this scene.



1) Shown in close-up Izvestia with a piece of Charlie Chaplin’s autobiography

2) At least two pages from Pravda with which Shurik beats Fedya

3) A newspaper that Shurik is going to collapse



And in order to find out, I had to stop watching the version on the official Youtube channel of the Mosfilm film concern and study the contents of sites, links on which it would be illegal to place on Habré.



Yes, that's right - the official restored version turned out to be unsuitable for use as a historical source.



And this is how it looks in comparison.

And if you say that reading newspaper headlines on such frames is not the best idea, then I will not agree, and that’s why.

Headline in the newspaper True: "Demonstration of power and unity"







Yes, if you watch Youtube, then there are just blurry spots. The word "Demonstration" was among my options, but there were no ideas about the rest of the title.



But what happens if you compare two options.







On the right is a well-read inscription "Demonstration of power and unity." The visible part of the word “TRUTH” is not related to this page, but on other frames you can see a small word “Truth” at the top of the page.

headline in the newspaper True: “Towards new victories in the building of communism”







At first I assumed the word “NEW”, but the ideas ended there. An unofficial source of inspiration suggested the right option.







"NEW" and "CONSTRUCTION" - for sure. "COMMUNISM" consists of different personnel.



The letters after “NEW” are more likely to be similar to “VICTORIES” than to “SUCCESSES” (both have 7 letters).



Particles “K” and “B” visible in other frames are selected according to the structure of the phrase.



And, no - you cannot read “CONSTRUCTION OF COMMUNISM” on any of the frames in the video downloaded from Youtube.

And this story is not about how good I am.

It’s not that I plan to find specific issues of newspapers and answer the question of whether there was ONE newspaper “PRAVDA” or whether Gaidai put bookmarks in the film in the form of newspaper headlines supplementing the main plot.



I just want to remind the name of my habr article about improving images with the help of neural networks - “When I hear the words“ restored the neural network, I’m going to check backups ”



And this is not a figurative expression, but a literal one - I really want to hope that somewhere there are backups with unrepaired scans of films, books, audio recordings, etc. ... Or at least somewhere there are backups of restored versions with a quality different from the one you need to upload to Youtube and online cinemas.







I want, but I can not, because there is no hope that a commercial company will store absolutely redundant sources. After all, we constantly hear news about the loss of source codes for computer games ( https://habr.com/ru/post/141022/ ), and the source programs, unlike films, are a really important asset.



Therefore, all that remains for me is to believe that the guys baking magnetic tapeswill be able to win us some more time. The time in which we can develop approaches that will help us reduce the damage from digital wear .
