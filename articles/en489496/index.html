Search for employees and job search: my view from inside and outside

It so happened that I was involved in the search for a new team based on a total of twenty people. And then a year later, she herself began looking for work in the labor market and passed about a dozen interviews. I want to share my experience in finding employees and participating in the competition in the candidate market.


STAFF SEARCH: view from the employer


I’ll note right away that I am not a professional HR recruiter and not a psychologist, but on the client’s order I was engaged in the selection and primary training of a team whose specialists worked in several cities of the Russian Federation. The experience on the “other side of the barricades” was obtained in such lessons for me.



  • Any contact with the employer may be the first and last, depending on what you say or not say. I conducted job interviews where constant communication with people was supposed and it was important to maintain a positive emotional contact. And when the interviewee in the third minute of the conversation said “b *** b” to such a position, then, consider, for me the conversation was over, even though he apologized. If a person has such a culture of speech, then he will not be able to constantly control it in client work. Although I do not deny that he is suitable for any other place where communication is not a key aspect of the work.
