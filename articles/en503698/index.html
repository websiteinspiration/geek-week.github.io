Remote accounting - as a business benefit

The introduction of a self-isolation regime, non-working days, self-isolation, and other restrictive measures in a pandemic prompted an even greater number of employers to transfer employees to remote work from home. Bookkeeping is an activity that can be carried out remotely. The main thing is to organize the work process properly and not to violate regulatory requirements.

Accounting is a procedure by which the organization and regulation of accounting of a company, organization or institution of any type is carried out. Since the work of accounting is directly related to the finances of the organization, experienced and trusted employees should deal with it. However, small and medium-sized enterprises today do not need their own accounting department. The same tasks, and at a lower cost, can be solved by remote accounting.


What is remote accounting? His task is to record all financial transactions of the customer and their documentation, preparation of reports, statements, preparation of payment documents, accounting of fixed assets, settlements with suppliers, sales accounting, etc., therefore, he must be well versed in the client‚Äôs business, understand what operations are worth for specific documents.

Benefits ...


Ideally, such a remote employee should provide a reduction in the tax burden on the business, offer the best option for documenting operations, avoid debts to buyers or contractors due to delays in paperwork, monitor the accrual and payment of taxes, salaries and bonuses to employees.

By hiring a remote specialist or an outsourcing company, the company can significantly save on bookkeeping. In addition, the outsourcer is interested in constantly improving the skills of their employees, they have extensive experience in communicating with regulatory authorities, and are always aware of legislative changes. An outsourcer also communicates with representatives of regulatory authorities. A specialist who conducts remote accounting, calculates the amount of taxes in advance and tells the company management how they are received, how to reduce them.

In addition, if bookkeeping is entrusted to an outsourcing company, the customer is not obliged to charge insurance premiums and provide accountants with other social guarantees, to deal with personnel training and advanced training. All this is undertaken by a specialized accounting company. The contract with her describes the entire list of services provided. And if you need professional advice, the remote company will provide a similar service for an additional fee.

... and risks


However, remote accounting is the higher risks: operational, personnel, reputational risks, information security risks, leakage of confidential information. It is also less able to effectively control the work of the involved specialists, a potential violation of the deadlines for reporting in the event of termination of the contract.

Therefore, despite the obvious benefits, an ‚Äúaccountant in the distance‚Äù is an option with certain inconveniences. An outsourcing company is a more reliable alternative for almost the same money. She can provide the most competent in a particular field employee. Some outsourcing companies provide such additional services as legal advice, personnel records, tax optimization, access to referral systems, check of counterparties.

The outsourcer can be entrusted with full accounting or part of the work, for example, reporting or payroll. It is most convenient to instruct the outsourcer to prepare and submit all the reports that the company has: reports to the IFTS (profit, VAT, personal income tax, PFR, FSS, MHIF, transport tax, land tax, property tax, excise taxes and others). The outsourcing company will take care of the creation of an electronic signature and use it in the preparation of reports and their transmission over the Internet.

The contract with the outsourcer, as a rule, prescribes the volume and schedule of work, criteria for assessing the quality of services, the size and mechanism of payment of remuneration, provisions regarding confidentiality of information, trade secrets and liability of the parties. It is necessary to prepare the necessary documents and agreements, determine the algorithm for the remote work of a financial specialist. The customer provides the outsourcer with access to primary documents and the accounting system. There are a number of accounting systems - 1C, the My Business web service, etc. The most common is 1C.

It is also necessary to determine the measure of responsibility of the outsourcer for their own mistakes. If an outsourcer insures his activities against accounting errors, then there is no fear that errors in accounting will entail fines.
