About Brand


In 2019, LED bulbs of the Spectrum LED trademark, which belongs to the Polish company Wojnarowscy, first appeared in Russia. The company started in 1990 as a supplier of lighting equipment for factories, railways, mines and medical institutions.

The products undergo a two-stage quality control, the first stage is carried out by the company's specialists at the manufacturing plants, and each batch is re-checked in its own laboratory in Poland.

Light flow




Spectrum LED lamps are preheated for 1 hour until performance stabilizes. When LEDs are heated, the luminous flux can decrease from 5% to 15%, this is one of the features of their operation. Both samples showed good results, the brightness in a stable mode after heating is much higher than stated. For a 7W model, the number of lumens is 18% higher; for a 10W model, it is 5% higher.

A good indicator is the temperature of the LEDs up to 100 degrees. This favorably affects the life of the LED lamps.





The maximum heating temperature of modern LEDs used in conventional lamps is 120-125 degrees. In the specifications of the highest quality LEDs, the maximum heating temperature is in the range of 140-150 degrees.

The LEDs of the Spectrum LED bulbs during testing showed a temperature of 84 to 96 degrees, that is, they work in a comfortable temperature range, which means you can expect a long service life.

Color Temperature and Color Rendering




Both tested LED lamps have a color temperature of 3000K, like incandescent lamps. In the specifications, the color rendering index is> 80 Ra. The measurement results confirm this value, which was Ra 81. For use in residential premises, a minimum of Ra 80 is required.
