Overview of the 3D dental scanner Medit Identica T300


In a new review, we introduce you to a device for quickly creating dental impression models. Introducing the Medit Identica T300 3D Scanner.

In addition to its own software included in the package, the Korean manufacturer offers users of its products a cloud server Medit Link to synchronize the work of various specialists.

We will describe in detail the functionality and features of the Identica T300 scanner and the supplied software products.

About Medit



In 2000, Korean digital specialist Minho Chang created Medit. From the very beginning, the founder was focused on equipment for the dental industry.

At the beginning of the 21st century, the niche was filled with large-sized and expensive scanners that individual companies could afford. The equipment was difficult to use, it required special knowledge and experience.

In 2004, a Korean company developed a range of large-size scanners familiar to the market. In 2008, Medit introduced its first compact 3D scanner, which was placed on the desktop. Since that time, the manufacturer has been developing and creating high-tech equipment for digital information processing in dentistry.


Source: medit.com

In 2013, Medit launched Identica Blue. The scanner worked on the basis of a completely new technology for that time, which was based on the use of 2 cameras and a blue LED beam. The device created a three-dimensional model of the wax imprint of the jaw. The revolutionary device was immediately noticed and highly appreciated by specialists in the dental industry.

In developing the following scanner models, Blue Light technology has been used repeatedly.


Source: facebook.com

2017 and 2018 were an important milestone in the professional growth of the manufacturer. During this period, the Medit Link cloud storage was developed, colLab 2017 software, the Medit i500 3D scanner.

Features Medit Identica T300



Source: medit.com




Description



Source: m edit.com The intra-oral

3D scanner Medit Identica T300 is designed for comfortable and quick digitization of the jaw. The device provides a finished picture in just 24 seconds. The platform is designed for the simultaneous placement of up to 8 casts of teeth, subject to a given intermediate distance. Using Identica T300 increases the productivity of orthopedic dentists, freeing up to 8 working hours per week.


The company has created an integrated system for the work of specialists: in addition to a 3D scanner that transfers data with an accuracy of 0.007 mm, users can use software with a clear interface and a cloud platform for collaboration. ColLab manages the scan settings (workspace, platforms) and processes the received data. To digitize the casts of both jaws, you need to use an additional platform.


Source: medit.com

Platforms are installed and fixed with magnetic locks on the rotating base of the scanner. On platforms, objects are fixed that remain stationary all the time during the work process. Despite the very strong attachment, it takes several seconds to install and remove casts.

Contents of delivery



The 3D scanner Medit Identica T300 is equipped with a set of necessary accessories:

  • 3 platforms: for working with jaw impressions, with separate teeth, standard;
  • Clamps for platforms;
  • Platform for calibration;
  • Screen to protect the bottom of the device;
  • Height adjustment device;
  • Cables: network, USB;
  • USB stick with software.

Preparation for work



Source: youtube.com

Before using the Medit Identica T300, you need to connect the device to your laptop and install colLab 2017. After that, set the print and monitor the workflow on the monitor in real time.

Source: youtube.com

For comfortable work and the ability to exchange data for the services of users of Medit - proprietary software colLab 2017 and the cloud platform Medit Link. In the software, you need to select the platform and mark the work area on the diagram. Models are stored in .stl format. Medit Link allows you to provide dental technicians with access to the data.

Optional accessories



Source: medit.com

Depending on the tasks facing the doctors, the manufacturer offers to purchase additional equipment: a module for scanning in color, a set of platforms for working with articulators.

Comparison of Medit Identica T300 and Shining 3D AutoScan DS-EX Pro

Advantages and disadvantages


To summarize the review, highlight the main advantages and disadvantages of Identica T300:

Strengths:

  • High speed and accuracy of information processing;
  • Clear software and cloud platform Medit Link;
  • Interchangeable platforms for scanning various elements of the oral cavity (teeth, jaws).

Weaknesses:

  • Large size compared to desktop scanners with similar functionality and intraoral models.

Summary


Medit Identica T300 is a 3D scanner widely known in the professional dental environment for creating three-dimensional models of jaw casts. The device saves time both for doctors and dental technicians, increases the performance of the clinic as a whole. Digitization of the jaw lasts 24 seconds, the measurement accuracy is 0.007 mm. Along with high speed and accuracy, an obvious advantage of the T300 is the ease of use of the scanner, intuitive software, and a cloud platform for storing and transmitting data.
