Cisco HyperFlex: Launch, Configure, Cloud Integration - April 21-22



Good afternoon friends!

Cisco and Linxdatacenter are inviting you to participate in the two-day Cisco HyperFlex Technical Webinar.

Description


At this two-day intensive webinar, you will learn how to launch and operate the Cisco HyperFlex hyperconverged solution, build disaster-resistant solutions on its basis, and organize backup and recovery.

In addition, the webinar discusses a number of additional Cisco software products for managing the data center infrastructure, optimizing it, and creating a private cloud with automation and self-service capabilities.

The course includes a demo part, which will show the process of launching HyperFlex, the main aspects of its administration, as well as the capabilities of automation tools.

The target audience


The webinar is designed for architects of data centers, administrators of virtualization systems, architects, system engineers and service engineers who are involved in the implementation and (or) management of server infrastructure.

Webinar dates: April 21-22, 2020
Webinar time: 10-00 - 13-15

  • 12: 45-13: 15 - Demo: HyperFlex Administration

    • CWOM - infrastructure optimization for current loads

  • 11: 45-12: 45 - Demonstration: ensuring data availability:
    • Replication, stretched cluster
    • HyperFlex + Veeam Backup
  • 12: 45-13: 15 - Demonstration: Control, Automation and Optimization Systems
