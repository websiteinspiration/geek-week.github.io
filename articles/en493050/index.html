Today, it is time to publish the results of the survey from last Friday and, to be honest, this is the first time that the data received has almost no meaning. The fact is that the voting of the participants practically did not solve the problem facing the survey. According to the comments, one may get the impression that the respondents did not quite understand what was required of them, but in fact the reason for such a misunderstanding is always an insufficiently accurately formulated question. What did we learn and what did we really want to know? The answer is under the cut.
