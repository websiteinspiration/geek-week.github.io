WWDC 2020 is coming to an end. In this regard, Avito, Redmadrobot, Touch Instinct, Wrike, as well as Alex Patsay and Slava Karpenko recall important points from conferences of past years.



Apple Worldwide Developers Conference (WWDC) - A global conference for developers on Apple platforms. It takes place every year in California, but this year, for obvious reasons, everything will be held online.



About how the trip happened



Many of the participants attended the conference more than once, and each of them has their own feelings from this moment.





- The first time I was at WWDC in 2002, when the conference was held in San Jose. The last two - in 2018 and 2019 - were also held there, by the way. And before this conference was held in San Francisco. I remember the times when Apple took developers to buses, drove them to Cupertino for an event called Beer Bash - this is a concert, beer, food right in the courtyard of the company's old office at 1 Infinite Loop. Generally unusual impressions, you will not forget them.

Alex Patsay, Parallels Product Director
