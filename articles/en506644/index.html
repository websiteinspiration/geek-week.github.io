New challenges for managers in remote work. Part 2

We continue to discuss the intricacies of managing a remote and distributed team. In the last article ( New challenges for managers in remote work ), we examined the technical and instrumental nuances in organizing remote work and proved the importance of using an administrative management system. Now we will consider particular issues that require attention in the current situation.

(This material was developed jointly with the head of the development department, who has extensive experience in building from scratch, preparing and managing development teams.)

General impressions of remote work


During the time that has passed since the last article was published, I read reviews and collected information from colleagues regarding the experience gained in organizing remote work.

The share of companies that do not plan to return to offices is growing in direct proportion to the time elapsed since everyone switched to remote work. If you extrapolate this trend, you can understand that the future lies in remote work. As positive examples of companies that, already now, understand and accept the introduction of remote work, you can cite: Mail ru - until September; Qiwi - until September; Avito - until September; T-Systems - until September; Dino Systems - until September; tutu ru - completely switched to udalenka; CityMobile - for an indefinite period; Oracle - for an indefinite period; RedCode - completely on the remote; Alfa Bank - until the new year; lamoda until September; Facebook - until the end of the year; Google - until the end of the year; Twitter forever; Discovery Channel until September. MSU plans to teach students completely remotely in the new academic year.

Many companies, especially international ones, before switching to remote work, arranged their work processes in such a way that remote work is no different for them from office work. Examples of successful organization of remote work include RUSAL, Severstal, Mars, Sberbank, Alfa Bank, which had a good backlog for the transition to remote work, which they have now further strengthened both in terms of technology and in terms of processes.

Most of the employees ( according to a survey conducted by hh in May 2020 - 65% ) are sure that working remotely is much more convenient and efficient than in the office. The exception is those who have a small living area and who are prevented from working by frequent distractions from family members (according to a survey conducted by hh in May 2020 - 47% ).

Managers and owners of such enthusiasm neither share. Many, for the present, plan to return to work in the office. Among the problems identified by managers are a drop in productivity ( according to a survey conducted by hh in May 2020 - 65% ), lack of coordination among the teams ( according to a survey conducted by hh in May 2020 - 40% ), a decrease in the quality of work ( by According to a survey conducted by hh in May 2020 - 30% ), increasing the complexity of control, the loss of the overall picture of the work.

In our life there have already been many events that have caused serious and irreversible changes, and many more such events will be. Often, we get used to living in a new reality, forgetting about what events shaped it. For example, until 2011 it was normal to smoke in an office, and now no one even remembers that once there were times when people smoked in offices.

We will talk about the importance of processes, requirements for managers, an administrative management system, and some nuances that need special attention when working remotely.

Using a single conceptual apparatus and methodology for organizing work


During remote work, the amount of interference and distortion in the communication channels is growing significantly. If, at the same time, several people exchange information at the same time, or people of different cultures, peoples, education or values, the problems become even greater. When working in one office, this is decided by the fact that you can come up and talk, draw on paper or a board, in extreme cases, ask another to help translate. When working remotely, all of these solutions are not available, and you must use other tools.

The main things to start the systematization of the conceptual apparatus:

  • a single knowledge base with a simple and understandable structure, which is constantly updated and updated;
  • availability of regulations and instructions on how to resolve typical issues, if something is not clear. Better if, the development of regulations and instructions will start the head, and then, you can pass on to others;
  • the presence of a management culture in which people are not afraid to ask questions when they arise, and the leader is open.

All this requires from the head:

  • maintain an open relationship in a team;
  • spend time on putting communications in order, training employees, developing knowledge bases and guidance documents.

You can start, for example, by creating a common glossary, which is initially filled out by the head, and then. complement and support employees.

Availability of collaboration tools


As you know, the most effective is communication on a whiteboard with markers, in the presence of the whole team. In the context of remote work, we lose this communication method. It is necessary to compensate for this loss. This applies not only to discussions of architecture issues in the project team. It may also be necessary to see, show or do together during the transfer of knowledge between team members, user training, or simply helping users.

I spoke in detail about the technical side of remote work in a previous article. Previously, security blocked such tools. Now it will be necessary to allow them to be used in order to increase efficiency.

And, there is a chance of being in a situation where it becomes completely unclear where we are, in general, and what is happening.

The ability to attract remote employees in the regions


Remote work has opened up unprecedented economic opportunities for employers. To work in the office, it was necessary either to attract employees in the city where the office was located, or to open an office in the city where the employees were located. Thus, offices were opened only in large cities with high labor costs and office maintenance.

Now there is no need to open an office, and it is possible to attract remote workers from the regions, the average wage in which is lower than in the metropolises. In addition, employees from regions have higher motivation to work remotely in large companies. This should help increase or at least maintain the profitability of the business, as well as increase the uniformity of distribution of qualified people across the regions.

At the same time, it does not matter where the employees performing the control function are located. To save them, you can also transfer to the regions.

Legal support for remote work


To minimize possible risks, it is necessary to modify and renew agreements with remotely working employees and other regulatory documents of the company.

I will leave a deep study of this topic to HR specialists and lawyers. I‚Äôll simply indicate that for productive remote work, you may need to make adjustments to regulatory and legal documents. For example, to normalize the time of availability or sanctions for not answering a phone call within a certain time.

The difference between free schedule and remote work


In the absence of fixed and approved agreements, each of the parties interprets all not clearly fixed points in its favor. One of such moments is the presence at the workplace, and the execution of work during working hours, when working from home. In addition, often, the concept of "remote work" is replaced by the concept of "work on a free schedule."

Earlier, I came across such manifestations during episodic work of employees from home, and now managers are massively paying attention to this. It looks like this - the employee does not answer the call, does not read mail, misses appointments, and generally does the work at the wrong time. In response to legitimate questions, you get the answer ‚Äúwell, I did everything until 23:59,‚Äù ‚Äúwell, I did everything until 08:00 the next day‚Äù ... (the list goes on and on).

To minimize problems with the substitution of the concept of remote work with the concept of work on a free schedule, they should be regulated and clearly divided. You need to pronounce this when applying for a job, when transferring to a remote job and regularly as you forget. First of all, these are the tasks of the leader.

Access time regulation


Even when working in the office, not everyone came on time. On one of my projects, employees could come to work in the interval from 07:00 to 11:00. But, there were 2 limitations:

  1. The employee should have been present at all the meetings at which he is required, regardless of the time they started. Here, the person had many options: agree with everyone about reschedule the meeting, connect by phone, come to the meeting at the designated time.
  2. Together with the employee, we designated "time of guaranteed presence" = the time when the employee is 100% at work and available for contact. The time of guaranteed presence of employees was communicated to the entire project team and the Customer.

For remote employees, we need a clear regulation of all manifestations of the presence of an employee at work, at least this:

  1. recording and publishing guaranteed availability time;
  2. fixing the time interval during which you need to respond to the letter;
  3. fixing the time interval during which it is necessary to answer an unanswered phone call;
  4. fixing and regulation of liability and sanctions for failure to comply with the paragraphs above.

The listed requirements should be fixed in agreements with employees, regulatory documents of the company level and work regulations. It is incumbent on the manager to communicate the restrictions to employees, to coordinate, if necessary, control the implementation and incentive measures in case of deviations.

Mechanisms for accounting and compensation for violations and errors


To help employees comply with the working conditions described above, additional incentive elements will be required that will reduce the risks of employee irregularities in the performance of their duties. Now there are simple concepts of ‚Äúnot going to work,‚Äù which are stipulated in the Labor Code. And how to fix not going to work when a person, in principle, goes nowhere. This is why it is necessary to process legal documents and corporate regulations.

In any case, it‚Äôs better not to punish people for a violation, but not to help them. This requires clarification, prevention and control. Which, in turn, will require increased involvement and control by the leader.

Spyware Control Methods


Against the background of remote work, advertising of espionage tools and methodologies for monitoring employees intensified. Screenshots, pictures through a video camera, fixing all actions and other dead man switches. Some providers offer to record the entire working day of an employee, and then view it with the help of a separate employee.

These tools were used before the introduction of remote work. There are cases when managers monitored the work of employees through a video camera. True, I did not see any analytics about the effectiveness, and there couldn‚Äôt be any, since it rather amuses the ego of an individual person, but does not give an increase, but rather the opposite.

In my opinion, their use indicates the desire of the head to imitate the work, and not organize it. The work of the leader involves planning work based on the available resource of employees, understanding who and what is doing at the moment, and issuing corrective feedback. If the manager is not able to provide proper management, it is necessary to study and implement more effective management methods and tools.

In addition, the use of such tools does not increase the confidence of employees in the organization as a whole and can cause a reciprocal desire to work less. Plus, it can affect the attractiveness of the organization for people if the labor market swings in the opposite direction.

Hybrid work format


The hybrid format is available in two variations:

  1. Employees who by their role cannot work remotely work in the office, the rest work remotely.
  2. Everyone works remotely, and they come to the office only for meetings and work for which a presence is required.

Now a lot of hope is assigned to the second format. I don‚Äôt really believe in him. Since people will get used to working from home most of the time, and the road to the office will create an additional negative that will destroy the benefits of temporary presence in the office.

Using remote work as an incentive


Previously, remote work was often used as an incentive, for example, the ability to work remotely while maintaining high quality or increasing productivity. Now you can use remote work as an incentive of a different kind - reducing payment, provided that remote work is preserved. Benefits for the employee, in this case, will be: saving time on the road, saving on transportation and food, the ability to live anywhere.

Work in the office, with a hybrid format of work, can be considered as a negative incentive in the case of poor quality work. In this sense, a hybrid work format can greatly help, if only by the availability of the opportunity to return an employee to the office in case of unsatisfactory results.

Regular face-to-face communication


A competently arranged process of work and reporting allows you to receive information on possible deviations from reports in a timely manner. However, when working remotely, it is important that the supervisor personally communicate with the staff on work issues. I pay special attention to the fact that:

  • it is necessary to communicate with everyone, and not just with those with common non-working interests;
  • It is necessary to discuss work, not personal issues.

During these conversations, it is possible to identify and resolve emerging issues and engage in employee development.

For large teams, I usually use this schedule of meetings:

  • Daily general meetings, like SCRUM rally, with the whole team. During them, it is advisable to disassemble the general and typical difficulties that may be of interest to the whole team.
  • Separate meetings, throughout the day, with employees who have identified difficulties that do not require general discussion.
  • Once a week meetings with employees who, in principle, have no difficulties, to confirm that everything is really good and the development of employees.

Holding such meetings is an important component of the work of the leader. When working in the office, it was possible to replace meetings with one-time communication on abstract topics. Now this will not work and the lack of regular meetings will lead to serious problems. If, instead of holding regular meetings, the manager analyzes the current state of affairs on the basis of reporting, he urgently needs to expand his arsenal of professional competencies.

Housing issue


Previously, people were forced to move and live near their place of work. What made many leave their places of birth. Buying apartments in big cities was not a cheap pleasure, and everyone bought apartments of not large area, in which it is impossible to equip a full-fledged workplace. In the transition to remote work, this problem seriously manifested itself.

Further, while maintaining this trend, people will be able to move to small cities, where they will buy larger apartments in which they can equip a full-fledged workplace. And people from small cities will get a chance to work remotely and earn a large area apartment. The real estate market is already starting to respond to new conditions.
