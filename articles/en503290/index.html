Two inertial measurement units (

IMUs ) The Matrice 300 RTK is equipped with two inertial measurement units ( IMUs ). They are widely used in aviation, including UAVs. The main task is to determine the accelerations and angular positions of the drone.

In the event of a malfunction of the IMU, the drone will not take off, and in the air this threatens with a serious accident. The developers of the M300 RTK took care of this issue and applied block redundancy technology. In other words, in the event of a failure of the main unit within 0.2 seconds, the spare IMU is automatically activated. This will help maintain flight stability and safely land the drone device on the ground.



Two barometers

The barometer determines relative altitude based on atmospheric pressure. Duplication of the M300 RTK barometer allows the drone to switch to the
backup barometer in the event of a primary failure.

Two compasses A

compass, or magnetometer, provides information on the flight path for a UAV. Matrice 300 RTK is equipped with two of them, and in this case they are interchangeable. Their simultaneous work improves the quality of work, and in the event of failure of one of the devices, the second takes on the full function of flight control.

In the event of a failure of both compasses, the RTK navigation system takes over the functions of the compass, determining the cardinal points from the relative position of the navigation antennas.

Two Matrice 300 RTK batteries

equipped with two batteries with a capacity of 274 Wh each. In fact, they are also duplicates. If one of the batteries fails, the second will be able to provide power for a safe fit.



Collision avoidance


Matrice 300 RTK is highly protected against accidents and collisions. Six pairs of visual sensors work simultaneously in it to study the situation in all directions in real time. The range of operation of the lower and upper sensors, which are respectively directed up and down, is 0.6 - 30 meters. The remaining four look around, as well as forward and backward. Their working range is from 0.7 to 40 meters.



There are six infrared ToF sensors. They help determine the distance to the object by measuring the transit time of the IR signal. And infrared sensors provide distance sensing in low light conditions. Their working range is 0.1 - 8 meters, and the viewing angle is 30 ¬∞.

Auxiliary lighting

Auxiliary lights mounted above and below the drone have a 5-meter effective distance. They can automatically turn on in low light conditions for the functioning of the visual sensor system.



Two control panels Dual Remote Controller

More precisely, the dual control mode of the Matrice 300 RTK . Thus, two operators can control the flight at the same time. And in the event of a loss of communication between one remote control and a drone, the function automatically switches to the second pilot. This will allow not to stop production tasks. In addition, the function is useful for beginners paired with a more experienced operator.

DJI AirSense Technology

Allows the operator to keep abreast of the external situation in the sky. Notifies about changes in weather conditions (air temperature, wind speed), as well as the presence of dangerous objects for a drone: airplanes, helicopters and other drones within a radius of 20 kilometers. Information includes the exact location of the object, speed, course and altitude. In the event of a collision risk, the system additionally notifies the pilot through the application.



Emergency landing

M300 RTKcan make an emergency landing with one failed engine. Basic drone controls such as ascending, descending and horizontal movements will remain available to the pilot. Of course, the drone and payload can be damaged during an emergency landing, but the pilot can land somewhere far away from people or buildings to keep them safe.

RTK


Real Time Kinematic (RTK) , or ‚Äú real-time kinematics ‚Äù, or in Russian KVR, - this system can be called differently. In general, she is responsible for receiving information from the air. In other words, this is an improved system for improving the accuracy of data received through the Global Navigation Satellite System ( GNSS ) - GPS, GLONASS, GALILEO or COMPASS. RTK is online.

The system constantly transmits to the pilot‚Äôs control panel data such as latitude, longitude and altitude, external climatic factors and reports about objects that could cause damage to your device.
