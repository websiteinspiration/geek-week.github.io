Path of transformation



Faced with the task of distance learning nose to nose, we got a chance to consider blind spots and change the education system. It is completely obvious that the strengths of online schools are in their current subjects, tested methods, ways of presenting material and the ability to learn in one's own rhythm. The strengths of the traditional school are personal contact with the teacher, the ability to socialize and develop soft skills. We should not be surprised either by the growth in demand for additional education, or by the increased requirements for the classical school curriculum. The current crisis in the educational system, triggered by a pandemic, can and should lead to tectonic qualitative shifts. Any transformation is always painful.
