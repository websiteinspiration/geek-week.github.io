Where to look for vacancies


Vladimir Pranov : On Facebook, at the request of a ‚Äúgame dev‚Äù it‚Äôs easy to find many interesting groups where vacancies are laid out, people from the industry share news, and you can post your resume. There are also inGame Job and Talents in Games platforms where you can register as candidates, create resumes, respond, write cover letters and try to find a job.

What should be in the resume



This is an instruction from Values ‚Äã‚ÄãValue. More details here

Alina the Wise : The summary should be structured, short (no more than one page), not variegated: black text, white background. Marital status may be important for the employer, but you are not required to demonstrate it. It is better to name a file with a resume by its first and last name.

Oleg Dobroshtan : It is better to change the resume to the requirements of a particular vacancy, indicating the required skills and keywords so that it answers the questions of the employer. It is enough to indicate the three previous places of work, while irrelevant experience is better to discard. Too frequent changes of places are likely to create a negative impression about you. It is also necessary to reflect four to five points of responsibilities, two to three points of achievements and a real role with a specific project.

Mistakes in creating resumes and interviews


Vladimir Pranov :

  • Too long and irrelevant resume.
  • Summary with insufficient information about tasks, achievements and platforms.
  • Another mistake is not to see which project (genre, monetization) and in which company the applicant is going to work.
  • It is very important to correctly formulate the desired salary, not to cheapen, not to overestimate yourself.

Roman Vasiliev : we need to complete all the test tasks, as they will tell you in which direction to develop, and will give a little experience. And the deadlines in the test should not fail, because this indicates a future attitude towards work. Do not drink before the interview, do not suck up at the employer, do not try to get friends and do not swear obscenely.

What kind of education do you need?


Vladimir Pranov : The importance of education for joining a game development company depends on the position. For programmers, skills, experience and a portfolio of projects are much more important. For game designers, the knowledge and skills that can be gained are also essential.

In general, it is not necessary to have a higher education: there may be enough courses, which are more valued in game dev, because they provide the necessary skills in a clear and concise form.


Learning, however, is always necessary. This broadens the horizons and provides new information. It is important to know the theory and practice it. For example, to create and edit game mechanics, you need to have a good command of mathematics, and although it is taught in educational programs simply, this may motivate some of the students to study it in more detail.

Roman Vasiliev : Many leaders do not look at education as a crust, but as evidence of the determination and learning ability of the applicant, who spent time on serious studies.

Education provides not only knowledge, but also the contacts and connections necessary for employment. 75% of students in the ‚ÄúGame Project Management‚Äù program find work either in the learning process, or after graduation, having no experience in game development.

What to do if there is no experience


Mikhail Kuzmin : If the applicant did not have experience in gamedev, he will most likely get to the initial position, regardless of what he did before. In addition to front-end and back-end programmers: there are no restrictions for them. The better you understand your desired career path, the sooner you will be able to move along it, and it will come out for you in a year or three - the company will find out about this at the interview and look further at how suitable you are. For a successful start, you need a lot of pretense in a variety of genres, which is expressed in the fact that you know the market, you can talk about it, and the desire to work a lot, at first even a little mechanically. Primary professions: Q&A, technical support and sometimes everything that comes with the prefix junior, if you have already learned somewhere or have a relevant portfolio.

TinyBuild often asks game designers for a link to an open Steam profile (or other platform) that can tell a lot about the applicant and his understanding of genres.

English is important, although not required for starting positions. But you need to be able to correspond in basic terms.
Sometimes almost nothing is required from the resume: show motivation and readiness to work with hands chore.
Starting positions depend on the company, sometimes burning eyes are enough.

Even if the project is not successful, it is still better to have it in your portfolio. This will mean that the applicant encountered most of the problems in the development process and was able to complete the work.

Internships are in demand in IT and media, but not in game dev - this takes a lot of company resources.
In game dev, each employee either does point tasks or teaches. And the company has to pay both for one and the other, often without meeting thanks in return.

In many companies, for example, 1C Games and 101XP, the initial positions serve as internships.

Pitch Tip (Project Presentation)


Konstantin Sakhnov : A common mistake made by novice developers - they are trying to come up with a complex concept. It is better to do something simpler, although ambition is good.

Mikhail Kuzmin : There are examples of successful cases from teams freshly collected on enthusiasm. Who is a game designer and how is it different from a producer


Konstantin Sakhnov : Game designer - designer: a person who is able to think through game features, mechanics in his head and knows how to implement them later. The game designer writes technical assignments - detailed descriptions of the mechanic and features, and thanks to such descriptions, other employees can implement the idea. If the positions of the game designer and producer are separate, then the producer just ‚Äúlegitimizes‚Äù the project: gives a vision to the game and sets the motion vector of the entire team, and the game designer embodies his ideas - makes the mechanism spin and is responsible for making the game interesting.

How game mechanics come up


Konstantin Sakhnov : Everything that we come up with, we consciously or unconsciously copy from nature or from the works of other authors. You can use this technique openly and consistently.

For example, like this: see all the competitors of a future project, write out all their ideas, transform and bring something from other platforms or old games. Ideas can be drawn from real life or books, transferring actions to virtuality.

There is another way: copy something and remove some element.
The easiest option is to expand your consciousness and horizons and actively look around you. Most importantly, do not try to transfer some games entirely to other platforms.

If Game Dev is a Super Dream


Mikhail Kuzmin : Do not romanticize game dev. This is the same job as the rest. There are only two differences: the game needs to be made fun, otherwise it will not be sold, and the development cycle here is much shorter than for companies creating something for the business. However, gamedev brings pleasure to those working in it.
