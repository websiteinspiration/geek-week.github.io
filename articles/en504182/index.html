General information



Source: top3dshop.ru

Solutionix C500 - a compact device. The mobile scanner easily fits both on the desktop in the office or at home, and in any convenient place.

The device consists of a rotary table rotating in 3 axes and providing an overview of the scanned item from all sides, and two cameras with a resolution of 5 megapixels for color scanning.

Ease of control and high data processing speed provide high efficiency of the device.

Strengths of Solutionix C500:

Weaknesses of the device:
