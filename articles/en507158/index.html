For the last time, Apple co-founder opens a new category of devices, relies on hardware, software, and the interface worked out before on the first iPhones. A successful attempt: tablets of the new generation are sold in millions of copies, after a couple of years they are used everywhere. Competitors are trying to repeat success on the Android platform, but they do not succeed right away.

Of all the computer devices, the tablet is perhaps the closest to natural storage media without batteries: books, magazines, birch bark letters and Egyptian clay tablets . The concept of a computer tablet, in turn, did not originate in military laboratories and private research centers. And in science fiction.


Stanley Kubrick is now often referred to as the "inventor of the iPad" in the 2001 Space Odyssey movie. The film was shot in 1968, but please: space travelers use something very familiar, thin and lightweight devices with a screen, with the ability to play video. In the cinema there is generally a demand for visually pleasing technologies, without the burden of keyboards, disk drives, punch cards and cables. It was guessed very accurately. When did the real predecessor of the iPad come up with similar functionality? Not later than 1989:


The GridPad wearable was clearly released sooner than needed. Nevertheless, it implements the basic requirements for the tablet: a portable device, a touch screen, and the lack of a keyboard. In 1990, GridPad even got a built-in hard drive of 20 megabytes (and screen backlight). They say that they managed to sell as many as ten thousand pieces.


Fast forward three years and look at the first attempt to create a commercial device with a full graphical interface. The video on the Lazy Game Reviews channel shows something cosmic for that era: a tablet with a wireless pen, on Windows 3.x and with a 386 processor inside. Ideally! . The technology of modern tablets is being developed on slightly more compact devices: Palm and Apple Newton handheld computers, and later on PDAs running Windows Mobile. In any case, in the nineties, at a time when a typical computer was strictly stationary, all this was inaccessible to the masses. Due to the high price and the lack of really useful applications. Buying an ordinary laptop would be a blessing.


Microsoft made its second trip to the country of touch devices in 2001, with the release of a special version of Windows XP and the announcement of an entire program for the release of Tablet PCs . In the 2007 Sony VAIO UX series, very compact models appear. 600 grams, 4.5-inch screen, retractable keyboard, Intel Core Solo processor and full Windows XP. Notebook makers experimented massively with the Tablet PC, trying to find a promising market niche, but, among other shortcomings, all these devices were notable for their high price. They were sold sluggishly; devices like the Sony UX280 are now expensive rarities. The price problem was solved by a category of devices that were not related to tablets, netbooks.


In 2007, the outstanding Asus Eee PC 701 model was released: a cheap, compact laptop with a microscopic screen. One advantage: price, $ 190 per version with Linux. Later, normal Windows netbooks will appear, such as the Eee PC 900 model in the photo above. Price, depending on configuration, from 400 to 600 dollars. Before that, there were almost no laptops with such a price tag, and certainly there were no cheap compact devices weighing up to two kilograms. Netbooks are available, limited in capabilities due to Intel Atom processors, but they run on battery power for a long time (up to 10 hours with a large battery), and are quite suitable for basic network entertainment. Watch a movie, chat in newly appeared social networks. The Internet does not yet require the resources of an average supercomputer from the late nineties; web pages do not weigh 20 megabytes each. You can do with a netbook.


The first Apple iPad attacks netbooks. In his favorite style, Steve Jobs announces that netbooks "are not really capable of anything," and he, of course, is not quite right. It shows a device that in a number of tasks is much more convenient, and this is true. Ten-inch screen with a resolution of 1024x768. Single-core Apple A4 processor, similar to that used in the iPhone 4 of 2010 (but slightly higher frequency - 1 GHz). 256 megabytes of RAM and a data storage of 16-64 gigabytes. The price of the base model is $ 500, and it is the price that becomes the main topic of the presentation.


But it is not only an affordable price. Apple gives iPad owners their own iWork office suite for free. Announces software from other developers, imprisoned for a large screen tablet. For the first time in a cheap device, such an ‚Äúexpensive‚Äù technology is implemented as an IPS screen with wide viewing angles. For the first time, 10 hours of battery life are achieved without the help of a thick battery. Apple is not required to provide backward compatibility with PC software, as in Windows XP and Windows 7, the interface of the system and all programs is initially compatible with finger control, it does not require a stylus. Perhaps, but the iPad definitely corrected several serious problems in the predecessor devices. Apple showed how to make such a device truly compact, lightweight, convenient to use, and long lasting on battery power. Takeoff and gloom



Apple iPad has seriously influenced the personal device market. Netbooks disappeared from the horizon: the Lenovo S10 netbook purchased shortly before the iPad 2 was quickly sold - it was no longer needed. Android device vendors have seriously invested in the development of their own tablets. The whole Windows 8 desktop operating system tried to impose a ‚Äúfinger-oriented‚Äù interface, even where there was never a touch screen. This attempt to transfer everyone to the touchscreen did not last long. For Apple, 2014 was the most successful year in terms of iPad sales. After that, a slow decline in supply began, as can be seen in the general statistics above ( from here ).


How did it happen? Along with the release of the iPad began a craze for smartphones with a large diagonal. The smartphone was set by the Samsung Galaxy Note smartphone with a huge (at that time) 5.3-inch screen. The modern Galaxy Note 10+ has a diagonal of 6.8 inches. Compared to 10 inches of the original iPad, this is not such a big difference to justify the purchase of another device. The tablets of the beginning of zero proved to be excellent as devices for consuming content, in other words, for procrastination. Social networks, videos, games and more - all this is available on a smartphone without any problems. Why then buy another device?


As a result, the tablets returned to where they were in 2007: into the niche of specialized devices, for those who need a large touch screen. Cheap universal iPads are still being sold and updated, but since 2015 a model with a 13-inch display has been released, which is trying to compete more with full-fledged laptops. Both in price and in performance. iPad Pro 2020 is positioned as a laptop computer. This is a great tablet for artists, a suitable portable device for processing photos, with a keyboard - a good machine for working with documents. But it costs accordingly: up to $ 1800 in a top-end configuration with terabyte storage.

The initially minimalist iPad wasn‚Äôt just brought back the keyboard. The stylus reappeared, which Steve Jobs unsuccessfully tried to rid us of in 2007. By features, price and performance, the iPad Pro is just an alternative to a laptop. Unseen previously possible features are added to the software - mouse support, multi-window mode. A tablet is not always the best choice, including for me: I have old devices on Android and iOS, but no new ones. For a keyboard worker, a classic laptop is more comfortable. iPad is evolving and again trying to find its niche, once having been a universal device for everyone. But these bright times are in the past.


In my collection, the iPad 2 was in the middle between older devices and modern ones. On Windows Mobile and Symbian zero software stores do not work, network services are disabled.
