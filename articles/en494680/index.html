What is this guide about

Most likely, when you try to implement a full range of reasonable measures, you will encounter a wide range of difficulties - from internal resistance and misunderstanding of others to quite tangible physical limitations. This guide is designed so that you can overcome at least some of these difficulties and do what you should.
