ED Words




Our development, training on which is built on a curve of oblivion, is also the Ebbinghaus curve.

In short, for the most effective study of vocabulary, you just need to observe the correct time interval between repetitions of words.

Using this technique, 90% of the new vocabulary can be remembered in 3 days:

  • The first repetition is immediately after learning the word.
  • The second repetition is 15-20 minutes after the first.
  • The third repetition is 8-12 hours after the second.
  • The fourth repetition is 24-32 hours after the third.

So in just 4 contacts with the word, you can learn 90% of the vocabulary , even if you think that you have poor memory and no ability to learn the language.

The fifth repetition, 2 weeks after the fourth, increases the percentage of learned words to 97.5 .
