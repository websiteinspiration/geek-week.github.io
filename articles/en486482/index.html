How to connect and configure face recognition


Ivideon cloud face recognition works on cameras with our firmware, or on cameras connected via Ivideon Server. The service will work via the Internet and on any mobile browser, send email or push notifications of the arrival of visitors from the lists.

The resolution of the camera matrix must be selected depending on the distance at which it is planned to recognize faces. It is worth adhering to the dependence: 1 megapixel per 1 meter of distance to the object. The minimum sensitivity threshold is 0.1 lux. The less light there is on an object, the more a photosensitive camera will be required.

By default, faces are detected from the video stream in the Ivideon cloud. But the detection can be transferred to Ivideon Server - this significantly saves Internet traffic. To do this, cameras must work on the same network as the computer on which the latest version of Ivideon Server (Windows, Linux) is installed . The service will not contact the cloud every time you need to recognize the next person.

There is a way to refuse a computer. To do this, we created Ivideon Bridge - a device for connecting to the cloud up to 16 different cameras of any manufacturers.

The face recognition service is open for full free testing - legal entities are given 2 weeks of a test period. You can activate "Faces" yourself in your account or send a request to connect.
