Features of XYZprinting 3D Hand Scanner 2.0








Description


XYZprinting 3D Hand Scanner 2.0 is a simple device for creating full-color three-dimensional models of objects. A clear interface and reasonable price are the business cards of the model.



The portability of the device is confirmed by the weight of 0.24 kilograms and the length of 157 mm. The image shows the constructive structure of the camera lens. Behind the one-way mirror are an infrared camera and a laser projector.



The Taiwanese device is based on the Intel RealSense F200B camera, which represents a set of technological and software developments. Volume scanning is one of the areas where Intel RealSense features are used.



The combined work of two cameras allows you to process objects in the range from 5 * 5 * 5 centimeters to 1 * 1 * 2 meters. An 40-fps RGB camera with a resolution of 1920 x 1080 pixels processes color data. The infrared camera performs 30 frames / sec and is responsible for transmitting information about the size of the object.

The working process




When digitizing objects, XYZprinting 3D Hand Scanner 2.0 must be connected to the computer. When establishing a USB connection, the user selects a convenient scanning mode:

- a separate object;
- a person in full growth;
- face;
- the head.



The utility, like the switch button on the device, enables and disables the workflow, changes the scanning modes.



XYZprinting portable scanner processes information in a standard way. To get the finished model, the scanner is sent to the subject and slowly move it until the desired result is obtained. The laptop screen broadcasts the process, allows you to control the stages of scanning in real time.



The scanner works at a distance of 30-50 cm from the object, depending on the task and operating mode. Creating a copy of the human body in full growth occurs at the minimum speed of the device. When working with the head, the manufacturer recommends starting with the most convex part in the desired angle. Face scanning is controlled by a specially designed mask.



An important clarification: the movement around the object should be in the "clockwise" direction, the device is in the left hand in a horizontal position. Given that the majority of people are right-handed, work requires little effort before acquiring a skill.
