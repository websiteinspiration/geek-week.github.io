Light flow




The measurement of light flow is also carried out after preliminary heating. During this period of time, the luminous flux stabilizes. The TL-STREET 55 outdoor LED luminaire is made using efficient 5000K Osram Duris S5 LEDs. Efficiency is from 140 to 170 lumens per 1 watt at a crystal temperature of 25 degrees. In this sample, after heating, the light flux decreases by 5-6%, which corresponds to the value stated in the documentation.

Spectrum and color temperature




According to the test results, the color temperature is 5000K, the color rendering index is 71 CRI. Low color rendering significantly reduces the cost of the LED, so it is used only for street lighting.



Power supply driver




This model works at a voltage of 90 volts without reducing the level of luminous flux of the street lamp. Measurements were taken using a laboratory power supply.
