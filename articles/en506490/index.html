How to regularly express yourself in the media

How to stay on hearing if the project does not have bright information lines? How can a small company attract media attention?

Answers to these questions are provided by PR support - a promotion method that works great even in routine times. To whom it suits, how to place oneself in the top media for free and how PR support is organized in the agency - read the article.



PR support vs PR campaign: what is the difference


To begin with, we will analyze how the two ways of promotion differ from each other. Empirically, these differences have been distinguished for themselves, based on work with IT companies and FinTech projects.



So, the task of PR support is not to talk about a specific event or product. This is a constant methodical work to promote the brand - even when there are no obvious information lines.

In what cases does a company need PR support, and not just a PR campaign?


1) Long transaction cycle. In B2B segments, partner selection can last for months . While a potential client studies the market, calculates the budget and makes a decision, the company must remain visible and declare itself in the general information field. Examples are IT companies, consulting and marketing agencies, equipment manufacturing and supply enterprises, etc.

2) Maintaining contact with existing customers . People regularly use mobile communications, banking services, buy insurance to travel abroad. Given that changing the provider is quite easy, companies must constantly remind the client about themselves using cyclical touches - in the form of articles, new projects, surveys, etc.

3) Reputation of sales. This includes a high-risk business in a competitive environment - crypto-exchanges, asset management, startups, etc.



PR Tools


This section is illustrated by agency cases. We want to use our own example to show how a small company expresses itself in the media - without large budgets for promotion, a scandalous founder and a line in the Forbes Global 2000 ranking.

1) Internal news feeds


An important event in the life of the company is the creation of a new product, partnership, access to the foreign market, etc. - can be used for promotion. Despite the fact that the company is not called Apple, the news of a small media project seems to be not interesting.

You need to write about the event in any case - even if as a result the news will only be available on your website and in social networks. This is important for building a reputation among customers, investors and future employees who, if they do not read all the news of the company, but if necessary, view it.

You can try to offer a press release and the media. But before you do this, answer the questions:

  • Does the event you write about affect industry?
  • Is something unusual and outstanding?
  • Do you offer something useful for your target audience?
  • Is this really what readers need to know?

If you did not answer positively to any of these questions, there is little chance that the media will host this news for free . If you still have a positive answer, the benefit of your release should be clear to the media editor from the title and first sentence.

Example

In 2018, we decided to start working with a new segment - the mobile application market. In this regard, they wrote a press release stating that we are looking for startups that are developing products for smartphones, and promised the best of them $ 2500 for promotion in the agency.

Press releases about this appeared in the regional Belarusian ( Onliner.b y, marketing.b y, dev.by , bel.biz) and foreign media .



The goal was achieved - with the help of this news feed we announced ourselves in a relevant market and received the first project in this category.

4) Expert comments for the media


Journalists regularly engage experts to write analytical articles. There are even special resources where the media publish requests for comments and textures for articles. For example, in the Russian-speaking space, this is the PressFeed journalistic inquiry service . The most famous international counterpart is Help a Reporter .



Establish yourself as a specialist who is ready to respond quickly, competently and on business. For this:

  • Make contacts . Meet the journalist who writes about your field in advance. Start with social networks: add a journalist as a friend, write how you can be useful to him, follow new posts and comment on them.
  • Work on your professional brand m. Keep an expert column or blog, participate in conferences, speak out about resonant events in the industry.
  • If a journalist asked you for comment , respond as quickly as possible, especially if the topic is topical. Remember that, most likely, the comment is taken not only from you - therefore, consider the topic from different angles and try not to be very banal.

6) Communication with readers


After the publication of the material, work on it does not end. It is necessary to follow the comments of readers and respond to them in such a way as to maintain the reputation of the company. Here are some suggestions:

  • Answer promptly - preferably during the day. In social networks, the time frame is even narrower: according to statistics, 42% of users expect a response within 1 hour;
  • Personalize each answer - even in the case of identical comments like ‚Äúthank you‚Äù.
  • Answer questions, thank for praise and feedback (even negative). When working with negativity, remember that not only the author of the commentary, but all readers will see your answer. At the same time, trolls and spammers should not answer;
  • Always be polite and professional, do not give in to emotions and write on the case. Do not forget that you represent the brand.
