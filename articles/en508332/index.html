Who and where is spending money in the global space industry.



The space budget of the Earth can be divided into two parts - commercial activities and state budgets. Commerce is twice as large and almost all of it is focused on the maintenance of mass technologies - navigation and data transfer (mainly television).



There are three segments of the overall budget - the ground-based infrastructure of the space industry, space information services and state budgets for the implementation of space programs.







U.S. analytics company Bryce has published a report on the global space budget for 2018. They make analysis and reports for various organizations on the topic of space and defense, including for NASA and FAA (its infographics and data are used in the annual reports of these respected organizations). These organizations are fairly accurate. He made a translation of the infographic from the report - the global space budget, divided by various parameters (countries, areas of activity, etc.). I made a separate fragment with state budgets.







Such a visual representation of where the main efforts and means are directed leads me to the obvious conclusions:



- Space activity is the destiny of a few.The result of the efforts, resources and aspirations of several states of the world. There are six of them: USA, Europe, China, Russia, Japan and India. What coincides (excluding Japan) with the "centers of power" of the modern world. These countries use state budgets for space activities and securing their positions (dominance and expansion).
