F # community founder: “OOP and AF of the brain must die” // We are doomed # 7

A year and a half ago, I wrote an article about the F # developer community. It seemed strange to me that people who have so many opportunities for career development are throwing gigantic forces into the study of a strange language that is not really needed by anyone. 

But inside, I met the most friendly and lively community of developers from all with whom I talked. The boys made many logical reasons why they chose F #, but I still got the impression that they choose not with their minds, but with their hearts. Very unusual garbage for such a pragmatic industry.

A year and a half after the article, I invited Roman Melnikov to the podcast and discussed how things are now.
