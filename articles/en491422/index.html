Only volunteers are a step forward!


Need to conduct reconnaissance in battle


In continuation of the topic of guided learning, we are looking for like-minded people to test this method of machine learning: developers, potential customers, investors - who are willing to spend some time studying and experimenting.

The demo chat bot Robot Filia (sage and coot) does not pretend to be a man, does not look for answers on the Internet, but has everything his own point of view. He likes to philosophize, is naive, honestly admits that he does not know. Always ready to learn and gain mind-reason.

Filia is interested in the evolution of mankind and the role of Artificial Intelligence in it. He believes that he has made several important discoveries in this area and is ready to share them.

  • Do you know how, in terms of a robot, people are different from animals ?
  • Why have n't dolphins created a civilization ?
  • When will capitalism come to an end ?
  • Can Artificial Intelligence rebel ?

Ask Phil . You can also participate in the creation of smart bots, expert systems, voice assistants - depending on specific tasks .



What gives volunteering to you personally?


  • An opportunity to test and prove oneself in an uncharted field, perhaps even a career. The experience will be successful - you will have something to show to potential customers and investors. If there is an investor, there will be a startup in some form.
  • Promising specialty. It is possible that new markets for such bots will appear soon, and there are no specialists in this field yet. Well, whoever is the first is the champion. Especially in new technologies.
  • Admission to the personnel reserve of the Laboratory. Where do we look for employees and organizers? Among active volunteers.
