Captcha sucks. Let's just say it. There are many ways to pass verification, and each of them is bad.



In recent years, bots have become smarter every day, and protecting the site from them is becoming difficult. With enough time and resources, you can get around almost any captcha. There are sites where, as an alternative, captcha with a slider is used (“slide the slider for verification”). But why would anyone even use such a simple captcha with a slider?



The reasons are as follows:



  • Most bots do not run on JS, so you can stop them.
  • Slider is more user friendly
  • For users of mobile devices, “swipe” the slider - the usual action


So for people, the sliders are clear and simple.



Let's try to get around some captcha with a slider.
