Ladies and Gentlemen!

Of course, we assumed that our daily life could change dramatically - but so much so and immediately? To be honest, I don’t even know which hub to publish this to. In theory, in the hub “the future is here”, people talk about expected scientific miracles, and many-fold improvement in the lives of ordinary people.

But what if the future will not be so beautiful? And it will be the other way round, in the style of Orwell. We, being in the Krasnodar Territory, already here and now, have received signals about this very expected future. And you must publish this. So that the community knows about the "insane and inadequate power of Russian justice." Especially - regional justice. The bottom line is that the registered media, by a court decision, is obliged to pay compensation for non-pecuniary damage for someone else’s publication (never appearing on the media’s website itself), and also to publish a refutation for 9 months on the Yandex main page !!!

Well, ok - any, even the most irresponsible requirements can be brought to court. And the court should consider them. Right?

And the court is considering. Just wonderfully considering this case. The judge’s response to the lawsuit of the Prikubansky District Court of Krasnodar Rogachev is simply excluded from the case file. And the fact of authorship of the publication in Zen is personally attached to the editor-in-chief of the media “For Krasnodar” on the basis of “extremely weighty evidence”:

Is a red tick, as well as in the opinion of the judge of the “opposition-scandalized style” enough to equate an elephant and a giraffe?



And then, literally from the lawsuit, he makes a decision: to oblige the editor-in-chief of the media to remove the publication (how? The channel in Zen does not relate to the media “For Krasnodar”), and publish a refutation on the “yandex.ru domain”:



What should I do with this, excuse me, delirium - is completely incomprehensible. The decision of the judge of the Kuban district of Krasnodar on the suit of the vice-governor Anna Minkova was made. But neither the channel in Zen, nor even the yandex.ru domain does not belong to the media. Therefore, this decision (if it enters into force after the appeal) will simply be physically impossible. But not to execute - that means the chief editor is subject to criminal liability under the 325 Criminal Code of the Russian Federation. And this is an opportunity to be imprisoned in places not so remote.

And now to the question of why all this shameful nonsense may be needed. Indeed, any, even the most seemingly strange act must have reasons. Sane or not is another matter. But within the logic of an individual, they must be reasonable.

The essence of the reasons, in our opinion, is simple: September 13 will be election day. On which the current administration of the region wants to extend the powers of itself and its own for another 5 years, excuse the “team”. However, they do not shine with results, to put it mildly. The introduction of quarantine in the region in 3 hours (from the moment of the publication of the decree to its entry into force) definitely hit hard on the economy of the region. Huge lines for passes, in which people were forced to stand several times, too.

The current leadership of the region also essentially has no other sane results: the number of unfinished projects and the number of equity holders left without apartments for all 5 years only grew, the federal garbage reform was completely failed, thanks to which it is not clear at all, but from the words of the head of the region, now from the Krasnodar Territory the name probably won’t stick out for a long time ... I'm sorry, but if you rewrite it very intelligently, you get the “ass edge”. And yes - perhaps for the first time in the history of the USSR and Russia, the Krasnodar Territory under the leadership of these leaders managed to turn from a donor region (previously it was always believed that only the lands of the Krasnodar Territory would feed not only local, but also many other regions) turned into a subsidized one.

There is literally nothing to be proud of. And the consequence is logical from here: it means - you need to keep silent about everything. Who can bribe, intimidate, or shut up their mouths. And since we are dealing with a person who cannot be bribed or intimidated, you need to deprive his audience and voice. And also desirable - and freedom. Is it logical? Quite. Is it legal? The question is rhetorical.
