How to teach everyone and everyone at the same time?

The short answer is to enable students to learn on their own and make it inevitable.

Let us consider in more detail the pedagogical experiment conducted at Novosibirsk State University. For 6 years at NSU I taught a special course on software testing based on the principles of mixed and dual training. We started with a group of 8 people, and in the last year 52 people out of 100 students of the stream enrolled in the course, including those who already worked in software testers.


The article discloses the organization of the educational process, and the pedagogical and managerial issues arising from it.


From Foresight to HR

One of them was a project for training software testers for a corporate customer. Later, on the basis of such preparation, we put together a special course for Novosibirsk State University. For us, it was important not just to conduct some kind of course, but to make it so that it helps students use the knowledge and skills they have in real work in the IT industry.
