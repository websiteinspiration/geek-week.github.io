7 days to transfer to the remote Rostelecom

Hello!



Like many companies, in the spring we switched to a remote mode of operation, and now we continue to work this way. Of course, there are employees who sometimes need to appear in offices anyway, but those whose work responsibilities can be quite successfully performed from home do it from home.



In this post we will tell you what exactly helped to make the transition in a large composition and in a short time, share experience and useful resources. Maybe you will also come in handy if you decide to leave your employees in this mode of operation. As polls (and news) show, some companies have already come to this.



To complete the picture, let's start with the fact that in terms of Rostelecom employees today it is:



  • 7 federal districts of presence
  • 11 time zones
  • 65 branches
  • Over 150,000 people


Processes and solutions



In startups and mid-sized companies, negotiating a transition is a bit easier. Deploy a VPN, agree with everyone what kind of software / messenger you use to work, how you fix and set tasks and, in fact, start working.



In a corporation of such a size as Rostelecom, you almost always have to write a detailed plan of action first, in order to try to foresee everything and then not slip at some stage, because such a slip risks very, very substantial braking of everything that you have in mind.



Therefore, we prepared IT for the remote in 5 main steps.



  1. Basic IT preparation for the transition
  2. Study of the solution and staff readiness for a new mode of work
  3. Forecast of possible scenarios
  4. Forecast of possible scenarios of our teams
  5. Defining teamwork and communication tools


Of course, we already had VPN gateways, as well as employees working remotely with their help. But their number was not designed for that horde, which now also required full-fledged secure access to the corporate network.



Therefore, in all macro-regional branches of the company, we, together with colleagues from information security, conducted a thorough audit of existing VPN gateways. Then they decided to increase their capacity by 5 times, as well as add backup ones. Just in case.



We worked on the issue of mass access for employees. It all sounds pretty obvious, but it's important - how exactly do these processes work for you. Because if an employee from home needs to create an application for access and a VPN, but for this he just needs to do something from the office computer at some stage, this is at least strange. Yes, it is safe, secure and generally accepted. But you can change such processes without losing security.







We should also mention the work of call centers - we provided the guys with remote desktops with full-fledged transfer of voice traffic to home PCs.



Such a scheme of work helped us.







Educational resources



It is important to collect information that is associated with the transition to a new mode of operation, on a single resource. Otherwise, employees will constantly ask in the chat what to do, where to find this or that instruction, a link to the software, and the setup procedure. Or, in general, send each other in confluence, which does not work for half, because first you need to make applications for access. Instructions for which are also in confluence.



Therefore, a separate portal with detailed information, accessible from an external network, but some sections of which are opened only after login to the working account.



On such a site you can collect everything - information on how those who need or want to work in the office now How are things with business trips around the country and abroad. Basic information about maintaining health. Motivation and efficiency on a remote site. Links to the necessary tools and instructions for setting them up. And many many others.



The main thing here is to first launch the portal in a basic form with critical information at the start, and only then in the process to supplement it. Because in attempts to first collect a cool resource that will be as full as possible, convenient, buggy and beautiful, you can get into a situation when you start it in about six months. And then, if the team will have few perfectionists.
