Calibry: 3D Breakthrough Price Breakthrough


Calibry Handheld 3D Scanner. Source: Thor3D

Meet - Thor3D's new affordable Calibry Handheld 3D Scanner .

The Russian-German company Thor3D has been developing 3D scanning solutions for many years, Calibry is their recent product announced in Shanghai.

This is a hand-held 3D scanner that can be used to capture medium and large objects, that is, those whose sizes fall in the range from 20 cm to 10 m. With the same success, you can make a three-dimensional scan of both the toaster and the whole car.

The positional accuracy of the scanner is 0.1 mm, and the resolution is 0.3 mm, which is quite suitable for fairly large objects that will be scanned by this device. Scanning speed - up to 3 million dots per second.

Scanning speed is a very important indicator, as it determines how quickly scanning can be performed. Many other handheld scanners with lower throughput are inconvenient to use. To get enough resolution, you have to move the low-speed hand scanner very slowly, repeating movements from time to time to provide coverage. In addition, these devices are quite heavy.
