Vivaldi 3.1 for Android - Big in Small



Hello, Habr!

In the development of large projects there are advantages - all this scope, scope for creativity, a lot of opportunities and available resources give a feeling of grandeur and scale of the business to which you give all your time and energy. Meanwhile, more modest projects also have their charm - here you can hone your skills in achieving maximum efficiency with a minimum of available resources. Likewise, we are very impressed with the large sea vessels, but in ship models we spend, sometimes, much more time, looking at miniature details and marveling at the skill of the author of this amazingly complex work.

Today we release the next stable version of the Vivaldi browser for Android at number 3.1, and the analogy with ships and models was not accidental - in fact, we are trying to repeat the power and functionality that is present in the desktop version in the mobile version of the browser. Moreover, the work is further complicated by the fact that the reduced copy should not be just a model, but a full-fledged powerful application that performs the same tasks as a large browser. How much we manage to do this - let's see together.

Since the release of the previous version, you have helped us fix a lot of errors and shortcomings that prevented comfortable work. But in addition to the corrections and updates to the Chromium core, we tried to add new features that you requested. The most noticeable thing here, of course, is the ability to change themes for web pages to dark - this allows you to more conveniently browse websites in the evening, in low light conditions.



Improvements also affected the address bar. Now in the browser settings there is an item that allows you to enable search tips when typing. By default, the option is disabled to increase the privacy level of the browser - search hints mean that the server receives data about what you are looking for and what you are interested in, and this data is also added to the user profile for further advertising. Therefore, the user must decide whether he needs tips or not. Also, work was done to further optimize the use of prefixes for search resources installed in the browser.

As in the desktop version, in the Vivaldi mobile browser, we always pay great attention to tabs. True, the small screen size of mobile devices imposes certain limitations. Therefore, some of the functions of the tabs went to the tab manager (access to it is opened by clicking the button in the lower right corner of the browser window).

Here you can switch with one touch between open regular and private tabs, as well as view recently closed tabs and even tabs opened on another device with which this copy of the browser is synchronized. Also, if the user wants even more screen space, he can completely disable the tab bar.

Another innovation is the ability to open links from a page in the current window or in the background tab. Try to make a long tap on the link - in the context menu that opens, you will see many available options for working with the opened link.

Work has been done to improve the blocker. Now, when changing the protection level, the page will reload automatically, for added clarity, the data on the blocked elements will be updated dynamically as the page loads. But it must be recalled that the blocker is disabled by default, and only the user decides at what level blocking of unwanted content and advertising should be carried out. And, of course, surveillance.

In the new version, the work of such a popular function as a screenshot was improved. Let me remind you that you can take screenshots of both the visible area and the entire page. Access to this function is carried out through the main menu, located under the V button.

We have also worked to improve the stabilization of not only the operation of all functions, but also of the browser itself - there should be even less drops in the new version.

And a few words about Vivaldi synchronization. The forums still ask questions about how everything is arranged here, so it‚Äôs worth reminding that our own synchronization allows us not to use Google servers, but to store user data on our own Vivaldi servers located in Iceland.

The synchronized data is protected by end-to-end encryption with a unique user password and cannot be read even by Vivaldi employees who develop synchronization. And therefore, if you assign a master password, please try not to lose it. If you forget it, then restoring your synchronized data will not work in principle.

That's all, you can download the new version from the link below. A complete list of changes can be found in the English-language blog . And do not forget to tell us about all the problems and wishes - this will allow us to make the browser even better.
