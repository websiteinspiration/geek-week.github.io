Pay for training in coding: Russian bachelors can win a scholarship to study for a master's degree in Switzerland

Hello, Habr! This post is intended for those who want to become a master of science in computer science and software engineering, and not at a Russian university, but in Switzerland. This opportunity is offered by the Schaffhausen Institute of Technology (SIT). The curriculum of this institute is built in such a way that the acquired skills and knowledge will allow graduates to apply for CIO or CISO positions in large corporations, or to develop their IT project at the international level. Also today we will focus on the competition, the winners of which will be given the opportunity to study at SIT for free. Everyone who wants to learn more about the new master's program, studying in Switzerland under the guidance of world scientists and Nobel laureates and about scholarships provided for the winners of the competition, we invite you under cat.



In a previous post, we already talked about the Schaffhausen Institute of Technology. The university was founded to train new generation leaders at the intersection of IT, business and academic science. The university is run by both businessmen and the founding fathers of modern programming, including the creator of the Eiffel language Bertrand Meyer and technical director of MariaDB Corporation AB, the author of MySQL Server Ulf Michael "Monti" Widenius.

Who needs free training?


Given that European education is not affordable for everyone (although the cost of studying at SIT Master's program is comparable to the cost of education at Russian universities in a similar direction), the SIT team launched the international online programming contest SIT Star Contest, which will be held this summer. Students of Russian universities are well aware of this format for the selection of applicants "for budget places" in the form of olympiads.

In the case of SIT, the winners of the competition will be paid training in the amount of 100% or 20% of the cost of the entire course. The number of scholarships will depend on the general level of participants and is not limited in advance. Winners will be able to get an education in one of two programs:

  • Master of Science in Software Engineering and Digital Leadership (4 semesters). A double degree is awarded in conjunction with Carnegie Mellon University (CMU) in Pittsburgh, USA, and National University of Singapore (NUS) in Singapore.
  • Accelerated Passage: Master of Science in Software Engineering and Digital Leadership (3 semesters)

Stages of the SIT STAR Contest:


If you decide to participate in the competition, then it's time for you to stretch your brains and fingers today, because the preparatory stage of the competition has already begun!

  • Until June 7, 2020 Training: First you will be given access to a training tour so that you familiarize yourself with the test environment. You can train at any time from June 1 to June 7, 2020. This step is optional, but we strongly recommend that you do not skip it. The results of this tour will not affect the final score.
  • June 17, 2020 SIT STAR Competition: The main tour will be held on June 17. Participants from different countries will have 4 hours to go through it.
  • June-July 2020 Interviews and Winners Announcement: Top participants with the highest scores will be invited for interviews with professors at the Schaffhausen Institute of Technology.

The competition is held on the Codeforces platform. You will be offered 8-12 tasks of various difficulty levels in the field of algorithmic programming. You can get more detailed information, study the rules of the competition and start training here .
