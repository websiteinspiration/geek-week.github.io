Crawc.net - creating and styling your media chats

I want to talk about my project. This is a chat , or rather a media chat platform - this means that here you can create and create your own one-room chat with support for media content in messages and insert it into your website through an iframe. The project has just recently left the beta stage.



I have been told many times that I am writing a bicycle, but this has never happened in the RuNet. There are a bunch of chat rooms, but they don’t provide even half the functionality of Kravcha , but they look incredibly bad. I spent a huge amount of time on design and usability, so that communication was as comfortable as possible.











Let's go over a bit of functionality



This is a semi-anonymous chat that does not require verification of identity by phone or email, as well as other personal information - for registration it is enough to specify the desired username and password, although if you wish you can also indicate your age, city of residence and write briefly about yourself - this information, together with your photo album will be reflected in your profile on the site. The idea of ​​Kravcha is to combine the room’s channel and its chat in one visibility area, in the same area you can view the same profiles, so viewing profiles or reading the channel does not distract you from the conversation. On the channel you can publish thematic news, chat rules, media content and all that’s enough for fantasy, because the chat room’s posts support HTML (a small visual editor has been added for users who are far from web development).Channel messages can be highlighted in color, or rather even two - text color and background color, you can also make more detailed settings by clicking Options and editing the styles of your room manually; there you can change the name of the room and set a password to protect it.



Now about the chat itself



Text messages can be formatted in messages: in this way you can highlight text with color, indicate font size, insert a quote, code, etc. (More detailed documentation on message formatting can be found here or on the channel in the main chat room ). In addition, in the messages, as mentioned above, you can post media content: you can pull it up either by reference or by downloading from disk. Voice messaging supported. There are two kinds of emoticons: a small collection from fontello font and a huge library of Japanese emoticons from jemoticons.com







Medicines and Tabs



As well as in the social. networks, there are private messages. In one window, you can open several tabs with chats and several personal conversations (in addition, their number in theory is not limited). Also, the tab will display the number of new unread messages. Switching between tabs, your url hash will change: by writing this hash in the address bar, you can get into a specific chat or certain personal correspondence (for example, https://crawc.net/ru/#anime , https: // crawc. net / ru / # emo , https://crawc.net/ru/#2X2FAN- using the same links to demonstrate the possibilities of custom styles). In personal correspondence, as well as in the chat room, you can view the profile of the interlocutor - he will be to the right of the dialogue. GIFs are supported in user avatars, their photo albums and in the messages themselves (correspondence with gifs looks pretty cool).







Additionally



There is an interactive search in chat rooms. Several languages ​​are supported (currently only Russian and English). In addition to the avatar, the user can also have a personal icon from the unicode set that will be displayed in the chat user list. This is done so that users can join in groups. Also on the site you can create quests in the form of text mini-games and draw ascii- and pixel-art.

Another feature: you can view multiple images at once on a scale, and in each tab they can be different, which is quite convenient.
