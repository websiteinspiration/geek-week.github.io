The role of soft skills and hard skills at the start


When choosing a future role, it is always better to focus on interests and soft skills - these are your character traits, data from nature or in the process of education. Hard skills are those skills that are acquired through experience. You will be told the necessary information in the courses and you will improve this knowledge in the process. In this area you will have to study regularly.



The key qualities of a good specialist for any of the areas are: the ability to perceive information and analyze it, using the search as independently as possible, take responsibility, constantly learn and learn a lot, and, of course, be agile. In a good IT team, everyone is equal, everyone has their own areas of responsibility and tasks, everyone works for a common result, which is the main value.

To begin, create an honest list of your strengths and weaknesses in character.

Example for Project Manager



Yes, soft skills are important. To a greater extent, these are qualities given to us by nature. For example, do you like meeting and communicating with colleagues or vice versa, do you like to work independently? You can correlate your habits and adapt them to circumstances, by understanding and working out the target qualities. 

Suppose you are an introvert, but you want to become a project. Such a case can also be quite successful. To do this, it is important to arrange processes in such a way that within the team and with all project participants all the necessary information is available in writing: using documentation, checklists, reports, notifications. In addition, consciously choose and understand where all the same communication is inevitable.

In order to reflect and better understand ourselves, to think about our weaknesses and strengths, I highly recommend passing 2 tests:MBTI and FIRO-B .

Although we are all unique, there are certain beliefs and patterns that somehow affect us. Therefore, the first step to becoming better is to accept yourself and work on yourself.
