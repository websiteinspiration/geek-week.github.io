For those who buy links

The article is not about “links working or not working”, but for those who buy them or want to start doing it on their own. In it, I will give some recommendations, based on my experience with reference exchanges. We will take three representatives for consideration.



General rule - if you do not want a free link on this site - do not buy.



1. trastik.com





I want to write in more detail about this site, because I learned about it not so long ago (previously it was simply not needed in additional exchanges). I will sign without water, the main pros, cons and personal recommendations.



pros



1. Good price for links. If on GGL, Miralinks and PR-SAPE the price tag for a “normal” link starts at 500 rubles, here you can get a similar donor for 150-200 rubles. Personally, I bought for 1000 p. link to a domain with 1kk unique traffic per month. Very decent price. Check the cost on other services - be very surprised :).



2. The quality of sites. In general, it is “okay”. These are not “virgin” portals with 0 outgoing links. The general ratio is within the normal range. This means that in 80% of cases, incoming more than outgoing. And the ratio (incoming divided by outgoing) in 60% of cases from 0 to 0.6. In simple words - sites are not heavily spammed by auto exchanges and cheap links for the sake of quick promotion. This means less likely to get backlink from the site under the filter in the future.



Explanation of the incoming / outgoing links: if the site has more outgoing than incoming links, this may mean the following:



a) The site is not in the best shape (traffic, stagnation, viruses, errors, etc.) are falling and the webmaster is trying to maximize profits selling links at a lower price.



b) The site was originally created for the sale of links. This means that the webmaster received a number of "puzomerki" (X, traffic, PR, the number of pages, etc.) and further plans to deal only with its monetization, occasionally keeping the resource afloat.



c) They plan to sell the site. Similarly - before the sale, the owner tries to squeeze the maximum profit. It is worth noting that this is rarely done and only unscrupulous and, honestly, stupid webmasters. A jump in the reference mass before the sale may void the transaction.



d) The site consists of an automated link exchange such as SAPE, SeoPult, etc. So most of the links from it are garbage.



3. Sane feedback. Personally, I have no complaints about feedback. Received answers in a few minutes. Some colleagues were unhappy with the long answers. But I didn’t notice anything like that.



4. Ideal for gray and black themes. Most sites on the site are hacked. That is, the webmaster does not know that the site is selling a link on the site. We will not discuss the moral aspect of this. Get a lot of perpetual links for little money - a great strategy for gambling, adult, farm and other similar topics. In addition, you can change the domain or link page for free. Banned the site - quickly change everything link to the new domain and wait for indexation. In fact, you have a dynamic link mass that you can apply to different sites.



5. A few more features:



a) Articles and links are the same. If you do not upload your article, then a random cheap article from the etxt exchange will be used, they are taken only for the sake of uniqueness. If you need a quality article, then order yourself and use.



b) There are foreign sites - maybe for the same "gray" and "black" that is suitable.



c) The guarantee for accommodation is one year.



Minuses



1. The domain address is not shown. I think this is a key disadvantage of the exchange. It’s clear who will show the broken site? I always recommend watching the site through the eyes before buying links. No "puzomerki" will not show the whole picture, if not climb the site yourself. Of course, the parameters that the system provides us are not enough for normal analysis. This problem is solved only when the amount of replenishment on the account reaches 100 000 rubles, then the domains are not hidden.



2. Few sites. With a high probability, you will not find the exact hit in your subject. Count on near-thematic sites. Bad for large sites where the link plays a significant role. Mix well with other exchanges, skimming the cream at a good price tag. But in general - you should not hope for a perfect hit.



3. Interface - a purely personal matter. In my opinion, here it is worse than on GGL, but better than on miralinks.



4. A good article is required. Otherwise, get a random article just for the sake of uniqueness.



Perhaps there are still disadvantages, for example, related to the analysis and placement of links in the standard interface, but I usually use the minimum settings inside the service.



General conclusion on the exchange: the tool certainly deserves attention. First of all, because of the pleasant price tags.



For white topics: when purchasing links, be sure to check out trastik.com. Check the exact or close hit in a niche and buy a couple of penny links from good donors (if any). Lead a bookmark on the principle:



1. Accurate getting into the subject;



2. Near-themed sites;



3. News sites.



Remove the most “cream”, and then check manually through other exchanges.



For the "black theme": I would buy a link only here. Again, because of the price and the ability to conveniently change the domain. It can be considered as an alternative to PBN.



2. Gogetlinks.net



One of the most famous exchanges. Everyone who heard about the links heard about the GGL.



pros



1. The most complete donor information for manual sampling. But the most important thing is that the domain is visible, you can upload and upload to the check trust and check there.



2. 90% responsive webmasters. After posting the links, I asked to correct the article in about 70% of cases. Curved layout, different font, lack of images - these are all standard problems with the purchase. As a rule, everything was fixed. If a person did not answer, a dispute opened and usually everything was decided in my favor. After that, the site immediately went to the black list. With inadequate webmasters, it makes no sense to work.



3. User-friendly interface (this is subjective). If this is your first time on a site, it may be a little shocking, but then very convenient. Viewing an article with a link directly on the site, searching for a variety of parameters, etc.



4. Many sites. Here you can find almost any subject (with the exception of the forbidden: porn, casino, etc.).



“Gogetlinks does not involve sites that violate copyrights, which contradict the laws of Russia, as well as the norms of public morality (porn, hacking sites, etc.), adult sites and dating sites. (from)".



Minuses



1. Often post terrible articles. Sometimes it’s absolutely not what I would like to see, even for free. It is decided, as I wrote above in 90% of cases.



2. The price of the link is from 500 rubles. There are cheaper, but usually it's rubbish. If we buy on GGL, it will not be a budget, but it will not be as expensive as on Miralinks.



3. Many overpriced links. It is very important to check each donor manually. Often webmasters mimic a normal site, selling for 1000r. full trash. In fact, this is a problem for all link exchanges.



4. In my opinion, there is redundant and misleading information. For example, the choice of PS promotion. I personally do not understand this point. What is the principle of filtering links with this function? Some link is more useful to Google, and some Yandex? Well, debatable. I do not recommend using it. As well as the "Trust". In fact, any “Trust”, be it GGL, CheckTrust, miralinks, etc. - This is some kind of self-derived indicator. It is formed from other abstract indicators, simply put, this is the "average temperature in the hospital." You can take into account if the trust is from 0 to 4. This means that it’s completely rubbish: no traffic, links ’auto-exchange, spammed, etc. But then, when choosing, focus on specific values: traffic, accessibility, the presence of a filter, car exchanges, appearance, etc.



5. Replenishment by bank transfer. From 15000 rub. with a commission of 9% - this is not suitable for everyone. Often customers (especially in the regions) were shocked by such prices.



The general conclusion on the exchange: convenient, moderately garbage, optimal for the average budget. Suitable for promoting service sites, online stores, white-related info portals. Not suitable for black and gray themes.

3. Miralinks



No less popular system. Expensive, high quality.



pros



1. Qualitative difference from competitors - a huge number of high-quality donors, very strict selection when placing the site on the exchange. You can place multiple links. When purchasing, I usually write an article of 5000 characters and put 2 or 3 links inside. For example, 2 anchor ones: “buy Lada largus” and “Largus prices”, and the other at the very end of the article, “based on sitename.ru”.



2. By analogy with GGL (this is one company), there are 90% adequate webmasters and support.



3. The most natural links. As close as possible to a natural link, which means it is most effective.



4. Like on GGL, acceptable filters (if you understand the interface)



5. There is a possibility of link strengthening. Amplification due to live transitions. Personally, I did not see the effect. Better do it yourself.



Minuses



1. Price. Very expensive. Article + link ~ 2000r. Only for high budgets. If the budget is medium or low - better GGL.



2. Awful interface (this is subjective). Not a specialist - without a manual, she won’t understand anything. If you have not visited the site for a long time, you have to sort it out again. Unlike the same GGL.



3. The same strange "quality indicators" as GGL.



4. Replenishment by bank transfer. From 12000r., 12% commission - banking and 12% - system commission. In short, the minimum recharge amount is 15500. Well, if you replenish by 500 000 rub., You will receive an additional VIP status, and a discount of 25000 rub. Total, you have to pay 610000r. :).



5. It will be difficult for beginners. There is too many different indicators: the quality of the article, the donor, the number of links in the article, price, etc. It is necessary not only to choose a donor, but also to prepare the correct article based on the semantic core. To get the maximum effect, everything should work in a complex, and for this you need some experience.



General conclusion: only for large budgets. With a proper understanding of the principle of purchasing links, you can build the highest quality link thanks to expert articles. Not recommended for beginners.



In conclusion, I will write a step-by-step option of procurement using a bunch of these three exchanges (white topics):



Budget 20 000 rub. and less. I consider such a budget, because campaigns / webmasters with a budget of 50,000+, etc. themselves understand where and what links they should buy.



1. We go to trastik.com, remove all the “cream” in the form of cheap and high-quality donors. You will find 2-5 pieces;



2. We go to the GGL, we study donors, we purchase according to all the rules to the most thematic sites. If there are a lot of them, we buy there to the maximum. If there are few exact hits on the subject, go to Miralinks;



3. Go to Miralinks, see if there are any “tasty” donors at an affordable price. We write a good expert article on our own. We place on a thematic platform.



4. We strengthen (we buy links to a custom-made article) on the budget residues a thematic article with miralinks cheap links from a trust. Just don't overdo it.



If you promote gray or black themes - definitely use trastik.com.



Dynamic link mass will save you money when blocking sites. No need to spend money on GGL and even more so on Miralinks.
