Bounce rate



One of the key parameters is the failure rate. The search robot takes into account how many people left the site by looking at just one page. For the system, this means that the resource is irrelevant, poor-quality, or inconvenient. His rating is automatically reduced.



One of the reasons for the high failure rate is slow page loading. A hard-to-open site undermines self-confidence. People conclude that here they do not really care about them and there is no need to waste their time.
