Dimension and fact tables in DATA VAULT


The most difficult to understand information:

  • Measurement tables are built on the information of hubs and their satellites;
  • Fact tables are built on the information of links and their satellites.

And this is obvious after reading an article about the basics of DATA VAULT . Hubs store unique keys of business objects, their satellites of the state of attributes of business objects tied to time, satellites tied to links supporting transactions store the numerical characteristics of these transactions.

On this theory, in principle, ends.

But, nevertheless, in my opinion, it is necessary to note a couple of concepts that can be found in articles on the DATA VAULT methodology:

  • Raw Data Marts - windows of raw data;
  • Information Marts - Information Storefronts.
