Unification of Communications


This year, Microsoft Teams and Zoom are running the Unified Communications Hall. Equipment manufacturers certify their equipment for them. Software makers strive to achieve Zen compatibility with them so that users can call their users and invite them to their meetings. Logitech drives among peripheral equipment manufacturers.

New negotiation solutions are built primarily on Linux, most of them on Android. This opens up opportunities for running different applications and services on the same equipment.

The space of most stands is divided into zones according to the size of the meeting rooms that you want to build at your place. If you need a solid conference room, they offer one solution, if a small meeting room for three is another. At the same time, it is difficult for the uninitiated to catch the difference between the stands of software and hardware manufacturers. They all advertise kits consisting of each other's products. Could unite and save a lot by buying 4-5 stands , as it is now fashionable, according to ‚Äúlife situations‚Äù.

This year Pexip built a huge stand and showed a new product - the Adaptive Composition software service, when regardless of the capabilities of your camera, the picture is scaled and cropped by artificial intelligence. He is trying to provide comfort and convenience, as in a normal meeting, when the surfaces of all tables are on the same level, plus / minus heads are the same size, and people who stop talking do not suddenly disappear from sight. All this is relevant for up to 12 participants, then you still have to swap active with inactive ones, otherwise everything does not fit on the screen.

Logitech booth

They say that Logitech gained advantages when booking exhibition space at the expense of Lifesize, which was owned for some time. According to the rules of rebuking, the owner company can rewrite the points accumulated by the subsidiary. This is a rather complicated highly competitive process at ISE, and Lifesize has a lot of experience in participating. The Logitech booth promises to outshine everyone in Barcelona next year.

The situation with booking seats on the ISE is generally quite tense. Not all comers fit in the Unified Communications room. One and a half points matter, just like in an exam. When some companies refused to participate because of the coronavirus, there was a stir and an attempt to redistribute the vacant space. The organizers were forced to forbid everyone to move, and on vacant lots they organized food and leisure.

Poly booth

Poly, which Plantronics + Polycom, brought the "classic" G7500 system: a hardware codec, a camera of choice EagleEye IV or EagleEye Cube USB, Poly IP microphone, Bluetooth remote control and a set of cables. Positioned as a kit for small and medium-sized conference rooms starting at $ 5000 with an EagleEye Cube camera.

For larger lounges with EagleEye IV, it costs about $ 7500. For a serious project, you can take a second camera with a stand, then the EagleEye Director system will start at $ 10,000.

Poly G7500 with EagleEye Director

G7500 supports third-party cloud communication services such as Zoom, Webex, MS Teams and so on. More information about all the components and features can be found on the Russian-language website Poly .
