Is it possible to increase site revenue by speeding it up

The year 2015 has long passed and, remembering "Back to the Future", I want to see flying cars on the streets. But not only do we still ride four wheels on the ground, so also sites do not ‚Äúfly‚Äù. And this makes serious losses to their owners.









Yes, in the field of Internet marketing the topic of website acceleration is now very relevant. Too much depends on a good page loading speed. But now we will highlight only the monetary side of this problem.



Revenue level depends on site speed.



The first thing you will hear from any well-known specialist in this field is the conversion, and hence sales, which means income, depends on the speed of loading the site. The famous words of Benjamin Franklin: ‚ÄúRemember that time is money‚Äù fits here perfectly. Because the longer your site opens, the more potential customers you lose - they do not wait and leave. Leave forever.



If your website loads longer than 5-8 seconds, it's time to contact specialists. But even with a relatively good situation, when the page is rendered within 3 seconds, part of the audience is lost, which leads to a decrease in conversion.



Is it just speed



For a better understanding, it is worth understanding the components of this entire process. There are three main time elements:



  • Server response - time until the first byte is loaded.
  • Page rendering.
  • Full page load.


The main influence on the conversion is provided by the server response speed and the full load time. The first value should not exceed 200 ms (0.2 sec.). Why is that? Below are the experiments of the giants of modern Internet marketing Google, Amazon and Facebook.



The score does not even go for seconds - in their tenths



The first was Amazon. Already back in 2006, an American company conducted a study, the result of which led to the strengthening of the rule ‚ÄúMore speed - higher conversion‚Äù. It turned out that every 100 ms (0.1 sec.) Inhibition of page loading reduces sales by 1%. And this is a serious result. Calculating losses is not difficult.



Further, in 2010, Facebook conducted an experiment with the addition of a delay before issuing pages. Just 0.5 seconds reduced page views by as much as 3%. Then the company of the young Zuckerberg decided to conduct a reverse experiment. A 0.6 second speed increase boosted page views by 8%. It turns out that the response speed of the site affects the time spent by the user on the page, as well as the number of pages viewed.



As you already know, Google did not stand aside. In the same 2010, she checked the impact of site loading speed on its indexing in search engines, that is, on the issuance of a website based on search results in the same Google or Yandex. Fast sites get more visitors than slow ones. And here is the result of the experiment and led to the appearance of the recommendation described above - the server response should not exceed 200 milliseconds.



There have been many similar studies. Similar experiments were carried out by various online stores, car sites and IT companies. And their results only confirmed the main rule - ‚Äúmore speed - higher conversion‚Äù.



Thus, we can safely say that your income depends on the speed of the site. And in general, the importance and benefits of website acceleration is obvious to the entire Internet business. Improving this indicator is worth the investment.
