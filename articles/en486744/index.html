Here you have to choose the position that you want to maintain and which the corrector will remember for you. The sensitivity of the accelerometer is from 1 degree . This is a small step to optimally tune your tracker.

After the detuning, the collection of statistics begins, which will show you how much time for the period you spent with the ‚Äúcorrect‚Äù back position. You can watch this in real time.
