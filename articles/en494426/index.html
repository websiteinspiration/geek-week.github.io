So far, everyone is at home. 100 quarantine gadgets



On the Internet, there are already a million selections and tips about udalenka and self-isolation. Madrobots has our secret. Gadgets help our team to stay in good shape and not lose "at a distance" efficiency. A lot of gadgets. We share our impressions on how to upgrade your “home office”, not get bogged down in constant snacks and relieve stress wisely.

We keep track of current information.




Let's start with the most urgent. The LaMetric Time smart desk clock is a 5 percent watch. Otherwise, it is a multifunctional LED display with stereo speakers. You can display any indicators from the application catalog on the screen: exchange rates, notifications of new letters, news feed, weather. There was no suitable program - write your own.

From the most recent - in the LaMetric collection, an application has appeared that tracks statistics on coronavirus in real time. The data source is the corresponding section in Bing .



We have a Lametric watch in this mode in the office, in a prominent place: as a reminder to the most desperate, why you should refrain from traveling and self-isolate.

Relieve stress


Too much news? Take a break from the vibrant LaMetric display with a couple of puzzles. According to MacArthur’s widely cited study on the Internet, puzzles help delay dementia and Alzheimer's disease; but this study seems to be a network myth. But what exactly puzzles will prevent - boredom, stress and anxiety.

Guardian Dragon




Unidragon "Protecting Dragon" is not a puzzle, but a work of art, the result of the painstaking work of an illustrator and designer. Each detail is made in the form of a thematically suitable figure: dragon, sword, shield. Together they gather in one big dragon. The puzzle is made of dense birch plywood, with a total of 193 pieces. You will collect the dragon in just 2–4 hours, and you can repeat this over and over again until the quarantine ends.

If mythological creatures are not yours, in the same style there is a lion, a fox, a fish and an owl.

Wooden motorcycle




And the hearts of a fiery motor! Ugears - wooden mechanical puzzles with which it is easy to forget about everything. Stylish models move and live their own lives. So, a brutal motorcycle rides 3 meters. No batteries: the mechanism is driven by a rubber engine of the original design. The elastic stretches, and the released energy starts the motorcycle forward.



The model has an emphasis, but the motorcycle is well balanced - it will stand on a shelf without it. Assembled from 189 parts in a couple of hours. It pleases forever, or until it gets into a tough road collision.

Own bookstore




What is better to do in four walls than to assemble four more walls, but small? Rather, two. Designer DIY House " Bookstore " - a roombox, a room layout in miniature. In this case, this is the corner model of a bookstore. Furnishings, walls and even working lighting are assembled from elements and materials from a set. Say, to make a tiny book, you need to cut out and fold the pages with illustrations, and the flower in a small vase is folded with hands from paper.



There are detailed instructions for assembly. You can add a roombox in 24 hours, but practice shows that it will take more time. Nowhere to rush! Now is the perfect time to put together your little second-hand dream corner. It looks amazing with backlight, but it is also beautiful in the sunlight.

Keep fit


There are people who do not forget about daily exercise and take a break for exercise when working at the computer. You feel jealousy for those. Especially when you discover that for the fifth hour in a row you sit hunched over a laptop in a burst of inspiration. And around - suspiciously many empty bowls with crumbs.

Gadgets take on some of the important tasks: they remind the owner that they need to protect their health, they track various metrics. Yes, and regular charging with them becomes more technologically advanced and from that - much more fun.

Smart scales. Nowhere without them




Everyone at Madrobots has Picooc smart scales at home. And not just stand, but regularly bring benefits. They determine more than 10 indicators (weight, muscles, bone mass, fluid, protein, etc.) in percent and kilograms. The basis of their work is the principle of bio-impedance analysis: passing an imperceptible impulse through the feet, the scales determine the composition of the body by the resistance of the tissues.

In the application, you will see not only the results of the last weighing, but also the entire history of observations. Having set a goal (to lose weight, pump up or, just suppose, to stay in the same great shape) - you will receive recommendations from Picooc on how best to act.



All Picooc scales, regardless of models, have the same functionality and accuracy. A good start will be the most basic model of the Picooc Mini. The differences between the scales - only in the way you connect to your smartphone (Bluetooth, Wi-Fi) and the size of the platform. Large scales will be more convenient for people with large foot sizes.

Clever skipping rope. In order not to lose count




Not the scales got up, sighed, got off - and picked up the smart rope. In Tangram Smart Rope Rookie integrated magnetic sensors that said jumping. The SmartRope application stores data on the number of jumps spent in time - and counts calories burned due to training.

Look at the rope and you won’t think that this is a gadget. Even there is no treasured ON button. Rookie starts automatically while driving. The batteries are enough for 9 months of work. You can only think about your own charging, and not about charging another gadget.

Massager-muscle stimulator. When there is no strength for jumping




To strengthen the success of a workout, relieve stress after a long work, or recover faster will help the Bluetens Duo Sport massager-stimulator . Miostimulation (electrical muscle stimulation) is a common procedure in sports medicine and body shaping. Home devices also exist on the market for quite some time.

The French brand Bluetens does not promise embossed cubes from just sitting on a sofa (like the famous Chinese "butterflies"). Duo Sport is not a substitute for physical exercise, but an addition to them or a way to quickly return to payloads. The device is equipped with two independent wireless units and a set of electrodes. The electrodes are easily attached to the skin due to the sticky surface. They are also removed without problems and leave no residue.

Modules can be used with almost any muscle in the body. The application will tell you everything. Among the zones - to choose from: back, stomach, hips, knees, wrists, even the feet. 4 modes: pain relief, tone increase, disease recovery / prevention and relaxation. Choose a zone and mode, turn on one of the proposed programs - and go!



We promise a detailed review of Bluetens gadgets in the near future. In short - the feeling that someone is wrinkling your shoulders vigorously or that you are pumping hard is very realistic.

We equip the workplace. Light, music ... a plant?


If you read the popular collections of "What to do at home", it seems that the main problem during quarantine is total boredom. For many who have long been familiar with udalenka and freelance, nothing has changed. Or, conversely, the work only increased while the business is trying to adapt to a dramatically changing reality. It's time to put a couple of new gadgets in the work area, so as not to slow down.

Charger lamp




If you prefer a wireless charging method for your gadgets, the Rombica LED L1 table lamp will come in handy. Futuristic design, touch buttons, height and tilt adjustment.

There is also a setting of modes. There are three options to choose from: warm (3000 K), neutral (4000 K) and cold (5000 K) light. The lamp has a built-in timer for auto power off after 40 minutes. It turns out a 3-in-1 gadget - a table lamp for work, Qi-charging and a night lamp.

Personal sound space




In the office there are times when you can isolate yourself from what is happening only with the help of headphones. Experienced "remote workers" are less likely to encounter this problem. Although during the quarantine period, while everyone is at home, an office life hack can come in handy for them. A fun and high-profile family is good, but not for projects.

The Bose QuietComfort 35 wireless headphones pleasantly surprise you with an active noise canceling system and good noise isolation - due to the material and shape. Ear pads - made of artificial leather with a fringing. The case is lightweight and durable, made using glass-filled nylon.

The frequency range of the model: 20–20,000 Hz. Equipped with a dual noise canceling microphone to receive and make calls and use the voice assistant. The battery lasts for 20 hours. There is an option to connect via a mini-jack.

Although using headphones like earplugs is a real crime. They must be enjoyed.

Green corner of the desktop




Have you ever wondered how plants decorate the production process? Firstly, it is beautiful. Smart Garden iGarden LED will help you diversify your diet with fresh herbs, without risky outings. The gadget will grow strawberries, lettuce, cherry tomatoes - or, more quickly, basil and other herbs. Everything on the desktop!

Since iGarden LED is hydroponic, it works with an inert substrate vermiculite. No dirt and problems that ordinary garden land causes. Two separate pots are placed in a water tank. The water level is monitored, the gadget will promptly remind you that it is time to add water. A built-in LED lamp on the bar will provide the plants with the right amount of light. When the plants reach her, the lamp can be raised.

In addition to edible greens, iGarden LED can also plant ornamental plants. The gadget will decorate your home office. With the "green corner" you will not be so yearning for spring walks.
