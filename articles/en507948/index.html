Bot creation



Now that you have installed all the necessary components, we can begin to create the bot itself.



Everything is simple here. We go to the developer portal and click on the button with the inscription "New Application" - it is located in the upper right corner.



In the window that opens, enter the name of the bot, after which, click on the button that says "Create" .







On this page we can change the name of the bot, upload an icon for it, fill out a description.



Now our task is to bring the bot to life. To do this, go to the tab "Bot" .







We click on the button with the inscription "Add Bot" and bring the bot to life.



Congratulations! You have created an account for your bot. Now he has a tag, token, nickname and icon.
