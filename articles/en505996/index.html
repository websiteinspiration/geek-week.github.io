Alfa Battle - Online Application Programming Championship

Hello!

Today we are launching Alfa Battle , an online application programming championship for Java developers across the country. The championship will be held online, and it will also be possible to compete for the best title live. We did not begin to add any general tasks on the topic, spherical cases, etc. to the program - instead, there will be real business tasks of backend development from us and from the partners of the event (Beeline and X5 Retail Group). You can participate from any city in the Russian Federation, the main thing is desire and Internet access. The prizes will include money (250,000 / 150,000 / 100,000 rubles for the first, second and third places, respectively), a job invitation for the authors of the best solutions (to the office or to a remote place from your city) and a Joker ticket for the most creative solution.
