There was a complete technical documentation for the microscope. Two ledgers with instructions for use, a description of how and what works there. A complete set of electrical circuits. What struck me - most of the electronic units have their own inverter power supply with feedback in at least two parameters to maintain the stability of the operating modes. Of the consumables, the microscope requires only electricity, water, and sometimes a replacement for the thermionic cathode. Differs in indestructibility and maintainability.
