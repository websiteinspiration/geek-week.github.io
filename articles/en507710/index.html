What is the peculiarity of our programs?



Participation of IT-companies in training



Training programs are developed with the participation of IT companies to provide really useful knowledge. Courses are taught by current programmers and scientists. Our teachers: Vitaly Bragilevsky , Dmitry Itsykson , Alexander Kulikov , Eugene Linsky ,   Denis Moskvin , Alexander Khrabrov , Alexey Shpilman . 



Individual approach



Students study compulsory disciplines that provide the necessary basic knowledge, and independently choose special courses in areas of interest to them. These special courses are formed into individual educational trajectories for each student.



Practical classes are held in small groups.



Feedback



Each semester, students participate in anonymous surveys in which they express their views on the courses taken. Feedback is taken into account when choosing teachers and developing programs.



During the semester, you can contact the curators who will help solve organizational issues or choose projects and special courses for study.



Project activities



Students of all programs work on semester research projects under the guidance of teachers or employees of partner companies. So they gain development experience in conditions as close as possible to real ones. The tasks that they solve within the framework of the project work are of scientific or practical value: for example, undergraduates of Machine Learning and Data Analysis worked on a plug-in to improve support for the natural language in IntelliJ IDEA . See examples of projects of Computer Science Center students or students of the St. Petersburg HSE: here , here and here .

‚ÄúApplied Mathematics and Computer Science‚Äù at HSE - St. Petersburg



The program is aimed at training specialists in the field of software development, programming languages, data analysis, machine learning. There is a lot of programming here: in four courses, a student tries a dozen or two technologies and languages, undergoes a code review from experienced developers (not always the first attempt), works on practical or research problems from partner companies, delves into some topic and ultimately defends the diploma. In the summer, students can intern in Russian offices of IT companies or go on an internship to another country. 



The ‚ÄúApplied Mathematics and Computer Science‚Äù has a very busy schedule and a lot of homework that you need to pass regularly during the module, so learning is not easy. But as a result, graduates of the program go to graduate schools at leading universities in the world or find work in IT both in Russia and abroad.

Machine Learning and Data Analysis at the Higher School of Economics - St. Petersburg



The program is for bachelor's graduates who studied both in core (programming, physics and mathematics) and non-core (economics, sociology, etc.) specialties, and who wish to learn from scratch or deepen knowledge in the field of machine learning and data analysis. CS  



Computer Science Center is a joint initiative of Computer Science Club, JetBrains and Yandex Data Analysis School.



Program. The center offers two- or three-year full-time evening courses in St. Petersburg and Novosibirsk, so that talented students and university graduates develop in Computer Science , Data Science or Software Engineering . The program consists of basic courses in each direction, elective courses and practice or research work. Examples of practical projects of our students .



Teachers. Scientists, employees of JetBrains, Yandex, graduates of the center. To get acquainted with the program and teachers, see the courses,published on YouTube .



Selection. Education is free, but there are entrance tests. Recruitment takes place once a year, usually in the spring, and consists of four stages: submitting a questionnaire, testing, an exam in mathematics and programming, and an interview. Announcements are published on the site and in the social networks of the center.



Students of the center. The Computer Science Center awaits young specialists, graduate students, students of secondary and advanced courses, as well as everyone who is passionate about computer science or programming, wants to develop in these areas and loves to study. That's what the graduates say about the center .
