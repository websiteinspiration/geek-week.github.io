I decided to turn to the topic of the future thanks to the knowledge of the intellectual and humanist Anatoly Alexandrovich Wasserman, whose predictions came true with high accuracy. Let us see how our Russian view of the coming decades and centuries differs from the appetites and desires of the Western world.
