Reaktive binary compatibility: how we provide it
Any library should observe binary compatibility whenever possible. If different versions of the library in the dependencies are incompatible, then the result will be crashes in runtime. We may encounter such a problem, for example, when adding Reaktive support to MVICore.

Understanding eSIM (+ interview with an expert)
Let's talk about eSIM (the full name of embeddedSIM - that is, built-in SIM) - SIM cards soldered into the gadget (as opposed to the usual removable SIM cards). Let’s see how they are better than conventional SIM cards and why large mobile operators oppose the introduction of new technology.
