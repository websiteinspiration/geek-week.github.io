A simplified approach to calculating the date of availability of custom-made production

Gantt chart method and suitable for small companies. Including with the stocks of the remains of work in progress.


The challenge is to get away from:


  • average term;
  • term with a large margin;
  • appeals to production with a request to calculate the period in the head (or do it as the second stage after machine calculation).

Without a production plan!


Difficulties


  • Production at sites can go sequentially and in parallel;
  • Depending on the project, the site may or may not be involved;
  • Production may include long projects, the production of which is carried out at intervals and does not fully load the site, despite the generation of a large "line";
  • Work in progress may be used;
  • There may be a different delivery time for the necessary components for different sections;
