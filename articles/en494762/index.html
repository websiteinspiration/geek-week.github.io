50 applications for the effective organization of remote work

Millions of people are now adjusting to work at home due to the forced self-isolation associated with COVID-19. They must work in completely new conditions, so it can be very difficult to rebuild and maximize their performance outside the office.

But there are a number of software products that can solve the problem. In this post, we will look at 50 tools and applications that will help you significantly streamline your workflow, and ensure that you can complete tasks in your home office (either at the kitchen table or in bed ... anywhere).

Take a look at this list - there are sure to be some useful tools and applications that you might not have enough.

Project Management / Task Tracking


1. Trello


Trello is a tool that displays your projects and tasks on special boards. This allows you to always be in the know of who is working on what, what is the current status of tasks and much more. Trello will help optimize work with linear task lists, as well as with large projects and large teams.



2. Asana


Asana helps you create, assign and manage tasks. It allows you to put each task in its own context and in the context of the project with which it is associated. Thanks to this, you can discuss the task in writing with a team member without losing context. This helps maintain a constructive conversation. You can also upload or export files from your computer, Google Drive, Dropbox, and other cloud storage.



3. Assembla


Assembla is a project management system for remote teams and freelancers. It allows you to centrally, within a single platform, manage teams, code, integration, reports and security processes.



4. Basecamp


Basecamp is one of the best tools for organizing the work of remote teams. It is used for project management, planning, meetings, for responsibility control, documentation and full monitoring of processes on the basis of a single platform.



5. Podio


Podio is a great option for organizing tasks and doing work. It provides the transparency and control of individual responsibility necessary for effective teamwork, and allows people to organize and track tasks in one easy-to-use control panel.



6. Taskworld


Taskworld allows you to manage your projects without unnecessary troubles, tracks tasks and facilitates collaboration in several projects.



7. SmartSheet


SmartSheet can be used to track and manage various types of work for large and small groups, from startups to corporations.



8. Monday


Monday offers a new way to manage your projects and workflow. It helps teams plan tasks together and complete projects on time. Leading your team to this platform is as easy as sending an email. Your employees will immediately get used to the tool as soon as they begin to use it.



9. Wrike


Wrike is the ideal project management tool for large teams. It implements such functions as Gantt charts, workload distribution for resource optimization, customizable dashboards, structuring by folders, projects and automatic task assignment depending on its status.



10. MeisterTask


MeisterTask is an interactive task management tool for teams. You can use MeisterTask to organize and manage projects in a beautifully designed, customizable environment that adapts to the individual needs of the team. Kanban-style project boards allow teams to streamline and automate workflows by doing more work together.



11. nTaskmanager


From creating checklists to managing projects, working with project teams, scheduling meetings, sharing files, and more, nTask lets you do everything out of the box.



12. Jira


Jira is designed so that every member of your software development team can plan, track your workflow, and deliver quality releases.



Work and technical process

14. Airtable

15. Paymo


Paymo is a workflow management platform that integrates task management, scheduling, time tracking and billing for timely delivery of updates and software products.



16. Scoro


Scoro provides all the tools your team needs to fully manage your business and work: project management, workflow planning and tracking, whiteboards and task lists, a contact and CRM database, preparation and billing, advanced reports, real-time dashboards and much more.



17. Kissflow


Kissflow provides complete process management, all in one intelligent and easily integrated platform where any team member can create an automated process and project board, manage the workflow and collaborate on work issues with colleagues.
