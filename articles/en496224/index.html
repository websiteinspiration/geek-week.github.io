First Slurm Evening School on Kubernetes Webinar: What, Where, When, and Why?

Yesterday, we held the first introductory webinar of the Slerm Evening School .


A total of 5,015 people signed up for the course. The first webinar was attended by over 3,800 people. Thanks to everyone who was with us. It was great - especially the atmosphere in working chat.


At the introductory webinar we raised the following questions:


  • Why should developers and administrators learn Kubernetes.
  • What tasks does Kubernetes solve and at what cost.
  • How is the evening school.
  • What is included in free theory and paid practice.

Webinar entry and key points below:



( 12:39 ) What is Slurm? This is a training center that has existed for the second year. We launched the first Kubernetes courses in Russian in Russia. And more than 1000 engineers have already trained. We not only conduct online and offline events, we also conduct corporate events - we train company departments. We are members of the Linux Foundation and CNCF (Cloud Native Computing Foundation). And we are the only Kubernetes Training Provider in Russia.
