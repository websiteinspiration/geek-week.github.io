Design


We take the device out of the box and take a closer look. SNOM D785 is available in two colors: black and white. The white version looks especially good in company offices, with the design of rooms made in bright colors, for example, in medical institutions.



Most modern IP phones are similar to each other and differ only in small details. Snom D785 is not like that. In order not to occupy the useful part of the display, the BLF keys are placed on a separate screen in the lower right side of the case. The solution is not the most common among most other manufacturers, due to the increase in the cost of the device, and, in our opinion, looks interesting.

The plastic of the case is of high quality, pleasant to the touch, metallized navigation buttons emphasize the individuality of the design, pressing them at the same time remains understandable. In general, the keyboard leaves only a pleasant impression on itself - all the keys are pressed clearly and softly, without failing anywhere, like on some budget phones.

Also, a very successful solution we see the location of the indicator MWI in the upper right side of the case. The indicator is successfully entered into the design, not too standing out when turned off, and clearly draws attention to itself when turned on, due to its location and size.



On the right side of the case, under the screen, there is a USB port. The location is very convenient, you do not have to look for anything behind the screen or on the back of the case, everything is at hand. This connector is used to connect a USB headset, flash drive, DECT dongle A230, Wi-Fi module A210, as well as the expansion panel D7. This model also has an integrated Bluetooth module on board, which allows you to connect your desired Bluetooth headset.

The phone stand provides 2 tilt angles of 46 and 28 degrees, which will allow you to position the device conveniently for the user and get rid of unnecessary glare on the screen of the device. Also on the back of the case are cutouts for mounting the device on the wall - you do not have to additionally purchase an adapter to place the phone on the wall.

Behind the stand are two gigabit Ethernet connectors, a microlift / EHS connector, a power adapter and ports for connecting a headset and handset - together with a side USB port, a complete set. Ethernet ports with a bandwidth of 1 gigabit will come in handy if your employees work with a large amount of data and transmit them to the network. It is not always convenient to connect the cables to all these ports after installing the stand, and we recommend doing this before installing it, for which there is a rectangular cutout under the connectors, it simplifies the process of connecting the cables as a whole.

Functionality and operation


Let's get back to our device and its operation. Let's take a closer look at the additional screen and the BLF keys located to the right of it. Some of the keys are already configured for the accounts we have registered, and the bottom four keys allow us to put together a conference, make a smart call transfer, put the phone into silent mode and view the list of dialed numbers. Let's consider these functions in more detail:



Conference. In standby mode, this key allows you to collect a 3-way conference by dialing the numbers of the desired subscribers or selecting their contacts in the phone book. In this case, the call of all participants is carried out simultaneously, which is very convenient and saves you from unnecessary actions. Also, this key allows you to turn the current call into a conference. In the process of communication in the conference itself, this key puts the entire conference on hold.

Smart transfer. To work with this key, you must specify the number of the subscriber to which the functionality embedded in the button will be tied to. After linking, you can call this subscriber if you are in standby mode, forward the call that came to the phone, or transfer the call if the conversation has already begun. This function is often used to transfer the current call to your mobile number if you need to leave the workplace.

Quiet. Sometimes in office conditions there are situations when the ringtone of the phone interferes, for example, an important meeting takes place, but at the same time, you can‚Äôt miss calls. At such moments, you can turn on the ‚ÄúQuiet‚Äù mode and the phone will continue to receive calls and display them on the screen, but it will stop ringing. You can also use this key to mute the sound of an already received phone, but still an unanswered call.

Dialed numbers . Another multi-function key, the use of which is very simple: clicking on it shows the history of all outgoing calls. The last number in the history is preliminarily prepared for further dialing. Repeated pressing makes a call to this number.

In general, the functionality of each of the keys listed above is not unique and is present in competitors' devices, however, for many of them you will have to perform several manipulations in the phone menu to get the desired result, while when we turn on the device everything is at hand. The multifunctionality of the keys is also important: depending on the situation, you can use them in one way or another.

The phone‚Äôs BLF keys can be configured very simply not only by the system administrator, but also by the user of the device. The algorithm is very simple: to start the configuration, you need to hold down the desired key for a couple of seconds and the main screen of the phone will display its settings menu.



Using the navigation keys, select the type, going to the corresponding submenu, indicate the number and label that will be displayed on the additional screen.





Exit the menu. This completes the key setup, in just a couple of simple steps.

We pick up the phone and pay attention to another unusual detail: the phone does not have the usual end-of-life mechanical tongue.
