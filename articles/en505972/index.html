Model description



Source: youtube.com

Shining 3D Scanner Einscan SE is a compact device for creating a digital model of objects. The model is designed for a novice user - for home use, in education and other fields. An important advantage of the device is absolute safety for vision, as the laser beam is replaced by a beam of structured light.


Source: top3dshop.ru

2 cameras (1.3 megapixels each) and a projector are involved in the workflow. The user can select the appropriate scanning mode:

Automatic - designed to create models of small objects of simple geometry weighing up to 5 kg and up to 250 mm in size on the longest side. Matte bright objects do not require additional preparation. Dark, transparent and shiny need to be treated with a special matting spray.

In this mode, you only need to place the item on the turntable. The rest of the work: rotation and shooting of the object, the device performs independently.


Source: youtube.com

Manual scan mode is used to work with larger and heavier objects of complex shape. The scanner is mounted on a tripod, the subject - on the platform, which is rotated manually.

Einscan SE uses EinScan-S proprietary software, which has the ability to combine scans automatically or manually. Depending on the scanning task, the developer offers 2 variants of the finished model. If the user plans to print the object, he selects the completed finish model. For further work, editors save an empty image.


Source: youtube.com

Getting Started




Before first use, you need to assemble the device, the process will take less than 10 minutes. A swivel platform is mounted on one side of the stand, and a scanner holder on the other. Using cables, the scanner is connected to the platform, the computer and connected to the network.


Source: youtube.com

Then begin the calibration of the device. The process takes place in a semi-automatic mode. The calibration plate supplied is placed and mounted on the turntable. An online guide guides the user. If the scanning tasks include obtaining a texture, they additionally adjust the white balance using a white paper sheet.
