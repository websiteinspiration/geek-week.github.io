Which visa is right for me?


There are three main types of visas that can suit most of those who come here:

  • H-1B Visa is a standard US work visa for a local company.
  • O-1 visa - a visa for talented professionals and business representatives.
  • L-1 visas - if you work or own a company outside the United States, you can move to work in a new office in this country or open it yourself.
