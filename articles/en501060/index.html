JetBrains .NET Days Online, May 13-14

At JetBrains, we organize webinars every month (in English) and love to invite speakers from the community. Our site is open to any exciting topic, regardless of the mention of JetBrains products, but, of course, it is nice when they are used in presentations.

In the spring of 2019, we opened Call for Speakers for those who want to conduct a webinar and received many interesting applications on .NET-topics. Even after selection there were so many of them that were enough for a full day of viewing. We did not want to refuse part of the webinars or stretch them for half a year, so we decided to hold a separate online event. So it turned out our first .NET Day Online ( here you can see the records of last year's presentations). The feedback on the event was positive, so we decided to repeat the event.

This year, more than 40 applications arrived, and with a scratch we narrowed the sample to 12 speakers, and extended the JetBrains .NET Days Online program to two days. I wanted to offer as many different topics as possible, and in the end we got a hodgepodge of speeches about C #, F #, refactoring, safe programming, web development, AWS, and even blog creation.

We will be glad if any of the topics interests you and is useful. For broadcasts, we use GoToWebinar, so, unfortunately, the number of places is limited, so do not forget to register . After registration, we will send a single link that will work both days.

There is no such time zone in the world that is equally convenient for everyone. Therefore, we will record all sessions and then send them to the participants as soon as we edit the video. And of course, during the live broadcast, speakers can and should ask questions - albeit in English.

Do not miss JetBrains .NET Days Online! The event will be supported by the .NET Foundation and Twilio.
