How cash desk delays analysis helps save customer service time

A project that makes it possible to ‚Äúspeed up lines‚Äù at the box office in stores is primarily socially significant. In December 2019, the # ITX5 development team was able to find and patent a solution that reduces service time at the checkout, optimizes technical operations and affects such important company indicators as NPS and turnover. As a result of the successful results of its research, X5 Retail Group received a patent for an automatic system for analyzing delays at checkout counters. The invention allows to know in advance and prevent 98% of malfunctions and failures in cash services, and, accordingly, save hundreds of millions of rubles on optimizing IT processes in network stores.


How the system is implemented


‚ÄúWhen creating a system, we first of all thought about how to make IT even more efficient, understandable and transparent for business, as well as how to derive additional benefits from our IT systems, ‚Äù Evgeny Roldugin, Head of X5 IT Systems Department.

At the first stage, with the help of end-to-end monitoring of success analytics, we plunge into the ‚Äútechnical guts‚Äù of systems, pulling out data that is not used in normal monitoring and analytics. Based on them, we can build various systems, provide for accidents or some kind of technical malfunction. In the future, we give the business fresh useful analytics that cannot be obtained in a standard format. Thus, we see the time of interaction between different blocks of one service, record deviations and show the work of a real business process in life.

Then comes the stage of automating the work of techies, that is, automating the API - the actions that the support service performs: restarts, working with the database, changing configurations.

The system for analyzing delays in cash transactions includes three modules:

  • POS-terminals - software and hardware systems at cashier jobs;
  • central server for data processing and analysis;
  • an integration bus connecting the server and the terminals.



When a cashier breaks checks, POS-terminals collect information on payments made and canceled - in particular, the following data:

  • check opening time;
  • the number of items in the check;
  • time spent on each position on the check, as well as on the check as a whole;
  • waiting time for the next check;
  • type of payment (cash or bank transfer);
  • payment time;
  • details of unpaid check items



This information from the POS-terminals automatically arrives at the central server using special software - the integration bus. There, the system processes and analyzes them as follows:

  • determines the markers of the beginning and end of operations, and then, using these markers, calculates the execution time of operations;
  • reveals logical sequences and relationships between technical and manual operations;
  • monitors changes in incoming cash flow when paying for goods at POS-terminals;
  • determines whether errors occurred while working with the terminal or unplanned exits from business processes;
  • monitors external factors.

‚ÄúObtaining data from cash desks is the most interesting information, since the cash desk is the closest point to our customer. Accordingly, it contains the maximum amount of information about the purchase, the operation of services and the equipment itself. The collected data is converted into mathematical models, according to which the data is visualized in the form of reports. This is a kind of algorithm: we collect data, process, render, ‚Äùsays Evgeny Roldugin.

After analysis, the system determines the delays and calculates the economic indicator - how these delays affect the profit of the store.

What is useful delay analysis system


First of all, the system allows you to reduce the time that the buyer spends at the checkout - which means to reduce queues and reduce the duration of stops at the checkout. It seems that we are talking about second delays, which can be neglected. In fact, if you look at the scale of 16,658 stores of the X5 chains, the savings are serious: for 4 billion checks, which are printed in trading floors in a year, every second turns into 98 years of savings. Cash desk optimization also positively affects NPS and turnover. Using this system, we also compare the work efficiency of cashiers in Moscow, regions and between specific stores.



The system of analysis of delays in cash transactions predicts peak loads and prepares for them in advance, recommending cashiers how best to build the operational process. For example, the program will tell you when it is better to scan a regular customer‚Äôs card or how to organize work with a universal transport module (UTM) for buying alcohol without crashes and reboots.



‚ÄúWe can identify problematic barcodes, the speed and efficiency of scanning, the speed of cashiers and determine the impact of technical processes at the cash registers on the business itself.



‚ÄúIn order to ensure and maintain the continuity of the business process, we need to collect various kinds of metrics from our IT systems. With the help of our invention, we increase our effectiveness in terms of eliminating and preventing technical problems, we help finances by calculating analytics, security and risks.
