NVMe over Fibre Channel


PS In this article, we will not compare Fiber Channel with other standards. In Russia, there are a lot of companies that use exactly FC solutions, which will be very difficult to refuse from in the coming years.



For those new to NVMe, this is an optimized parallel protocol designed to maximize performance when working with low latency flash memory.

For example, NVMe supports 64K (65536) queues with a depth of 65536 each, unlike SATA, with only one queue and a depth of only 32 teams. This advantage is the main reason why the new standard has high expectations. About 80% of IT managers who are familiar with NVMe technology said they expect it to eventually replace traditional flash memory with SAS or SATA2 connectivity.
