From the moderator



In general, such posts are not a Habr format, it is better to write in a detailed and interesting way. But this post cannot but be left. Whatever happens around the coronavirus, doctors, nurses, nurses, nurses and orderlies remain outside theories of conspiracy, hypotheses and games, and do their job - all over the world, in any conditions, in and without personal protective equipment. Just like they always do it. Ambulances rush along the roads, hospitals work at the limit, professionals die - but do not leave their seats in such a hellish time.



Today I would like to wish that after difficult “covert” months we still have respect for the work of the doctor, that there will be no division into nurses and cleaners, that there are always payments, and the working conditions of doctors are honestly improved in all regions of our country. Health, strength and a quick vacation to you, dear medical workers! May grief pass you by!



And of course - you give advanced automation of medicine, telemedicine, robots to help doctors and cool user-friendly interfaces for doctors and patients. This is just in our power.
