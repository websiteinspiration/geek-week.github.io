The Leadership card says: if I take a leadership or leadership role on a project or in a team, I‚Äôm ready to sacrifice any subsequent card.

Not everyone is ready to be a leader, a leader, or even stand out from the team as a key person, but it happens that a person is quite inconspicuous, but diligent and it‚Äôs not always clear where he wants to develop. In discussions on this card, this is exactly what you can find out.

Speaking on the basis of IT, the key role is not always the team leader, although this slightly contradicts the name of the card. Maybe a person wants to develop as a technical team leader? Or maybe he wanted to try himself as an assistant manager?
In addition to discussing what role a person strives for, you can talk about what qualities a true leader should have. If the player himself wants to be a leader, we can discuss how he can develop in this direction and how he can develop the qualities that he described. Call


In the description of the Challenge card, it is said that if there is an opportunity to avoid routine tasks and to do more that allows you to constantly be in good shape and develop, I‚Äôm ready to sacrifice any card.

A rather interesting map that allows you to find out how interesting a person is for complex tasks, how much he is ready to put up with routine tasks, how much a person currently lacks a challenge and tasks that allow breaking his head.

In addition to the complexity of the tasks and the challenge itself, it is possible to touch on the topic of the content of the current tasks of a person, how much they motivate him and encourage him to further development. But maybe a person sat up doing one and the same thing every day and he wants to develop in a different direction?

From how much for the playerimportant complex tasks, you can go on to the topic of development, where a person would like to develop. And if he calmly relates to routine tasks, now you will know that motivating a person with other things (for example, a bonus or the ability to leave early at work on some days or something else depending on the motivational series), you can delegate to him part of the routine work. However, be careful, this also cannot be overdone: throwing all the routine work on someone, there is a risk that the Challenge will quietly rise in the motivational line and the person will simply burn out or even quit.

Lifestyle


So, the Lifestyle card . It is written on the card, if I have the opportunity to flexibly customize my work schedule and work from a convenient place for me, I am ready to sacrifice any card.



Many IT companies allow you to work not only remotely, but also flexibly configure working hours, but this is not always important for people. I have met many people for whom this is not a plus at all. The reasons for this are different: for example, a person simply does not want to work remotely, and not even because he does not know how to do this, but because he wants to be closer to his colleagues or communicate on professional topics during breaks. In addition, not everyone will be able to work remotely: maybe someone needs periodic help and the availability of opportunities for a more experienced colleague to sit nearby and suggest.

Also there are such people for whom going to the office is a big minus. And in order to understand what type a player belongs to , you need to talk about this card and find out what a person means by ‚Äúflexible work‚Äù. Perhaps he lacks something and in order to be happier he just needs to be given the opportunity to work from home a couple of times a week?

If for a player, on the contrary, fixed hours of work from the office are important, then the card can be reviewed and understood how important it is for a person and how much he is ready to put up with a floating schedule if necessary.

Communication


Communication card says that if I have the opportunity to spend part of the working time communicating, I‚Äôm ready to sacrifice any subsequent card. Very often people say that this is one and the same thing as the Team . In this case, they need to be helped to look at their work a little from the other side, not being attached to a specific team, but to talk about Communication in the company in general.
Moreover, by communication people understand different aspects of their activities. For some, this can be communication during breaks, although if the card is placed low, this does not affect this form of communication and does not exclude the possibility of talking with colleagues at a cooler or coffee machine.

For others, this is communication during work: holding meetings in which the team or the whole company participates. In addition, it may be less formal communication during working hours: lectures, trainings or master classes. Often such events are not included in working hours, but it is quite possible to touch upon their implementation when discussing this card.
You can also talk about events that a person lacks in the company and which may indirectly be related to communication: for example, gather with colleagues in a bar or play a game of desks in the office, or participate in an office competition (not necessarily on IT topics, these can be table football or billiards competitions).

The feedback card says that if I receive feedback about my work on a basis convenient for me, I‚Äôm ready to donate any subsequent card. The phrase ‚Äúconvenient form‚Äù is intentionally written on the map, as for one it can be a month, for another - three. In addition, a person may lack recognition of his merits and a feedback for him may be required even more often. A conversation on the topic of feedback and recognition is precisely what such a card should trigger.

In addition to the interval with which it is important for a person to receive a feedback, there are several more important points. The first of these is related to who to receive feedback from: from colleagues, from management, from a client. The player can be asked to rank the importance of receiving feedback from different sides.

The second aspect is related to the form of receiving feedback - it can be in the form of a personal conversation, in writing, anonymously.

According to the experience of the game, this card can be located both at the very top and at the very bottom. In addition, very often a feedback can be important for people who are just starting to take their first steps in a new role: a person has been promoted and without feedback it is difficult for him to understand how well he does his new job. Over time, he gains experience and confidence, and Feedback fades into the background.

In any case, to understand how people lack or lack feedback, recognition or praise is a very important thing.

If he wants to talk about this topic, then you can try to talk about the current tasks of a person and how, working on them, he brings something of his own or suggests what can be corrected, improved or changed. There are also cases when a person has ideas, but he is either embarrassed to voice them, or throws them aside, considering the ideas nonsense.
