About FARO


FARO company develops and sells coordinate measuring machines and devices for processing three-dimensional images, as well as software for them. FARO technology allows you to take three-dimensional measurements with the highest accuracy, visualize and compare parts and complex structures, which helps to improve the production process and ensure high quality. Devices are used to test components and assemblies, quickly prototype, 3D-document for large spaces and structures; in geodesy and construction, as well as for investigation and reconstruction at the scene of accidents or crimes.
