Image quality


On live test materials, the Dell U4919DW did not disappoint us. All the good words that were said about IPS matrices apply to it: for example, the monitor has really wide viewing angles. Distortion appears only at a large angle or when viewed diagonally, which is vital for such a large monitor. Resolution 5120 √ó 1440 pleases with clarity of image even with a diagonal of 49 inches.
