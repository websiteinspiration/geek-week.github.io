How to make a quote generator in an hour

The beauty of generators is that you can automate your work and save time. They are useful for applications, websites, blogs. The technique will itself remind your readers about your product without using the same annoying picture.

Creating a machine that works like InspiroBot.me is easy!

Techcrunch first wrote about InspiroBot AI in 2015, saying that such an instrument is a good substitute for human labor in creating philosophical posts for social networks.

InspiroBot announces itself: “I am an artificial intelligence designed to generate an unlimited number of unique inspirational quotes for the endless enrichment of meaningless human existence.” In other words, the author of the bot is Peder Jorgensen.(Peder Jørgensen), uploaded a dictionary and background pictures to generate an infinite number of quotes.

Here's how it works, according to a blog author in the Steemit.com tech forum :
Words are divided into semantic groups and types of phrases are written according to their meaning, for example:
1) <strive-to> <time / occasion> - <desire to> <result> < time / event>
2) <as-relationship> - <reason> <object1> <c> <verb> <object2> <as a relationship> <description> <object2> <as is> <object1>

There is a choice of such grammatical structures different tools, for example: Link Grammar Parser , where there are cyclic relationships between words - syntax rules.The punctuation corrector in Word Office works the same way.. In addition, there is a dictionary in English - Word Net, where parts of speech are grouped into sets of synonyms.
