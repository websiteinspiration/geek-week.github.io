Podcasts for Developers

It is interesting to watch the story. Netflix, YouTube, Twitch gradually squeezed TV out of our lives. Now a similar process is happening with podcasts and radio. Over the past 2 years, the number of podcasts in the world has increased from 525,000 to 900,000, and the number of episodes has increased from 18,000,000 to 30,000,000. In Russia, this wave began last year, but now it is becoming difficult to navigate them. We have made for you a selection of 57 techno-podcasts and so on.
