Briefly about great goals



Graduate recruitment - these are young people who are still recruiting students or graduating from universities. A man is more eager for an acquaintance. And he is afraid of the unknown. With the help of GP-programs, the company receives a unique tool. It is like an organism growing nodes for itself that perform certain tasks.

Create a healthy environment



Just as a scientist from a university in St. Petersburg can leave for Sweden, so other company recruiters, having the skills, are able to get to outside "owners". And it is beneficial to everyone. In the CIS countries, you can often notice the opposite effect when a successful person tries to deprive competitors of their knowledge. But this is already a thing of the past. Since the business understood - if he does not create a team and environment for himself, then competitors will do it. And here we come to the most interesting question - how to act?



How to arrange with students about Graduate Recruitment



In the US and Europe, GR programs are standard. The educational institutions have their own consultants who collaborate with employers on an ongoing and one-time basis. There are also recruiting agencies like the British GRB, which provides a full package of services for recruiting graduates and students. In the Russian Federation, technology is more likely to be applied in isolation, by each company separately. Although the intermediary market is developing. Large brands work in their own offices, training potential employees in them as well as in regular courses. The following recruitment methods can be distinguished directly from educational institutions (UZ).



Analysis of job fairs



KM inevitably carry them out. Sending a recruiting specialist to such events is one of the key methods. As you can already understand from the above, large companies open their academies. And they recruit people in them by any convenient means. The job fair is just an advertising platform where data is collected and offers are made. Information on such events is easy to find online. Usually, the conditions of participation are prescribed in advance by the university.



Direct and permanent work with universities on GR-programs



A more interesting method is direct recruitment. For these purposes, universities are selected with the necessary faculties. An employee of the company contacts the leadership of the educational institution, after which the possibility of allocating time for a recruiter is discussed. Usually this is a full lecture at which the presentation is held. Universities inevitably need money, and the provision of such services is the norm. These issues are easily discussed with the management of KM. A more difficult task is to create a circuit that looks as follows:



  • The employer on a contractual basis collaborates with KM, annually recruiting students;
  • The university openly uses the opportunity of employment in large companies as an advertising advantage (usually: ‚ÄúBring a young man - they‚Äôll immediately take him to Yandex, Mail.Ru, etc.);
  • The university transmits a database with student data so that the employer can recruit them using media technology. And here marketing comes into play.


This combination allows you to simultaneously advertise both brands and create a stable outflow of students directly to workplaces. This is similar to the scheme used in the USSR when a young man was assigned to a factory from a school, only with the possibility of choosing a place, etc. Since GP programs are underdeveloped, their large-scale creation takes a long time. No wonder at the beginning of the article, donations from businessmen were mentioned. Thanks to them, the latter have privileges in recruiting employees from the best teachers, as well as access to technologies and scientific works developed by the KM communities. And this is a little shady, but quite standard practice - big players set their own rules, and today everyone needs money.



Graduate Recruitment is not a new, but also underdeveloped area in hiring personnel. However, it is already widely used and the future lies with it.
