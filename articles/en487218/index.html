Fantastic and semi-fantastic designs, elements similar to Lego and AirPods, simple controls via a smartphone and a little AR. Pretty curious mix from the company we know. More details inside.

Botzees robot is positioned as an educational toy for children from 4 years. In its structure, it is a constructor that develops motor skills and mindfulness.
