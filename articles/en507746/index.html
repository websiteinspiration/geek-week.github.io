How does speed affect SEO?



Website loading speed is one of the ranking factors. Google reported this back in 2010. Well, by the 2020s. the significance of page speed has only grown. 



Performance directly affects SEO, and here's why:



  1. Search engines directly measure download speed, increasing the priority of fast sites.
  2. Search engines monitor people's behavior: if pages are less frequently visited and more often closed, the rating goes down.


And if direct measurement of speed does not play such an important role, then behavioral factors today are the basis for building search results. Of two equal sites, the faster will always be higher, and it will also bring more sales.



High download speed is a top priority. Pages should open as quickly as possible - almost instantly. For this, appropriate technical solutions are needed.



Which site is fast and which is not?



The speed of loading a site depends on a sum of factors, including the quality of the Internet connection on the user side, the type and settings of the browser, and the type of device. But if you take the most average indicators, speed should be 2-3 seconds.



A delay of 5-7 seconds is still permissible. But if the content is loaded for more than 10 seconds - you need to take action.



64% of users expect pages to load in no more than 4 seconds.



In general, a site should be considered fast that does not annoy the user. Resource pages should load quickly enough - at least not worse than that of the main competitors and on average on the Internet.

Why is the site slow?



Performance is determined by two main factors:



  1. The speed of the server where the site files are stored.
  2. The software part of the resource itself.


Pages can open slowly if the server has a problem or is located geographically far away.



But even if there are no complaints about the server, the site itself can be "heavy." Cluttered code, a large number of non-optimized content and the use of outdated solutions - all this slows down the resource.
