All Cups: The Story of a Great Ecosystem Design



Before using the product, we pay attention to its condition. Imagine that you come to a car dealership and choose a car. If the car looks bad, then we begin to experience negative emotions and feel the lack of attention from the creators of this product. Yes, and does not cause confidence. This is a waste of time. Of course, a person knows how to adapt to any changes, or simply can accept a fact, even if the product is functionally very good. But agree that convenience, color and overall style are important. Not everyone is ready to use what makes him put on his glasses once again, adjust the brightness on the screen or the light in the room. Everyone knows a great proverb that says that they are met by clothes - they are escorted by the mind. On the example of one of our directions in the company, we want to share with you what this means for us - not just in words,but in practice.

Since 2011, we regularly hold large online championships for industry experts. There is a separate area within the company that implements such initiatives. The results are not bad - 200 thousand unique users who spent an incredible amount of time solving sports programming tasks, artificial intelligence programming, developing highly loaded services and others. 



The picture shows our old logos. For each championship, different sites were created with their own language and style. The main audience of our projects is developers. You need to tell the designer about the design championship differently than programmers, agree?

Each design is a huge work. We always start with sketches and draw a chore.


Any competition is a real challenge, and we want to be remembered not only for tasks.

The sites for the championships are not one-page resources at all, but their own engines with various mechanics for holding competitions. Under the hood, a powerful engine with its own isolated code start / execute system and incredible infrastructure. We, as a technology company, are proud of this, since the created solution is unique. We will talk about this in another article.



The strategy for organizing the championships has changed a lot this year. A platform is already being created that will unite everyone under one brand and ideology - a single ecosystem.

First of all, we decide what we want from a new design that will satisfy the new strategy? We need to talk about our professional communities.


All championships, including future ones, get their own logo, which is designed to immediately immerse the user in context. Despite the uniform style of emblems, certain elements are unique:

  • the shape of the cup and the color of its field,
  • geometric pattern on blue fields,
  • as well as a symbol that is a metaphor for the IT industry that the championship is dedicated to.

Since the finals of many championships are held live, it is important that the design is equally well suited for digital and offline use: on handouts, souvenirs, advertising, etc. The staggered arrangement of the fields makes the new design very convenient for placement on physical media of any size: from pens to roadside billboards. There are more images on the All Cups page on Behance , and you can learn about design principles on the website of our Paradigm design system .
