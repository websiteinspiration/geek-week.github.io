Boston Dynamics: From US Army Orders to Spot Commercial Robot



For the past ten years, Boston Dynamics has been surprising us with a video of its robots. The company began with the creepy BigDog commissioned by the US Army, and ended with a bright yellow and friendly SpotMini for the industry. Their cars can scan rooms, carry loads, and also - balance on ice, dance and perform parkour - even more abruptly than people. It seems that you need to wait a little longer, and the robots will begin to walk freely through the streets. Now, when half the world sits home, it would look especially comical. The history of Boston Dynamics began almost 30 years ago, and maybe all 40 - when the founder of the company came up with a one-legged jumping robot.
