The basis of the concept is that for happiness people lack personal space and sockets. They understood this in a cafe a long time ago, but they just started to realize it on trains. The first important change - now you can fence off your shelf with a curtain and completely close yourself from the outside world.



But this could not be done while there was not enough space on the shelves. Therefore, at first I had to come up with something with the third shelf (it was removed to see what happens). And they tried to expand the upper shelves to the connection. It turned out very cool - the large beautiful floors of the capsule hotel.



Then, a wooden wall was placed between the upper shelves. It turned out a reserved seat with a lower floor and an upper floor.

Falling off the top shelf is no longer possible.

In this situation, the lower passengers would be very affected by the invasion of the upper by the table, so the tables were made on the shelves themselves. And they decided to take out one more from the rack on the side of the aisle.

Then the lower shelves were also enlarged and also divided by a partition. The result was a reserved seat, completely divided in half - 4 separate ‚Äúrooms‚Äù of the capsule hotel.

Compartments for compartments were made in the partition.

We watched the passengers sleep. And they found out that a person in a dream has far from the shape of a rectangular parallelepiped. But rather a truncated cone. Hollywood has long tried L-blankets, and now L-shelves. That is, at the top you have 20 centimeters more space. This is, in fact, a cabinet shelf from the previous version of the prototype, only now you can sleep with your head or hand in the cabinet. It sounds strange, but it looks very comfortable.



On the lower shelf, a table was made as on the side in old cars (only more convenient and smaller) so that you could sit together.





All this pulled a lot of problems in the engineer. Because you need to meet the mass of requirements. The basis of sanitary requirements is passage, light and air.
