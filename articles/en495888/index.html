Formats for participation at PyCon Russia 2020


The PyConRu program consists of keynotes, reports, workshops and Lightning Talks.


  • Keynote - 60-minute long report on the future and present of the language.
  • Report - a traditional report for 30-40 minutes.
  • Master class - 1.5-2 hours of work on a task or topic in order to learn something. The master class consists of theoretical and practical parts. This is the format with the most comprehensive coverage of the topic.
  • Lightning Talks - five minute reports from everyone.

This year we want to introduce another format - sprints. Sprint is a format for many hours of training and work on an open source project, together with the author of the project and other participants.
