Smart home in a smart city



On the May morning of 2022, before you woke up, there were a lot of events in the surrounding digital world. The sensor of sleep and awakening together with a smart bed fixed your mode of rest. The data was sent via the IoT gateway to the cloud service, which in turn sends reports to your mobile device.

A double-circuit boiler, a house watering system, security and video surveillance systems are served by another cloud service connected to the seventh generation distributed Wi-Fi network. You do not need a desktop computer as such, since the smartphone and VR / AR technologies provide a more convenient system to use. In the arsenal of your smart home is a gateway for foggy and boundary computing connected to a 5G Internet service provider and to the global web. A wired connection does not correspond to your lifestyle, you are mobile and independent of your place of stay. You can further develop this story and it does not matter if it comes true or not, but its proximity to modern reality is obvious.
