How I first streamed a university lecture

Coronavirus makes its own adjustments to student life, but while universities decide what to do and how to switch to distance learning, the progressive part of the teaching staff must take matters into their own hands. So I decided that I would not wait for my alma mater to figure out what to do, but decided to instantly transfer the lectures to remote mode.


Today was the first lesson and it was decent. Now I’ll tell you how I did it.


TLDR: A bunch of laptop + tablet with pen + Zoom is quite enough. But there are nuances.
