Where is the end of capitalism?

Capitalism will end when automation completely displaces people from material production. In addition to mass unemployment (with high social benefits), this also entails the collapse of a market economy. The main market regulator - the cost of goods - is determined by the cost of living labor (working time) for its production. This is the axis of the supply-demand swing, if the axis is pulled out, the swing will fall. How many zeros can be exchanged for one zero? Exchanges simply will not be able to work in conditions of price chaos. What is the way out of crises?

Marxism failed

Actually, no one disputed the economic part of Marxism. There is even a modern version, "21st Century Capital" by the French economist Tom. A completely different social picture is obtained: they don‚Äôt climb barricades just because someone is richer. How is information different from a classic product?

Information differs from the classic product on which market exchange is based. When transferred to another, it does not disappear at the source. The production of information requires labor, but copying is practically free. Therefore, artificial legal restrictions are created (rights, copyrights, etc.) in order to exchange it for traditional goods, but this will lose its meaning when the ‚Äútraditional‚Äù goods themselves are created by information technologies.
