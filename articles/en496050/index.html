Tigers and Lions get coronavirus in New York


Tigress Nadia

In the Bronx Zoo, the coronavirus was confirmed in the 4-year-old Malaysian tigress Nadi. Symptoms of the disease - dry cough, breathing and digestion problems - are also observed in her sister Azul, two Amur tigers and three African lions. The zoo has been closed to visitors since March 16, apparently, they were infected by one of the zoo employees who did not show symptoms.
