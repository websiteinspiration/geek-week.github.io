Welcome to Robot Operating System MeetUp

Sberbank Robotics Laboratory and the Russian ROS community with Intel support are inviting you to the next, third MeetUp on Robot Operating System , which will be held on Saturday, April 18 . And even if for all obvious reasons the event this time will be held in an online format, this will not spoil the mood. Moreover, the prizes for the best reports will be Intel products for robots.



Russian ROS Meetup 2020 is the assembly point of the Russian ROS community, an opportunity for ROS developers and robotics of all levels, from beginners to experts, to devote the weekend to practical training and communication with like-minded people. Engineers will share real experience in using ROS in research and commercial projects.
