Marina Alex, CEO of University of Business Agility: “Agile is out of IT. Agile - More Than IT ”

In 1951, clinical trials of isoniazid, an anti-TB drug, began in New York. As a result, it turned out that isoniazid is the first synthetic antidepressant. Coca-Cola was invented in Atlanta, Georgia, in 1886, as a remedy for indigestion, and along with the nervous system. Now it is one of the most popular carbonated drinks without any significant medical properties. In 1818, William Cabitt created a spinning wheel designed to punish prisoners - prisoners had to walk 3,352.8 meters daily on a moving staircase. As a result, it became a prototype of a treadmill in every sports center. Scientists from the British company Pfizer tried to create sildenafil citrate for the treatment of coronary heart diseases - well, what the whole world probably knows about “blue pills”.


Sometimes an invention takes on a new life. And the creators are surprised to see what their brainchild has turned into.


Why are we sure that DevOps or SRE can only live in the IT environment - perhaps this philosophy will save thousands of lives in disaster medicine, ambulance, aviation, space or the development of drugs and vaccines?


Marina Alex marina_alexAgile gave a second life - and in fact proved that Agile is applicable not only in IT, but also in sales, and in the transformation of large, hierarchical companies that do not keep pace with the market. Just need to be able to look at the idea a little from the outside - and see what was previously imperceptibly different.
