IP telephony. Types of VoIP devices, an overview of the pros and cons. What to choose?

According to research by DISCOVERY Research Group, the average annual growth rate of IP-telephony in Russia is 30%. Presumably, the IP telephony market in Russia will grow to 14.2 billion by 2021. As you can see, the growth is quite serious.


With the development of IP-telephony services, the VoIP equipment market is growing. And for each company it is important that the equipment is convenient to use and works stably.


The article talks about what types of final VoIP equipment exist, the pros and cons of each type, and which are more stable in operation. The information in this article will help companies, system administrators, managers, directors and other users who decide to connect IP-telephony to themselves, choose the right category of VoIP equipment.


And also, at the end of the article, a bonus : a brief recommendatory review of specific equipment models from our own experience.
