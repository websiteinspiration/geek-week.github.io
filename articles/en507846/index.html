Access is open



In October 2017, at the most famous Russian-language underground forum exploit [.] In, an announcement appeared on the sale of access to corporate networks of a number of companies - a rare underground service for that time. This lot, as reported in the media, was billed for $ 300,000. Fxmsp wrote that it was a targeted campaign. It took him a little over three years to become one of the main players in the Russian-speaking underground, with his pool of regular customers and even a sales manager, from an ordinary user of a hacker forum who did not know how to monetize his hacking skills.











Examining activity at hacker forums for more than 17 years, Group-IB Threat Intelligence experts began to record the growth of offers related to the sale of access to corporate networks, starting in 2017 - with the appearance of Fxmsp on the hacker scene. After that, the niche was empty for some time, and since the beginning of 2019, cybercriminals have followers who today are active in the underground, using Fxmsp equipment. According to a Group-IB study, since the beginning of 2020, more than 40 cybercriminals have been trapping Fxmsp in the underground forums. Market capture



The Group-IB report traces the activities of Fxmsp from the first registration on the underground forum recorded by the Group-IB Threat Intelligence system until it disappears from hacker sites. Fxmsp did not specialize in compromising specific companies. The top 3 victims are state organizations, IT service providers and retail. The data obtained during the study using the Group-IB Threat Intelligence system made it possible to identify the tools that Fxmsp used to compromise companies, to determine, with a high degree of accuracy, the number of its victims, and to establish the alleged identity of the cybercriminal. The Group-IB report gradually reveals how, according to the most conservative estimates, the Russian-speaking hacker Fxmsp earned about $ 1.5 million from an ordinary darknet user who started cryptocurrency mining in less than 3 years - and this does not include sales in private , lots without indicating the price, as well as repeated sales of accesses in the network of victim companies.



Together with their accomplice under the nickname Lampeduza, who took over the advertising and support of all transactions, from October 2017 to September 2019, they put up for sale accesses to 135 companies from 44 countries, including the USA, Russia, England, France, Italy, the Netherlands , Singapore, Japan, Australia and many others. Despite the unwritten law in the underground environment not to work "according to RU", Fxmsp sold two lots for Russian victims, for which it was "banned" by the forum moderators, but this did not stop the criminal.
