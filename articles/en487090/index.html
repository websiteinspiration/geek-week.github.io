A new level of Windows optimization

About two years ago, our team told Habr about his project to optimize Windows using neural network technology.

Over the past two years, much has changed, but the most important thing has remained unchanged. And in this article we want to share those discoveries and those conclusions that have been made over these two years.



If you are interested in the situation with independent production of PC software in 2020, welcome to cat.

But before moving on to the main narrative, it is necessary to clarify that everything said below is described from the perspective of an independent company, which in the process of development moves forward only at the expense of internal resources.

What has been done in these two years


UWP

Publication in the MS Store has become one of the most difficult, but also one of the most important achievements when working on a project. It was this step that allowed us to go beyond the CIS market and develop the global market for Windows software.

New functionality.

The next step, after it became possible to manage CPU priorities and CPU cores to optimize Windows, it was necessary to teach the application to manage all other key components of a PC in order to use neural network algorithms with maximum efficiency.
And if everything was relatively simple with managing the cores and priorities of the CPU, then to manage the priorities of RAM and the priorities of I / O, I had to dive into Windows architecture so much that it was time to write dissertations. For example, from all the software we know, only WPS and Process Lasso can set ‚Äúhigh‚Äù I / O priority.

Well, as soon as the full control of the CPU, RAM and ROM was mastered, the matter remained with the peripheral devices. Therefore, the next development step was power management, where WPS was the only application we know that manages power dynamically, not statically. This means that for maximum performance, you do not need to constantly keep the CPU frequency at 100% and do not allow the cores to park, which leads to constant operation at maximum loads and increased power consumption. Now, due to the analysis of applications by the neural network, it became possible to get the same maximum performance, but just when the user is working with heavy software or playing demanding games so that the rest of the time the computer does not translate electricity into heat without any benefit.

And in addition to optimizing the key elements of the PC, data transfer optimization was added (through traffic compression and blocking ads on the VPN), as well as automatic cleaning of junk files.

Stability

Over the past time, we have repeatedly seen the correct choice of application architecture. Due to the fact that all operations with the system are performed through WinAPI, the issue of stability (system and software) when making changes to the operating parameters turned out to be solved almost perfectly. For more than two years of the application and more than a million devices on which it was installed, we have not registered a single case of problems with the stability of the system or incompatibility with third-party applications.

UI / UX

One of the key areas of work over the past time has been the improvement of UI / UX, which allowed us to lower the threshold for new users to master key application functions. Rating of hardware performance

There was an opportunity not only to say how many conditional parrots a particular PC gives out, but to evaluate the actual conformity of the performance of an individual computer relative to all the actual PCs on which the application is installed. Such a function solves three problems at once. The first is the question of which component of the computer is the most outdated and needs to be replaced. The second - how much this computer is faster / slower than all others in general. Third, embedded algorithms can use various approaches to increase the performance of powerful and weak PCs to achieve the best results.
