CC Search





ccsearch.creativecommons.org



CC Search is tailored to search for copyright free content. So if you are a content maker, especially a beginner, and there is no money for paid subscriptions yet, but you still want to be valid, then this search engine is what you need.



If you need music for video, an image for a blog post, or something else, you can safely take any materials from the issue, without worrying about who will come for you with a subpoena for copyright infringement.



CC Search works quite straightforward: it retrieves results from platforms such as Soundcloud, Wikimedia, and Flickr and displays results tagged as Creative Commons.



In the left panel, you can choose the type of license by which the content will be filtered, well, other standard filters - file type, size, you can also filter by production sources.
