For a digital initiative to grow and prosper, the chief executive has to support it. Less than unflagging support probably means that the effort will fail. Blending Clicks and Bricks

Target marketing and mass customization mean that customers can be segmented profitably in many ways. But abolish online and offline customer categories. Your business can‚Äôt afford an "us vs. them" attitude when it comes to making e-business work. Marginal Middlemen

The benefits of digital technology have nothing to do with the size of your business. When digital services are deployed wisely, they magnify and confer advantages to businesses of all sizes, whether they are small, medium or large. Be First

Seize the advantage of being first over the advantage of being right. Caution and over analysis can kill a company in the world of Internet time.   • Traditional companies that have corporate cultures that already engender risk-taking and rely heavily on individual initiatives are well prepared for the digital challenge.

New Economy Opportunities

The Internet makes it much easier to convey product differentiation. For example:

  • Companies shouldn‚Äôt leap into new technologies just because they‚Äôre new, according to the leaders at Rosenbluth. First, you should decide how this technology fits your company‚Äôs unique culture and comparative advantages.
