Stocks Can Make You Rich

If you want to think about a scary risk, think of the risk of running out of money before you die. That is a real problem. Avoid Taxes Legally

Don‚Äôt pay more taxes than you must. Research suggests that, on average, 2% of investors‚Äô returns go to taxes. You can reduce your U.S. income taxes very legally by taking some precautions.
